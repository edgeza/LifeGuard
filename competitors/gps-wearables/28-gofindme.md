## Differentiation

GoFindMe is a local off-grid coordination device, not a satellite rescue service. It works only if another equipped group member is within the LoRa/mesh network, but avoids both cellular dead zones and monthly fees.

## Strengths

- ✅ No cellular network or subscription required.
- ✅ Physical SOS, off-grid peer location and messaging.
- ✅ IP67 and 72-hour historical battery suit outdoor use.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ⚠️ Current hardware availability/support is not verified despite live app listing.
- ❌ SOS reaches only the equipped group, not emergency services or a response centre.
- ❌ Range is local and terrain-dependent; not equivalent to Garmin inReach.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Clearly distinguish peer mesh, cellular and satellite emergency coverage.
- Explore local caregiver mesh as a backup, never the sole emergency path.
- Use no-subscription group communication for events/campuses with an operator gateway.
