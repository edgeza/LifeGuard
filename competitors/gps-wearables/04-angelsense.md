## Differentiation

AngelSense is purpose-built for elopement and wandering rather than generic family location. Continuous updates, caregiver-controlled voice and school-aware controls create a high-touch special-needs proposition.

## Strengths

- ✅ Standalone multi-network 4G connectivity in three countries.
- ✅ Unlimited guardians, geofences, detailed daily timeline and live transit tools.
- ✅ Auto-answer voice lets a caregiver hear or speak when the wearer cannot operate a phone.
- ✅ Support is explicitly staffed by special-needs parents.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Short 16–24 hour battery creates daily charging risk.
- ❌ No fall detection or professional emergency triage.
- ⚠️ Current pricing page exposes plan structure but not stable dollar amounts in extracted public content.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Serve autism/dementia use cases with longer battery and sensory-friendly mounts.
- Add optional professional escalation without removing caregiver control.
- Publish a simpler total first-year price and funding/reimbursement guidance.
