## Differentiation

GeoZilla uses aggressive app-membership bundling to reduce apparent hardware cost. Its SOS is a family notification layered onto a general location platform, not dispatch.

## Strengths

- ✅ Low advertised entry price and combined phone/device family map.
- ✅ Physical SOS and real-time GPS.
- ✅ Multi-year service option lowers effective cost.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ⚠️ App membership and tracker cellular plan are easy to confuse; quoted prices differ by funnel.
- ❌ No voice, fall detection or professional monitoring.
- ⚠️ Battery/water specifications are not prominent.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Present one transparent total-cost table that separates app, device and cellular fees.
- Add two-way voice and unanswered-SOS escalation.
- Use a shared family map without upselling basic safety visibility.
