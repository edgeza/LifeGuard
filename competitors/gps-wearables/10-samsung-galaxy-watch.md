## Differentiation

Samsung is the principal Android-side equivalent to Apple Watch, with fall detection, SOS, emergency-contact location and an audible Ultra siren. It remains an emergency-call feature set, not monitored PERS.

## Strengths

- ✅ Broad Android/Wear OS reach.
- ✅ Configurable fall detection and emergency-call/contact workflow.
- ✅ Ultra siren provides an immediate local deterrence/attention feature.
- ✅ LTE variants reduce phone dependency.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Fall detection is off by default and cannot detect every fall.
- ❌ No trained operator or caregiver care-plan workflow.
- ❌ Bluetooth models still depend on phone pairing/network.
