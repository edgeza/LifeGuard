## Differentiation

SecuLife combines a standalone cellular wristband, GPS, fall detection and family calling at a mid-market price. It is closer to MyLifeLine's likely wearable stack than phone-tethered jewellery.

## Strengths

- ✅ Standalone cellular, physical SOS, GPS and automatic fall detection in one wrist device.
- ✅ Multiple plan commitments and clearly posted pricing.
- ✅ Lifetime device warranty while subscription remains active.
- ✅ Broad adjacent SKU range for kids, seniors and assets.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No clearly described independent 24/7 monitoring centre in standard offer.
- ⚠️ Month-to-month price is materially higher and adds an activation fee.
- ⚠️ Battery and water rating are not prominent in current public product extraction.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Lead with certified professional response and disclose its location/standards.
- Make battery endurance and shower safety first-class proof points.
- Offer one all-inclusive plan below SecuLife's no-contract US$34 tier.
