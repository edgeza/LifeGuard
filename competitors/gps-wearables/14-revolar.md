## Differentiation

Revolar's one/two/three-click language is a useful interaction pattern: it communicates safe, uneasy and emergency states without opening the phone. It lacks current-market certainty and direct dispatch.

## Strengths

- ✅ Nuanced alert states reduce the all-or-nothing panic-button problem.
- ✅ No mandatory fee and replaceable long-life battery.
- ✅ Live location and customizable messages to ordinary contacts.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ⚠️ Product and app appear stale; current support/inventory cannot be confidently verified.
- ❌ Phone-tethered with no fall detection, voice or monitoring centre.
- ❌ Historical corporate distress creates continuity concerns.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Adopt graded check-in/uneasy/emergency interactions in MyLifeLine.
- Guarantee long-term app support and publish service status.
