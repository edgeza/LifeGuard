## Differentiation

LandAirSea is included as a category boundary: it is a robust, inexpensive asset tracker, not a wearable emergency device. A shared location link should not be mistaken for SOS response.

## Strengths

- ✅ IP67 enclosure, integrated magnet and long low-power battery.
- ✅ Very fast optional update rates and one-year playback.
- ✅ Low hardware price and mature app/web tracking.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No SOS button, voice, fall detection or monitoring centre.
- ❌ Form factor is unsuitable for normal wrist/jewellery wear.
- ❌ Fast updates can cost US$49.95/month and reduce battery.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Do not benchmark asset trackers as full safety substitutes.
- Borrow low-power motion wake and rugged enclosure design.
- Offer shareable live incident links to responders alongside actual monitored SOS.
