## Differentiation

Kanega is the strongest purpose-built watch benchmark: standalone connectivity, voice, included fall detection and a battery system that can be swapped without removing the watch. It sacrifices caregiver software and price competitiveness.

## Strengths

- ✅ Three activation paths: fall, voice and button.
- ✅ Swappable batteries address the charging-gap problem better than consumer watches.
- ✅ Standalone Verizon plus Wi-Fi and US monitoring operators.
- ✅ Fall detection, medication reminders and cellular service included.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No caregiver app or location portal.
- ❌ High first-year and monthly cost.
- ⚠️ Current official monthly rate (US$84.95) conflicts with older product-page/search prices; quote the FAQ date.
- ⚠️ A 2026 third-party review warns of financial/availability uncertainty; confirm before purchase.
