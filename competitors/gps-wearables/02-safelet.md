## Differentiation

Safelet is a low-cost, no-subscription guardian-network product. It prioritizes coordination among trusted contacts over professional dispatch, making it closer to a wearable family alert than a monitored PERS service.

## Strengths

- ✅ No recurring fee.
- ✅ Guardian map shows responders to one another and supports coordination.
- ✅ Live iOS and Android app updates in 2025 indicate current software support.
- ✅ Simple one-click wrist form factor.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Phone-tethered and dependent on mobile data.
- ❌ No automatic fall detection or embedded professional monitoring.
- ❌ Limited current public technical detail on battery and water resistance.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Offer optional professional escalation while retaining a free family-response tier.
- Add standalone connectivity and explicit device health/battery telemetry.
- Use multi-responder coordination as a baseline caregiver-app feature.
