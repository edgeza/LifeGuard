## Differentiation

Tracki competes on low hardware price, global SIM and configurable update speed. The SOS button is useful, but the product remains a tracker-to-owner alert rather than monitored personal safety.

## Strengths

- ✅ Very low hardware entry and broad international coverage.
- ✅ Physical SOS plus multiple custom alerts.
- ✅ Configurable update rates, geofences, history and extended batteries.
- ✅ No activation/cancellation fee.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No two-way voice, fall detection or professional triage.
- ❌ Battery collapses to a few days at high-frequency tracking.
- ❌ Water protection requires an accessory in many use cases.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Offer a clear battery-versus-update-rate slider in caregiver UX.
- Add monitored SOS and voice while retaining global roaming.
- Avoid teaser hardware pricing by publishing first-year total cost.
