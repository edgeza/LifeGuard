## Differentiation

Apple puts reliable fall/SOS primitives in a mass-market device and can route some emergencies by satellite, but it skips the trained human triage layer central to PERS.

## Strengths

- ✅ Large installed base and trusted consumer brand.
- ✅ Automatic fall detection, direct two-way emergency call and contact notification.
- ✅ Medical ID and ecosystem integration.
- ✅ Satellite extension on supported hardware.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No 24/7 PERS operator to verify, triage or follow a care plan.
- ❌ Daily charging and wrist-detection requirements create coverage gaps.
- ❌ Full experience is iPhone-centric; cellular model/service increases cost.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Offer a MyLifeLine watch app or event-integration layer that adds human monitoring.
- Market cross-platform caregiver coordination rather than trying to replace Apple hardware.
- Differentiate on multi-day battery and purpose-built senior UX.
