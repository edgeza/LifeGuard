## Differentiation

Spytec is software/fleet-first: hardware is subsidized by recurring service and the same Hapn platform scales from one tracker to many. SOS is secondary, and professional response is absent.

## Strengths

- ✅ Mature multi-device web/app platform and flexible hardware.
- ✅ Free-hardware subscription framing lowers procurement friction.
- ✅ Long-battery configurations and geofence/text/email alerts.
- ✅ No contract or activation fee.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No voice, fall detection or emergency monitoring.
- ❌ Consumer personal-safety ergonomics are weaker than wrist/button devices.
- ⚠️ Battery claims vary enormously with update rate and enclosure.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Use B2B fleet-style administration for organisations managing many lone workers.
- Separate emergency response SLA from technical support.
- Provide wearable mounts and a dedicated, protected SOS control.
