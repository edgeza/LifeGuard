## Differentiation

Nimb is the purest panic-jewellery concept in the set: a concealed ring button that remains accessible when a pendant or phone may not be. Commercial execution and current support are the central concerns.

## Strengths

- ✅ Ring ergonomics make the trigger highly discreet and always at hand.
- ✅ Vibration confirms transmission without alerting an aggressor.
- ✅ Explored both phone-tethered and autonomous cellular architectures.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ⚠️ No current official retail/service proof; do not represent as an active alternative.
- ❌ Short battery versus non-rechargeable charms and no fall detection.
- ❌ Crowdfunding/app complaints indicate fulfilment and software risk.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Prototype a ring/button form only after validating battery, RF and false-alarm ergonomics.
- Use silent haptic acknowledgement on every discreet MyLifeLine trigger.
- Avoid launch until production, monitoring and app support are operationally proven.
