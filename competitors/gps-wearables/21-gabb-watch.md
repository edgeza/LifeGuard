## Differentiation

Gabb deliberately limits the SOS destination to a parent/guardian and strips social media/browser access. It is a controlled first phone, not an emergency-response device.

## Strengths

- ✅ Simple, school-friendly device with no browser/social media.
- ✅ GPS, safe zones and approved contacts.
- ✅ IP68 rating and clear contract choices.
- ✅ Parent/guardian SOS remains available in Focus Mode.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ SOS explicitly does not call 911 or professional monitoring.
- ❌ Very short 14–18 hour normal-use battery.
- ❌ Lowest plan requires a two-year commitment.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Differentiate with emergency escalation after a guardian misses the call.
- Make multi-day battery a headline advantage.
- Retain distraction-free design for children and cognitive-care users.
