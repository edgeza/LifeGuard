## Differentiation

Optimus is a straightforward caregiver tracker with a usable SOS button and month-class battery. It undercuts many wearables but lacks voice, fall sensing and response operations.

## Strengths

- ✅ Central tactile SOS and long claimed battery.
- ✅ One simple US$19.95 monthly service with prepaid discounts.
- ✅ SMS, email, push, geofence and low-battery alerts.
- ✅ No contract.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No two-way voice, fall detection or professional response.
- ❌ Not inherently waterproof without accessory.
- ❌ US-centric direct coverage and non-wearable form.
