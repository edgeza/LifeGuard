## Differentiation

Garmin is the remote/off-grid safety benchmark. Garmin Response and Iridium provide global interactive SOS where cellular PERS cannot operate, but its ergonomics and pricing target outdoor users rather than passive senior care.

## Strengths

- ✅ True off-grid, global two-way SOS coordination.
- ✅ Long battery endurance and rugged hardware.
- ✅ Mature response centre and rich tracking/messaging ecosystem.
- ✅ Consumer and professional service plans.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No general automatic fall detection or voice call on inReach.
- ❌ Mainstream watch Assistance is phone-dependent.
- ❌ Hardware and satellite plan cost exceed basic cellular buttons.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Offer satellite escalation as a premium rural/remote MyLifeLine tier.
- Translate Garmin's suspend/seasonal plan flexibility to travel users.
- Add senior-focused automatic fall and simpler one-step operator voice.
