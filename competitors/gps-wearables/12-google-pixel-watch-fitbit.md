## Differentiation

Google combines Fitbit health sensing with one of the broadest consumer safety stacks: fall, crash, safety timer, loss of pulse and satellite SOS. The safety functions belong to Pixel Watch—not ordinary Fitbit trackers—an important category correction.

## Strengths

- ✅ Automatic fall and loss-of-pulse emergency workflows.
- ✅ LTE independence and satellite SOS on current flagship hardware.
- ✅ Strong Android health/location integration.
- ✅ Medical information and emergency sharing are native.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No trained PERS monitoring/triage centre.
- ❌ Daily charging remains a material coverage gap.
- ❌ Region, model and LTE/Safety Signal dependencies complicate eligibility.
- ❌ Fitbit-branded bands themselves do not supply the same SOS feature set.
