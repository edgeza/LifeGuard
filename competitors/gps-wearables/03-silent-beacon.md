## Differentiation

Unlike minimalist BLE buttons, Silent Beacon puts a microphone and speaker in the remote so the user can talk without reaching the phone. The company now emphasizes workplace deployments while keeping consumer hardware available.

## Strengths

- ✅ Two-way calling from the button is unusual in the phone-tethered category.
- ✅ 42-day standby is materially longer than smartwatch batteries.
- ✅ Consumer and enterprise tiers support multiple go-to-market channels.
- ✅ Physical device, current app and official manuals remain available.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Still fails without the nearby paired smartphone and its data/cellular service.
- ❌ Not waterproof and no fall detection.
- ⚠️ Multiple app/plan prices make the consumer proposition less simple than no-fee peers.
