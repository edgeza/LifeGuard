## Differentiation

Yepzon's mode-based architecture let the owner trade tracking frequency for battery and even run SOS-only mode. It is useful historical product design, not a procurement-ready current competitor.

## Strengths

- ✅ Multi-technology positioning including early Wi-Fi indoor location.
- ✅ SOS-only and interval controls can extend battery.
- ✅ Shared caregiver access and embedded SIM.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ⚠️ Current sales, app/backend and subscription continuity are not verified.
- ❌ No voice, fall detection or monitoring centre.
- ❌ Residual stock pricing does not establish a supported product.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- Expose explicit normal, battery-saver and SOS-only modes.
- Guarantee backend continuity and communicate end-of-life support.
- Add voice/fall without sacrificing configurable tracking intervals.
