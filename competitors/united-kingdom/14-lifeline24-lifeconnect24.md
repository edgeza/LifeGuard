# Lifeline24 / LifeConnect24

- **Website**: https://lifeline24.co.uk (Norwich-based brand); https://www.lifeconnect24.co.uk (sister)
- **Headquarters**: Norwich, Norfolk, UK
- **Founded**: Lifeline24 established ~2010s

## Business Model
**B2C primary** — direct-to-consumer personal alarm service; Lifeline24 offers **TSA-accredited 24/7 Response Team**. Also operates in Republic of Ireland (LifeConnect24 IE).

## Core Products
- **Lifeline Personal Alarm** — Tunstall Lifeline VI based, pendant + base unit
- **MyAmie pendant** (Tunstall)
- Optional fall detector, smoke alarm, bogus caller button, GPS pendant add-ons
- Next-day free delivery

## Pricing
- **£128/year** (cited in MSE forum — basic annual plan)
- **£35 set-up** (one-off)
- Plans from ~£18.99/month

## Reach
Norfolk-based; serves customers across UK + Ireland; Lifeline VI hardware; positioned as a value alternative to Careline365/Taking Care.

## TSA Status
**TSA-accredited** 24/7 Response Team (explicit on homepage).

## Tech Stack
**Tunstall Lifeline VI** hardware — the de facto UK industry standard.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **TSA-accredited response team** (smaller providers often aren't)
- UK + Ireland dual market coverage
- Lower price point than Taking Care/Careline365

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- Smaller brand than Careline365 / Age UK / Taking Care
- Tunstall hardware dependent (no proprietary platform)
- No mobile GPS pendant as a hero SKU
- No B2B SaaS / reseller programme
- No mobile app with family tracking (where MyLifeLine excels)
