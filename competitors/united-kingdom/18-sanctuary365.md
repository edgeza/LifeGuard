# Sanctuary365 (Sanctuary Housing Group)

- **Website**: https://www.sanctuary-housing.co.uk (parent) — Sanctuary365 is the housing association's telecare brand
- **Headquarters**: Worcester, UK (Sanctuary Housing Group HQ)
- **Parent**: **Sanctuary Housing Association** — one of UK's largest housing associations (~80,000+ properties)
- **TSA Status**: Listed service provider

## Business Model
**B2G/B2B primary** — Sanctuary Housing tenants as primary customers; also available to private customers and other LAs.

## Core Products
- **Personal alarm** — pendant + base unit
- **Dispersed alarms**
- **Warden-call systems** for Sanctuary sheltered housing schemes
- **Telecare sensors** (smoke, CO, falls, flood)
- 24/7 monitoring

## Reach
- Tied to Sanctuary Housing's 80,000+ property portfolio
- Operates across multiple UK regions where Sanctuary has stock

## Pricing
Not publicly published — included within Sanctuary tenancy for residents; private pay pricing on enquiry.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Built-in customer base via Sanctuary Housing's 80k+ properties
- LA/housing-association procurement expertise

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Not a standalone consumer brand** — niche captive audience
- No mobile GPS pendant hero product
- No white-label SaaS for resellers
- No international footprint
