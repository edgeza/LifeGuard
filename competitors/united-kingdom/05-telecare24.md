# Telecare24 (Telecare24 Ltd)

- **Website**: https://www.telecare24.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: Cardiff, Wales, UK
- **Founded**: 2008
- **Parent**: **Independent** (private; sister brands include **LifeConnect24** and **Telecare Choice** — same ownership group; Cardiff-based)
- **Company No.**: 06600164

## Business Model
**B2C primary** (D2C subscription personal alarms) + **B2B Telecare24 Commercial** — sells digital telecare solutions to retirement properties, RTM schemes, resident associations, charities, social-housing organisations, with portfolio management services.

## Core Products
- **Home personal alarm** (digital, landline or no-landline options)
- **GO GPS / GO Active!** — mobile GPS pendant with fall detection
- **Fall alarm**
- **Key safe**
- **Smartwatch option**

## Pricing
- Monthly plans ~£19.99/mo (after discounts), cheaper than Taking Care
- Annual plans save more; **£15 loyalty discount** for returning customers on annual plans
- Free info pack, free next-day delivery

## Reach
Sister brand to LifeConnect24 (UK & Ireland: lifeconnect24.ie); the group is one of the largest direct-to-consumer personal-alarm groups in UK by customer count. 0800 180 85 40.

## TSA Status
TSA-listed partner; group participates in TSA accreditation.

## Tech Stack
WooCommerce + Stripe ecommerce; cellular pendants; mobile-app for family monitoring.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Three-brand portfolio (Telecare24, LifeConnect24, Telecare Choice) gives cross-sell reach
- B2B Commercial division for retirement/housing portfolios — institutional revenue line MyLifeLine lacks
- UK VAT-exempt pricing model optimised for disabled/elderly buyers
- Active digital switchover migration program (a key UK competitive differentiator 2025-2026)

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- Pure UK consumer play — no international / 7-country reseller footprint MyLifeLine has
- No white-label SaaS for security company resellers
- Falls behind Careline365 on Trustpilot review volume & behind Appello on digital infrastructure
- Hardware subscription model only — no family-monitoring or self-managed SKU
