# Other Notable UK Competitors (TSA-listed)

Compiled from the TSA Service Provider Directory and Member Directory — these providers round out the UK competitive landscape MyLifeLine would face. Each is a smaller regional/municipal or specialist provider that doesn't warrant a standalone deep-dive but is part of the fragmented UK market.

## 19. Forestcare (Bracknell Forest Council)
- **Website**: https://www.bracknell-forest.gov.uk/health-and-social-care/adult-social-care/forestcare
- **Model**: Local-authority in-house TEC service (Bracknell, Berkshire)
- **Customers**: Bracknell Forest residents + private; emergency response team
- **Why it matters**: LA-funded subsidised model; competes on price for residents

## 20. Medvivo Careline (Medvivo Group)
- **Website**: https://www.medvivo.com
- **Model**: B2G/B2B — Wiltshire-based, NHS 111 + TEC combined; one of the bigger LA outsource partners
- **Customers**: Wiltshire CC, BaNES, Swindon CC and others
- **Why it matters**: NHS-integration differentiation

## 21. Calico Homes Lifeline
- **Model**: Housing-association subsidiary (Burnley, Lancashire); part of Calico Group
- **Model**: Captive customer base of Calico Homes tenants
- **Why it matters**: Vertical integration with housing provider

## 22. Bournemouth Careline
- **Model**: BCP Council in-house telecare service
- **Why it matters**: Regional LA competitor

## 23. Bolton at Home
- **Model**: Housing association (Bolton); in-house TEC
- **Why it matters**: Captive customer base

## 24. Eden Community Alarms
- **Model**: Eden District Council (now part of Westmorland & Furness Council) in-house
- **Why it matters**: Small rural LA service

## 25. Milton Keynes Community Alarm Service
- **Website**: https://www.milton-keynes.gov.uk/community-alarm-service
- **Model**: MK Council in-house; £4.40/week Level 1 equivalent
- **Reach**: MK residents; includes welfare calls, falls lifting
- **Why it matters**: LA in-house competitor — same subsidised pricing as Welbeing/Progress

## 26. NHS Lothian / East Ayrshire / Highland / North Lanarkshire CCs (and ~100+ other UK councils)
- **Model**: In-house LA telecare services across the UK (TSA Service Provider Directory lists ~140+ accredited UK LA providers)
- **Why it matters**: Collectively this is the largest segment of the UK market by subscriber count (most UK TEC end-users get service via their local authority, not a private provider)

## 27. Tunstall Response
- **Model**: The **consumer/B2C ARC service** arm of Tunstall — runs monitoring for Lifeline Digital home units sold to consumers
- **Why it matters**: Sibling to Tunstall Healthcare (UK) Ltd — direct to consumer

## 28. Alcuris
- **Website**: https://www.alcuris.co.uk
- **Model**: B2B SaaS — proactive telecare using "Akuura" platform; activity-of-daily-living monitoring
- **Why it matters**: Modern preventative/proactive TEC platform — direct software competitor to MyLifeLine platform
- **TSA Status**: TSA member

## 29. Alertacall
- **Website**: https://alertacall.com
- **Model**: B2B/B2G — proactive daily-check telephone calls to vulnerable residents; combines hardware + proactive wellbeing calls
- **Why it matters**: UK pioneer of "proactive check-in" model; counter-position to panic-button-only MyLifeLine

## 30. Chiptech / TeleAlarm / Essence / Tynetec / Doro (Hardware OEMs)
- **Model**: B2B hardware-only OEMs that supply the platforms above; some have direct ARC partnerships
- **Why it matters**: Component layer of the UK TEC stack — not direct MyLifeLine competitors but constrain/disable any UK entry that needs compatible hardware

---
*Source: TSA member directory (telecare.org.uk/member-directory), TSA service-provider directory (telecare.org.uk/service-provider-directory). Researched Jul 2026.*
