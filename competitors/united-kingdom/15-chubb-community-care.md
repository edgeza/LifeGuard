# Chubb Community Care (Chubb Fire & Security)

- **Website**: https://chubbfs.com/uk-en/chubb-personal-alarm/ and https://chubbcommunitycare.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: Manchester / UK-wide operations
- **Parent**: **Chubb** (a brand of **Carrier Global Corp**, NYSE-listed). Chubb Fire & Security UK division. Chubb Community Care was part of UTC Climate, Controls & Security until Carrier/UTC merger.

## Business Model
**B2G/B2B primary + B2C** — supplies telecare to local councils, housing associations, NHS trusts (e.g. Dumfries & Galloway Housing Partnership, South Ayrshire HSCP); also sells personal alarms direct to consumers.

## Core Products
- **Chubb Personal Alarm** — 4G digital, IP connectivity, no landline required
- **Fall Detector pendant** with "Intelligent Sensing"
- **Dispersed alarms, integrated telecare & telehealth systems** for LAs/housing orgs
- **Cloud Care Control** — cloud-based ARC monitoring platform (powered by Skyresponse, Swedish SaaS)
- Warden-call systems for sheltered housing

## Pricing
Bespoke contract pricing for B2G; consumer pricing not published on main landing page (sales-led).

## Reach
- Multiple LA contracts across UK (South Ayrshire, Dumfries & Galloway, dozens more)
- **Own ARC** for 24/7/365 monitoring (TSA standards)
- Heritage: "over 200 years" of Chubb fire/security expertise

## TSA Status
TSA member; 24/7/365 ARC operates to TSA standards.

## Tech Stack
**Cloud Care Control** (cloud-native, IoT-based, supports digital + analogue, SaaS via Skyresponse partnership — Skyresponse is the fastest-growing alarm platform in Nordics elderly-care market).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Carrier / Chubb brand equity** — globally recognised life-safety brand
- Cloud-native ARC platform (Cloud Care Control) vs many UK competitors on legacy on-prem systems
- B2G scale with LAs and housing associations
- Strong fire-security cross-sell

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **No direct consumer GPS mobile pendant hero product**
- No white-label SaaS for security-company resellers
- Smaller B2C direct funnel than Careline365
- Corporate / industrial heritage less suited to "family trust" MyLifeLine positioning
- Hardware partner dependency (Skyresponse platform)
