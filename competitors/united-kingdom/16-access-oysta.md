# Access Oysta Technology (formerly Oysta Technology)

- **Website**: https://www.theaccessgroup.com/en-ie/health-social-care/access-technology-enabled-care/personal-alarms/
- **Headquarters**: UK (HQ of Access Group is Birmingham)
- **Founded**: Oysta founded 2010s; acquired by **Access Group** (UK software conglomerate, £1bn+ revenue) and now branded **Access Oysta** within Access Technology Enabled Care

## Business Model
**B2B/B2G primary** — sells telecare/lone-worker solutions to social-care teams, local authorities, NHS; supports both self-monitoring and ARC monitoring.

## Core Products
- **Access TEC Pendant** — discreet safety net, links to Access Device Management Platform (DMP)
- **Access TEC App** — turns an existing smartphone into a telecare device (cost-effective)
- **Access TEC Watch** — discreet wearable with SOS button, makes/receives calls
- **Oysta Pearl+** — flagship mobile telecare device (lone-worker / VIP)
- **Oysta Pearl II** — fall detection
- **Oysta Rio** — fall detection variant
- **Oysta Watch** — basic watch
- **IntelliCare™** — care platform

## Reach
- 10+ years in mobile telecare
- Lone-worker + VIP (Vulnerable Independent People) market leader
- Used by social-care teams across UK; Scottish digital telecare security-assessed supplier scheme certified

## Pricing
Bespoke — typically hardware + subscription to ARC or self-monitoring (IntelliCare platform).

## TSA Status
TSA-aligned; Scottish digital telecare security-assessed scheme certified (high-bar government assurance).

## Tech Stack
**Access Device Management Platform** (DMP) — cloud-based platform for care-circle management, geo-fencing, welfare checks, reminders; GSM mobile + GPS satellite; integrates with IntelliCare.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Mobile telecare / lone-worker expertise** is a true specialist niche
- **Software-platform-led** — IntelliCare is a real SaaS for care teams, not just hardware
- Owned by Access Group (£1bn+ revenue software firm) — financial stability
- Cost-effective App SKU leverages existing smartphones (no hardware required)
- Scotland's certified supplier scheme = government-grade assurance

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **No consumer panic-button direct sales** — purely B2B
- No SMS-to-5-emergency-contacts model (MyLifeLine's hero feature)
- No white-label reseller model for security companies
- Less recognised by end consumers (who know Careline365/Age UK brands)
