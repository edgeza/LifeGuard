# SureSafe (personalalarms.org)

- **Website**: https://personalalarms.org (NOT suresafe.co.uk — that domain is a PPE/workwear retailer; the personal-alarm company operates personalalarms.org)
- **Headquarters**: UK
- **Founded**: 2014 (entrepreneur Karl Gibbs)
- **Tagline**: "British designed and produced" — UK-based alarm manufacturer

## Business Model
**D2C/B2C primary** with hybrid monitoring options: both **24/7 professionally-monitored** and **family-monitored** plans. Also operates a **lone-worker alarm** line.

## Core Products
- **24/7 Connect** — neck alarm, wristband option, 100m range from base unit, landline-based, battery backup, bath/shower-proof, add-on automatic fall detection
- **SureSafeGO** — flagship **mobile GPS tracker alarm** with auto fall detection, GPS, talk-through pendant, UK-wide mobile coverage, NOT affected by landline digital switchover
- **SureSafe Dementia Tracker** — lockable strap wristwatch, fall detection, GPS, family app
- **SureSafe Lone Worker Alarm** — same hardware for B2B lone-worker (watch or pendant)
- **SureSafeGO Family Monitored** — for self-managed family alerts

## Pricing (Indicative)
- SureSafeGO 24/7 monitored: **from £75.00** (one-off device)
- Dementia Tracker: **from £125.00** (one-off)
- Lone Worker: **from £75.00**
- Monthly monitoring subscriptions layered on device purchase

## Reach
Direct-to-consumer UK brand; positioned as "leading provider of personal alarms within the UK" per their marketing (unverified claim vs Appello/Careline365 scale). 0800 061 4501 phone sales.

## TSA Status
UK-based 24/7 monitoring centres; partners with TSA-aligned operations.

## Tech Stack
Mobile-first (4G pendants + GPS); landline base-station for home variant; in-house family-monitoring app for non-professional option.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Family-monitored option** is a UK-unique model — appeals to cost-conscious families who don't want monthly monitoring fees
- British-designed positioning resonates with UK buyers (MyLifeLine is SA-designed)
- Mobile pendant with GPS + fall detection + talk-through (feature parity with MyLifeLine EV07BA)
- Dementia tracker with non-removable strap addresses wandering risk (unique SKU)

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **No B2B white-label SaaS** for resellers — purely D2C
- Smaller brand awareness vs Careline365 / Taking Care (8k+ reviews vs probably hundreds)
- Hardware purchase model (vs MyLifeLine's subscription model) requires higher upfront
- Not as well established in South African / international markets (MyLifeLine is in 7 countries)
- 100m pendant range on home unit (vs MyLifeLine's mobile-anywhere positioning)
