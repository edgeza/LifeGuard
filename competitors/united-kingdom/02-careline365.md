# Careline365 (Appello Group)

- **Website**: https://careline.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: Norwich, UK (operational) / New Milton, Hampshire (Appello Group HQ)
- **Founded**: Careline365 as a consumer brand acquired by Appello Group; CarelineUK brand heritage dates to 1980s
- **Parent**: **Appello Group** (160+ staff, founded 1988; formed from 2014 merger of CarelineUK + Cirrus Communication Systems + Appello Telehealth)
- **Trustpilot**: 4.6/5 stars, **8,644 reviews** (claimed most reviewed UK personal alarm provider)

## Business Model
**B2C primary**, with B2B ARC platform (CareNet EVO) licensed to other ARCs across UK/Europe.

## Core Products
- **In-home pendant alarm** (digital, no landline required, 300m/984ft range, lifetime warranty, 5-yr battery)
- **Fall detector pendant** (auto fall detection)
- **SOS GPS Alarm** — mobile GPS pendant, two-way voice through pendant, optional fall detection
- **Alarm watch**
- **Key safes**
- **24/7 UK-based Emergency Care Team** (TSA accredited)

## Pricing (Indicative)
- Personal Alarm monthly plan: **£19.99/month**
- Fall Detector plan: **£16.99/month**
- GPS Pendant: ~£24.99/month (33% discount codes common)
- Annual plans save ~£100; NHS staff £20 discount on fall alarms

## Reach
- **54,080+ emergency calls handled in last year** per their site
- Careline365 advertises "more 5-star reviews than any other personal alarm provider in UK"
- 10-second average response time (claimed)
- Sister brands Telecare24 / LifeConnect24 / Telecare Choice sit in a **different parent group** (Telecare24 Ltd, Cardiff)

## TSA Status
TSA-accredited Emergency Care Team.

## Tech Stack
Hybrid — runs its own consumer ARC backed by **Appello's CareNet EVO** digital call-handling platform (Microsoft Azure + AWS + Genesys); first end-to-end digital ARC in UK; 1m+ digital calls handled.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Pure-play direct-to-consumer UK leader with massive Trustpilot moat
- Owned by Appello — gets world-class digital ARC infrastructure
- Mobile GPS pendant with two-way voice in the pendant (a feature MyLifeLine also has)
- High review volume is a defensive moat vs any new entrant

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- 10-second response is SLOW vs MyLifeLine's promise of SMS-to-5-contacts in ~3 seconds
- No white-label / reseller B2B SaaS platform like MyLifeLine offers to security companies
- Higher price point than the MyLifeLine model
- UK-centric; no international reseller footprint (MyLifeLine operates in 7 countries)
