# Progress Lifeline

- **Website**: https://www.progresslifeline.org.uk (shop: https://shop.progresslifeline.org.uk)
- **Headquarters**: Leyland, Lancashire, UK
- **Parent**: **Progress Housing Group** (housing association / social enterprise, Lancashire)
- **Founded**: 1990s as a service to Progress Housing tenants; opened commercially

## Business Model
**B2G/B2B primary + B2C** — operates the Lancashire County Council TEC contract, also commercial customers.

## Core Products
- **Personal Alarm & Pendant** — £23.74/mo + VAT (3-month subscription min)
- **Digital Alarm Pendant**
- **Emergency Home Response (EHR)** — £16.18/mo + VAT — provides physical responder who attends to lift person from non-injurious fall (only in Lancashire area); 24/7
- **Bed/Chair sensors, Falls detectors, Smoke detectors**
- **Wellbeing calls / weekly visits** package

## Reach
Largest single local-authority TEC contract — **Lancashire CC subsidised service to all adult residents regardless of Care Act eligibility**; supplies assessment, installation, equipment free of charge (subsidised).

## TSA Status
TSA member; Lancashire County Council contract holder.

## Tech Stack
Tunstall equipment (Lifeline home units + MyAmie pendant).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **In-home emergency responder service** — physical lift-from-floor team. A unique service line vs any MyLifeLine offering.
- Local authority contract scale (whole county)
- Subsidised pricing for residents (Council-funded element) is unique

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Geographic limitation** — emergency response service only in Lancashire
- No mobile GPS pendant
- No B2B SaaS / reseller platform
- Hardware-centric (Tunstall); no proprietary platform
- Smaller consumer brand than Careline365 / SureSafe
