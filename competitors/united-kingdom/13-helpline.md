# Helpline (helpline.co.uk)

- **Website**: https://helpline.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: Colchester, Essex, UK (helpline247.co.uk contact: 01206 769779; HQ: 03458 247 999)
- **Founded**: 2007 by Maureen Cable (former nurse — founded after seeing the "First Golden Hour" problem in hospitals)

## Business Model
**Pure B2C** — small, nurse-led direct-to-consumer personal alarm provider; regional installation & support teams.

## Core Products
- **Lifeline pendant (landline base unit)** — primary home product
- **Pendant/wrist strap**
- **24/7 Monitoring + Falls Pick Up Plan** — £11.17/mo upgrade option
- **Digital-enabled alarms** (post-analogue switchover)
- Mobile pendant options

## Pricing
- Base monitoring plan — competitive vs Careline365/Taking Care
- 24/7 upgrade: +£11.17/mo (small premium for professional ARC monitoring)
- Replacement pendant fee if lost

## Reach
- Self-described "UK's leading provider of personal lifeline alarms, serving thousands of families across the country" — claim smaller than Careline365/Taking Care
- Nationwide delivery (next-day)

## TSA Status
"Works in partnership with TSA" (helpline.co.uk blog) — not the same as full TSA accreditation but engaged.

## Tech Stack
Standard pendant + base-station; mobile pendants being rolled out.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Nurse-founded brand story** — authentic clinical credibility
- "First Golden Hour" narrative resonates with worried adult children
- Smaller = more personal customer service (per their messaging)

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Smaller scale** vs Careline365/Taking Care/Age UK
- No mobile GPS pendant as a hero SKU
- No B2B SaaS / reseller programme
- Less tech-forward than Appello/Careium infrastructure
- Smaller review volume / brand awareness
