# Tunstall Healthcare (UK) Ltd

- **Website**: https://www.tunstall.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: Whitley Bridge, Yorkshire, UK
- **Founded**: 1957 (UK arm incorporated 1977); Group HQ in Spain since 2022 buyout by C Capital/Atlantic Bridge
- **Parent**: Tunstall Group (formerly listed, now PE-owned; C Capital and Atlantic Bridge acquired in 2022)
- **Group Revenue**: UK revenue ~£100m+ (FY2019 £100m, with £68m core UK); group supports 5m+ people globally

## Business Model
**B2G / B2B primary** — dominant UK supplier of telecare hardware, software platforms and ARC services to local authorities, housing associations and NHS trusts. Recently launched a **B2C "Tunstall 365"** consumer personal-alarm service on the Lifeline Digital hub.

## Core Products
- **Lifeline Digital** — next-gen IP home hub (Ethernet/WiFi/4G with 3G/2G fallback, CENELEC EN 50134-9, supports 64 wireless sensors, DMP remote management, Tunstall+ app)
- **MyAmie pendant** — wearable trigger (neck/wrist/belt, 75m range, waterproof, 5-yr battery)
- **iVi / Vibby** fall detectors, bogus caller buttons, bed/chair sensors, flood/gas/CO detectors, epilepsy enuresis sensors
- **Tunstall 365** consumer app + service
- **Tunstall Response** — one of the UK's largest ARCs (Mexborough, Yorkshire)

## Pricing (B2C Tunstall 365)
- £25/month (consumer direct), single base unit supports multiple pendants
- Setup/installation quoted per case for B2G contracts

## Reach
- **UK's largest telecare hardware supplier** — historically ~50% UK housing market share
- ~250k ARC connections (via Tunstall Response)
- Lifeline Digital deployed across UK local authorities (digital switchover 2025 driver)

## TSA Status
Founding TSA member, multiple sites QSF-accredited.

## Tech Stack
Fully digital-first; CENELEC SCAIP/NOWIP compliant; own Device Management Platform (DMP); over-the-air updates; partnered with Vodafone multi-network SIM.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- 65+ years brand trust; entrenched in UK local authorities; vertical integration from hardware→platform→ARC
- Genuine digital switchover leadership; Tunstall Response ARC is one of largest in UK
- Massive installation/field-engineering footprint for housing & LA contracts

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- B2C is a small afterthought vs B2B/B2G; product UX is dated (industrial design)
- No standalone mobile GPS pendant for the "anywhere" market; relies on Lifeline Vi for fixed home
- Consumer pricing (£25/mo) is high vs MyLifeLine's R39-R180 ZAR (£1.60-£7.50) wholesale pricing model — but different value prop
- No white-label B2B SaaS for resellers/security companies (the core MyLifeLine business)
