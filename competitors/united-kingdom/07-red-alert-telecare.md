# Red Alert Telecare Ltd

- **Website**: https://www.redalerttelecare.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: UK (Suffolk-based)
- **Founded**: ~2005 (20-year heritage per their site)
- **Business Model**: **B2B/B2G only** — wholesale to local authorities & commercial TEC providers

## Business Model
**Pure B2B/B2G** — does NOT sell direct to consumers. Specialist telecare & telehealth technology assessment, installation, and management for local authorities and commercial care organisations. **Manufacturer-agnostic** — recommends the best equipment for each individual case rather than pushing own-brand.

## Core Services
- Technology Enabled Care (TEC) service delivery
- **Analogue-to-digital switchover programme management** — delivered full programme for Haringey Council (~1,200 residents)
- Equipment assessment, supply, installation, maintenance
- Triage support
- Partnership with **PA Consulting** (10+ years) on Argenti Care Technology partnership

## Reach
- Award-winning (multiple TEC industry awards)
- Trusted by UK local authorities incl. Haringey, plus commercial TEC providers nationwide
- Smaller scale than Appello / Centra Pulse / Tunstall but specialist reputation

## Pricing
Not published — bespoke contract pricing per local authority.

## TSA Status
TSA member (per directory).

## Tech Stack
Manufacturer-agnostic — installs Tunstall, Appello, Legrand Tynetec, Chiptech, Essence, Doro, TeleAlarm, Alcuris, CSL Dualcom, Oysta (per Appello CareNet integrations — same vendor ecosystem).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Manufacturer-agnostic advisory position** — a niche MyLifeLine (single-platform) doesn't serve
- 20 years of analogue→digital switchover expertise — UK-specific competitive moat
- B2G contract expertise MyLifeLine hasn't built
- PA Consulting partnership gives strategy-grade trust signal

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Zero direct consumer product** — entirely services-led
- No mobile pendant hardware of their own (vs MyLifeLine's 4G pendant with SIM)
- No white-label SaaS to resellers/security companies (a key MyLifeLine value prop)
- UK-only, smaller team
- No GPS tracking mobile offering at consumer level
