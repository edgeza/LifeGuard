# Taking Care (formerly PPP Taking Care)

- **Website**: https://taking.care (legacy: https://www.ppptakingcare.co.uk redirects to taking.care)
- **Headquarters**: Ashburton, Devon, UK (operations); London (AXA Health HQ)
- **Founded**: Aid-Call brand 1984; AXA PPP healthcare acquired Aid-Call from Age UK in **2016**; rebranded as PPP Taking Care in 2017; further rebranded to **Taking Care**
- **Parent**: **AXA Health (AXA PPP healthcare Group)** — FTSE 100 AXA SA subsidiary
- **Headcount**: ~150 staff in 2017; expanded since

## Business Model
**B2C primary + white-label to charities (Age UK, Age Co Personal Alarms)** + B2G contracts with 20+ local authorities.

## Core Products
- **Digital Personal Alarm** (no landline, 300m range, 5-yr battery, lifetime warranty)
- **Digital Fall Alarm** (auto fall detection, 2-yr pendant battery)
- **Taking Care Anywhere** (GPS fall detector, anywhere in UK, talk-through pendant)
- **Alarm watch** (Tunstall hardware, 12-mo warranty)
- **Key safe** (£65.99 one-off)
- **Emergency Resolution Centres** — 3 UK-based, **TSA QSF-accredited**

## Pricing (Indicative, Excl VAT)
- Digital Personal Alarm: **£22.49/mo** or £247.39/yr (1 mo free)
- Digital Fall Alarm: **£28.49/mo** or £313.39/yr
- Taking Care Anywhere GPS: **£31.49/mo** or £346.39/yr

- Trustpilot: **3,517 service reviews**

## Reach
- **~100,000+ customers** (per their TSA profile) — one of UK's largest TEC providers
- Serves 20+ local authority contracts (took over Chichester Careline in 2019)
- White-label provider to **Age UK Personal Alarms** and **Age Co Personal Alarms**
- ISO 9001:2015 accredited

## TSA Status
TSA QSF-accredited; 35+ years industry experience.

## Tech Stack
**Tunstall hardware** (Lifeline Vi / Lifeline Digital) — confirmed technology partnership with Tunstall 2020; Appello/Mobile platform for CareNet integration in some contracts.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Insurance-backed financial stability (AXA) — strong for trust & longevity
- Powers the official Age UK / Age Co personal-alarm brands — exceptional brand-association moat
- Multiple UK-based QSF-accredited ERCs (3) — operationally mature
- 30+ years data on TEC outcomes

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Not a mobile-first / GPS-first player** — Anywhere is the only mobile SKU; pendant is the primary product
- No white-label SaaS for security/reseller partners (different value chain than MyLifeLine)
- Premium pricing (£22-32/mo) — higher than digital-only competitors
- 3-second MyLifeLine panic vs traditional alarm-response workflow is faster for next-of-kin notification
