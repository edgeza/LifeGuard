# Careium UK (formerly Centra Pulse / Invicta Telecare)

- **Website**: https://www.careium.co.uk
- **Centra Group**: https://www.centragroup.org.uk
- **Headquarters**: Newcastle (The Catalyst); operations across UK
- **Founded**: Invicta Telecare founded ~30 years ago (1990s); rebranded **Centra Pulse** under Circle Housing; acquired by **Doro Group** (Swedish) in Sept 2019; further rebranded **Careium UK** when Doro divested its Care division in 2022
- **Ownership**: Careium AB (Swedish public company, listed on Nasdaq Stockholm)

## Business Model
**B2G/B2B primary** — one of UK's largest ARC operators and TEC monitoring providers to local authorities (Kent CC, Sheffield CC, many housing associations).
Also runs **Out-of-Hours** call handling for councils.

## Core Products
- **UMO platform** — proprietary call-handling platform ("one of the leading systems in the industry")
- **In-home dispersed alarms**
- **Mobile telecare solutions** — Vega (mobile telecare), Limmex Emergency Watch
- **Protect and Check** — automated welfare checks (medication reminders, appointment prompts)
- **Out-of-Hours contact centre services** for councils (e.g. Sevenoaks DC)
- **B2C Pulse Connect** for individuals

## Reach
- **~200,000 connections** across all services (post-Doro acquisition)
- **150,000 emergency calls/month** answered (per LinkedIn)
- **100,000+ TEC customers** monitored
- 180+ ARC staff
- Major contracts: Kent CC, Sheffield CC, Sandwell, Blackpool, Cumbria CC, North Lanarkshire CC, Dumfries & Galloway, etc.

## TSA Status
TSA member, large accredited ARC.

## Tech Stack
UMO platform (proprietary); Vega mobile telecare (Evron); Limmex watches.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Top-3 UK ARC by connection volume** — 200k connections scale
- Publicly-listed parent (Careium AB on Nasdaq Stockholm) — financial transparency
- Out-of-hours call handling is an adjacent B2G revenue line no MyLifeLine SKU addresses
- 30-year heritage with UK local authorities

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **No consumer-branded mobile panic-button product** — Vega and Limmex are part of B2G contracts
- No white-label SaaS for security-company resellers
- Hardware partner (Evron Vega, Limmex) not as well-known as Tunstall / Chiptech
- No UK retail brand (rebranded away from Centra Pulse consumer-facing identity)
