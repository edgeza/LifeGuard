# Appello (Appello Careline / CareNet EVO)

- **Website**: https://appello.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: New Milton, Hampshire, UK (international presence: Botswana, Australia, Sri Lanka)
- **Founded**: 1988; Appello as standalone entity formed 2014 from merger of CarelineUK + Cirrus + Appello Telehealth
- **Headcount**: 160+ (+21.7% YoY)

## Business Model
**Multi-sided platform:**
1. **B2B ARC platform** — licenses **CareNet EVO** digital call-handling software to other monitoring centres across UK/Europe (also runs UK's largest ARC)
2. **B2C via Appello Careline** (now branded Careline365)
3. **B2G housing/LA** — Smart Living Solutions digital telecare for 650+ developments / 40,000+ residents
4. **Hardware** — SmartLife (UK's first interoperable digital dispersed alarm), SmartBridge (analogue→digital converter)

## Core Products
- **CareNet EVO** — cloud-hosted call-handling platform on Microsoft Azure + AWS + Genesys; supports NOWIP (BS8521-2), SCAIP (EN50134-9); first UK platform to handle 2 million digital calls
- **SmartLife** — UK's first interoperable digital dispersed alarm (works with products from multiple manufacturers — unique)
- **SmartBridge** — cloud analogue→digital converter
- **Appello Careline** — consumer service (rebranded to Careline365)
- **Out-of-hours call handling** for LAs and housing providers

## Reach
- **400,000+ connections** monitored at the Appello ARC (the UK's largest)
- **50,000+ residents** on fully digital Smart Living Solutions
- **4 million+ digital end-to-end calls** placed across Appello systems
- Powers monitoring centres for Sovereign Network Group, Hanover Scotland, Manchester City Council, etc.

## TSA Status
Long-standing TSA member; QSF-accredited ARC.

## Tech Stack
Microsoft Azure + AWS + Genesys cloud stack; SCAIP/NOWIP compliant; CENELEC aligned; integrates with 10+ telecare OEMs (Tunstall, Cair, Appello, Legrand-Tynetec, Doro, TeleAlarm, Alcuris, Chiptech, Essence, CSL Dualcom, Oysta).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **UK's largest digital ARC** — 400k connections vs MyLifeLine's total customer base in single thousands
- Platform plays — Appello licenses the same technology to competitors (a SaaS model MyLifeLine also does, but at much smaller scale)
- Multi-protocol / interoperable hardware story (SmartLife) is unique
- Out-of-hours call handling for LAs is an adjacent revenue line MyLifeLine doesn't have
- 4M+ digital calls is a credibility proxy for platform reliability

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Doesn't have a self-branded mobile GPS pendant** for the consumer panic-button market (where MyLifeLine dominates)
- 3-second SMS-to-contacts model is MyLifeLine's; Appello relies on ARC-mediated response workflow (slower)
- No white-label SaaS for security company resellers in the same way MyLifeLine sells to SA security firms
- Hardware-agnostic platform approach sacrifices single-vendor simplicity
