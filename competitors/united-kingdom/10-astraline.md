# Astraline (Johnnie Johnson Housing Trust)

- **Website**: https://www.astraline.co.uk
- **Headquarters**: UK (originally a Johnnie Johnson Housing Trust subsidiary)
- **Founded**: Trading as Astraline; ~75,000+ customers (per TSA profile)
- **Parent**: Johnnie Johnson Housing Trust (housing association)

## Business Model
**Hybrid B2C + B2G** — provides TEC services to JJ Housing tenants and private individuals; also delivers TEC services to other housing associations and local authorities.

## Core Products
- **Astraline at Home Alarm — Broadband** — £18.25/mo + £50 set-up (TeleAlarm base unit, router-connected)
- **Astraline Personal Alarm Watch** — £17.20/mo + £190 set-up
- **Astraline Personal Alarm** (home unit) — £22.25/mo + £50 set-up
- **Pearl Advanced Pendant** (with fall detection) — £27.25/mo + £50 set-up
- **Astraline Go Pendant** (mobile, Chiptech Go) — £39.00/mo + £50 set-up
- **Pebble Pendant Alarm** (Zintouch-powered mobile) — £25.00/mo + £50 set-up
- **Wearable sensors, fall detectors, smoke alarms, CO sensors, flood sensors**

## Pricing (starting from £3.50/week per their FAQ)

## Reach
- **70,000-75,000 customers** (one of UK's largest single housing-association TEC providers)
- Serves Johnnie Johnson Housing residents + private + other housing associations

## TSA Status
TSA member, long-standing.

## Tech Stack
TeleAlarm, Chiptech (Pearl Advanced, Chiptech Go), Zintouch — multi-vendor hardware strategy.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Multi-vendor hardware partnerships** (TeleAlarm, Chiptech, Zintouch) — flexible SKU matrix
- "Astraline at Home Alarm — Broadband" is a true broadband-based offering for the post-analogue UK market
- Watch SKU (£17.20/mo) is one of the cheapest watch-format personal alarms in UK
- Strong social-housing heritage

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- No white-label B2B SaaS for resellers
- Higher set-up fees (£50-£190) than consumer-friendly competitors
- Single-parent dependency on JJ Housing Trust
- No mobile-anywhere emergency response team (like Progress Lifeline has)
