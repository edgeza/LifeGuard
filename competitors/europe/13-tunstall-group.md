## Differentiation

Tunstall is the **vertically integrated incumbent of European telecare** — hardware + cloud platform + ARC + service all under one roof. In Spain, Televida is the largest private teleasistencia operator with 190,000 users and 6 ARC centres. Versus competitors that are single-layer (e.g. Chiptech is hardware-only, Skyresponse is platform-only), Tunstall owns the whole stack. Post-2022 PE ownership has accelerated digital switchover leadership (Lifeline Digital) and B2C diversification (Tunstall 365).

## Strengths

- ✅ Vertically integrated — hardware + platform + ARC + service.
- ✅ 65+ years heritage — institutional trust.
- ✅ UK's largest ARC (Tunstall Response, ~250k connections).
- ✅ Spain's largest private teleasistencia operator (Televida, 190k users).
- ✅ Digital-switchover-ready hardware (Lifeline Digital).
- ✅ PE-funded innovation roadmap (post-2022).
- ✅ Tunstall 365 B2C consumer entry.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ B2C is small afterthought vs B2B/B2G.
- ❌ Consumer pricing (£25/mo) is premium.
- ❌ No white-label SaaS for resellers (MyLifeLine's wedge).
- ❌ Industrial-design reputation — dated aesthetics.
- ❌ UK-centric brand recognition outside UK + Spain.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **White-label reseller wedge**: Tunstall sells direct to LAs/housing; doesn't offer white-label SaaS. MyLifeLine's reseller model is the missing tier.
- **Mobile GPS complement**: Tunstall 365 is home-centric; EV07BA + Tunstall 365 = bundled home + away.
- **EU mainland expansion**: Tunstall has limited penetration in FR/IT/NL; MyLifeLine's 7-country footprint overlaps.
