## Product lineup

- **Navigil 580 wellness wristwatch** — analog LTE Cat-M1 wristwatch phone with GPS, A-GPS, BLE (beacons), heart-rate monitoring, activity/sleep tracking, AI-analyzed wellness trends. Up to 2 weeks battery on 4G. IP67. Diameter 46 mm.
- **Navigil S1 emergency watch** — GSM/GPS watch phone; smallest analog watch in telecare market; multi-day battery.
- **Navigil Rafael / Navigil Service** — cloud SaaS for alarm routing, wellness analytics, caregiver dashboards, AI-based change detection.
- **Mini beacons** — Bluetooth beacons for indoor location + dementia wandering alerts.
- **Wearer profiles** — dementia, elderly with long-term conditions, active elderly, cognitively impaired.

## Differentiation

Navigil is the **only EU telecare watchmaker** that ships a real analog wristwatch form factor (not a chunky smartwatch) with multi-week LTE battery life, GPS + A-GPS + indoor BLE beacons, and AI-driven wellness analytics. The form factor matters: in customer testimonials, Navigil's watch is "the only product where wearers aren't labeled as needing help." S1 was the world's smallest analog telecare watch at launch. Direct competitor to Navigil: Apple Watch (smartwatch) and VitalConnect/Philips (medical wearables) — but neither matches the analog-watch aesthetic + multi-week battery + telecare-SaaS bundle.

## Strengths

- ✅ Analog watch design — avoids the stigma of medical-device wearables.
- ✅ Multi-week battery life (vs 1-2 days for Apple Watch) on LTE Cat-M1.
- ✅ Full SaaS for partners — AI wellness analytics, recipient reachability, smart notification.
- ✅ Indoor + outdoor location (BLE beacons + GPS).
- ✅ 30-country footprint; reference customers include AddSecure Smart Care (Finland) and Stella Kotipalvelut.
- ✅ 2014 S1 + 2018 S3 + 2019 580 — three generations of product evolution.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No direct consumer brand — must go through B2B partner.
- ❌ Wristwatch form factor is premium-priced; not accessible to all segments.
- ❌ No Apple-style consumer features (apps, payments, music).
- ❌ Service provider overhead means higher end-customer price.
- ❌ Limited brand awareness outside the Nordics + DACH.
