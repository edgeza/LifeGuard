## Differentiation

CareTech is now **a brand layer inside Careium UK** — its 50,000 connections and ARC operations have been folded into the Careium group alongside Centra Pulse, Eldercare, and Connect. The CareTech name persists for legacy contract continuity. Distinct from **SECOM CareTech** (the UK security company that provides "CareTech" personal alarm + Smart Wellness smart-home monitoring services) — different entity.

## Strengths (historical CareTech, now Careium)
