## Product lineup

- **NOVO carephone (NOVO / NOVO Go / NOVO 4G)** — IP/4G digital carephone with SCAIP protocol, 4G/3G/2G + LAN, VoLTE, Bluetooth Low Energy for peripherals, remote management via CMP (Care Management Portal). Two-way speech. IP connection.
- **NOVO Go mobile pendant** — 4G/2G mobile telecare pendant for use outside the home; two-way audio; GPS location; wrist or neck wear; integrates with NOVO home hub.
- **Reach IP 4G (Tynetec)** — UK digital at-home alarm with VoLTE; multi-network SIM (roaming); CENELEC-compliant; up to 32 bi-directional peripherals; pre-PSTN-switchover migration path.
- **Reach at-home range** — Reach, Reach Plus, Reach Plus GSM.
- **Advent xt** — warden-call system for grouped living.
- **Advent xt + Intelligent Care** — cloud-based activities-of-living monitoring.
- **Touch pendants (ZXT600/606/607)** — wearable triggers.
- **Tynetec Roam GPS** — UK lone-worker GPS device.
- **Full peripheral ecosystem** — 100+ telecare devices (bed/chair sensors, fall detectors, CO/smoke, property exit, enuresis, epilepsy monitors, bogus-caller buttons, telehealth BP/blood glucose interfaces).
- **Pulse CMP (Tynetec) / Care Management Portal (Legrand Care)** — remote device programming, status monitoring, alarm handling.

## Differentiation

Legrand Care is **the largest single telecare OEM in Europe by revenue** — the consolidation of Tynetec (UK), NEAT (Sweden/Denmark), Intervox (France), Jontek (UK ARC platform), and Aid Call (nurse call) under one Legrand-owned roof. Its Reach IP 4G and NOVO families set the digital-telecare reference architecture for the UK PSTN switchover (2027) and the EU digital migration. CENELEC TS 50134-9 + SCAIP compliance is built in. Direct competitors: TeleAlarm (TA74), Chiptech (SEVEN), Essence (Care@Home).

## Strengths

- ✅ Five-brand consolidation gives Legrand Care category-leading product breadth.
- ✅ Legrand Group funding + distribution (90+ countries, 36,000 staff).
- ✅ Full SCAIP / TS 50134-9 / VoLTE compliance — UK digital switchover-ready.
- ✅ NOVO Go = one of the few **professional mobile telecare pendants** in market (vs consumer SIM-pendant).
- ✅ 100+ peripheral ecosystem — one-stop for housing-association / LA tenders.
- ✅ CMP cloud portal — fleet management at scale.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No direct consumer brand — B2B/B2G only.
- ❌ Tynetec Reach IP 4G pricing is at premium vs Chiptech SEVEN ⚠️.
- ❌ Industrial-design reputation — older Tynetec hardware aesthetic.
- ❌ No built-in wellness/AI analytics (vs Navigil/Vivago).
- ❌ Multiple legacy brands create integration complexity for partners.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Mobile pendant partnership**: NOVO Go + EV07BA together cover indoor + outdoor mobile use for partners. Legrand Care could source MyLifeLine hardware as a companion SKU.
- **ARC-as-a-Service gap**: NOVO hub connects to ARCs; a smaller, faster white-label ARC platform (MyLifeLine's) could be a complement for security-company resellers who don't need a full ARC.
- **Price-point white-label**: Legrand Care prices are B2B-set; a budget-tier white-label pendant (MyLifeLine) could be sold into Legrand Care's customer base for cost-sensitive segments.
