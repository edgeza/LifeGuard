## Differentiation

Loxone is the **premium Austrian smart-home automation system** with strong DACH market share via a certified-Partner installer network. In PERS terms, Loxone is a **complementary infrastructure layer** — its motion/door sensors can trigger "no activity" alerts via partner logic, but Loxone itself doesn't ship a pendant, an ARC, or a caregiver alert flow. It's the closest European analogue to Control4 (US) or Crestron. For PERS operators, Loxone is the **upstream home-automation layer** whose events can flow into a telecare ARC.

## Strengths

- ✅ Premium brand in DACH smart-home.
- ✅ Miniserver designed for 24/7 multi-year operation — no fans, low-power.
- ✅ Tree + Air dual-bus strategy covers new-build + retrofit.
- ✅ Large Partner network (electricians / smart-home installers).
- ✅ Apple HomeKit certified.
- ✅ Strong aftermarket / service-orientation (Loxone Partner programme).

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Not a PERS product** — requires partner development for telecare use cases.
- ❌ No ARC, no caregiver app, no SOS pendant.
- ❌ High install cost — €5k+ for typical smart-home deployment.
- ❌ Partner-channel complexity for new entrants.
- ❌ Limited recognition outside DACH.
