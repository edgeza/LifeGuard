## Differentiation

Skyresponse is the **leading pure-cloud ARC platform in the Nordics + UK** — 100% cloud-native (not cloud-washed). Its decentralised ARC model (handle alarms from any connected device) is a genuine innovation over physical-ARC models. Direct competitor: Verklizan UMO (NL/DE strong), Skyresponse (Nordic/UK strong). Versus both, Skyresponse is the **most modern** in cloud architecture and mobile-app ergonomics. Chubb's UK Cloud Care Control is built on Skyresponse — significant UK footprint.

## Strengths

- ✅ 100% cloud-native — true SaaS, no on-prem.
- ✅ Decentralised ARC — alarms from any connected device, anywhere.
- ✅ ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, QSF certified — UK public-sector ready.
- ✅ Chubb partnership → UK NHS/housing reach.
- ✅ TeleAlarm CMS partnership → EU DACH reach.
- ✅ Open APIs — vendor-independent.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Platform only** — no own hardware, no consumer brand.
- ❌ Smaller than Verklizan (1.1m end-users) on total monitored population.
- ❌ Nordic/UK-centric — limited Southern Europe.
- ❌ Limited consumer marketing — operator audience only.
