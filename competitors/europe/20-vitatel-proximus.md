## Differentiation

Vitatel is the **Belgian équivalent of France's Bluelinea** — a domestic télé-assistance operator with owned cloud ARC, partnered with the national telco (Proximus) for the CCaaS backbone. The Proximus/Vitatel relationship is unusual: Vitatel is the service operator; Proximus NXT provides Genesys Cloud CX and Voice Managed Services. Versus Proximus's own "Home and Care" product (more home-automation than PERS), Vitatel is the dedicated telecare player.

## Strengths

- ✅ Owned Belgian ARC with cloud-native architecture.
- ✅ Average client age 85 — captures the highest-need segment.
- ✅ Proximus cloud + Genesys CCaaS — enterprise-grade reliability.
- ✅ Voice Managed Services — power-outage continuity.
- ✅ Helan health-insurer partnership — distribution reach.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Belgium-only — limited cross-border.
- ❌ Smaller scale than Careium, Tunstall, Legrand Care.
- ❌ No white-label SaaS for resellers.
- ❌ Limited brand recognition outside BE.
- ❌ No consumer-facing app for family (ARC-mediated).
