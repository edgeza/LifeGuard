## Differentiation

Chiptech's standout feature is **dual-SIM with roaming algorithm** — SEVEN automatically selects the strongest UK network (EE, Vodafone, Three, O2) via primary SIM and falls back to secondary SIM on core-network outage. Combined with **voice-guided installation** (Audio Assisted Install — allows family member or carer to install without an engineer), Chiptech is the **most-resilient digital alarm** for UK digital switchover. Direct competitor: Legrand Care Tynetec Reach IP 4G, TeleAlarm TA74.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Dual SIM roaming** — most resilient cellular connectivity in market.
- ✅ **Voice-guided install** — reduces install cost; democratises deployment.
- ✅ Open-protocol (SCAIP, TS 50134-9, CONTACT ID) — works with all major ARCs.
- ✅ Purpose-built GO-4 pendant — paired "at home + away" solution.
- ✅ CENELEC / SCAIP / digital-switchover-ready.
- ✅ SmartCare Cloud / Locate / Response — full partner software stack.
- ✅ Manufacturing in Christchurch — quality-controlled.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Smaller UK brand awareness vs Tynetec, TeleAlarm, Tunstall.
- ❌ NZ HQ — UK/EU customers prefer EU-based supplier for data residency.
- ❌ No owned ARC.
- ❌ SmartCare Response family app less mature than i-care® plus (Careium).
- ❌ Limited EU mainland deployment (UK primary).
