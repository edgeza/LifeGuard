## Product lineup

- **UMO Platform** — cloud-based alarm-receiving-centre (ARC) software. Digital + analogue alarm handling; SCAIP/TS50134-9/CONNECT-ID protocols.
- **UMO Hosted** — managed SaaS (private cloud) — most customers use this.
- **UMO Demo / Trial / Production** — three-tier onboarding model.
- **Digital apps + web portals** — operator-facing.
- **Open-platform philosophy** — UMO connects to **250+ healthcare products from 100+ product partners** (Tunstall, TeleAlarm, Legrand Care, Essence, Chiptech, etc.).
- **Modules** — alarm monitoring + call centre + lone worker + GPS location + video + security.
- **EsPider (legacy name)** — the original ARC product line, superseded by UMO.

## Differentiation

Verklizan UMO is **the largest open-platform ARC SaaS in Europe** — 1.1m+ monitored end-users, 250+ compatible devices, 300+ customers across 5 countries. Versus single-vendor stacks (Tunstall, Legrand Care), UMO is **vendor-independent** — a service provider can mix Tunstall base units, TeleAlarm pendants, Essence Care@Home sensors, and Chiptech GO all in one ARC. This is the closest European competitor to **Cleric (Skyresponse's ARC equivalent)**. Versus Skyresponse, UMO is more established in NL/DE/ES, less in UK/Nordics.

## Strengths

- ✅ 1.1m+ end-users — largest independent ARC platform in Europe by user count.
- ✅ 250+ compatible products — true open-platform.
- ✅ Cloud-hosted SaaS — minimal customer IT burden.
- ✅ Tiered onboarding (Demo/Trial/Production) — low-risk customer entry.
- ✅ PE-backed growth investment.
- ✅ Strong NL/DE/ES footprint.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Platform only** — no pendant, no sensors, no consumer brand.
- ❌ Smaller in UK than Skyresponse.
- ❌ ARC operators must staff 24/7 — UMO doesn't sell the staffing.
- ❌ No consumer-facing family app (vs Careium i-care® plus).

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **UMO as upstream ARC**: MyLifeLine's 3-sec-SMS-to-contacts model bypasses the ARC entirely. But for security-company resellers who want a real ARC, Verklizan UMO is the SaaS they should pair with MyLifeLine pendants.
- **API integration**: MyLifeLine's events could flow into UMO's alarm dashboard as "additional channel" — partnership opportunity.
- **NL/DE market access**: Verklizan's NL/DE customer base is a pre-qualified reseller audience.
