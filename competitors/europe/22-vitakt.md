## Product lineup

- **Vitakt-Basis** — landline home unit + wrist + necklace pendant transmitters; connection to PSTN.
- **Vitakt-Vario** — wireless home unit (no landline required), GSM/M2M cellular. Two radio transmitters (wrist + necklace).
- **Vitakt-Hausnotrufbereitschaft** — add-on: key safe + on-call mobile response service (Pflegedienst) for in-home visits.
- **Vitakt-Seniorenportal** — web portal for family + customer management.

## Differentiation

Vitakt is one of the **bundesweit commercial Hausnotruf pure-players** in Germany — independent, no charity affiliation, no religious sponsorship. Direct competitors: Malteser, Johanniter, ASB, DRK (charity-affiliated, often preferred by older German demographic for trust) and Bosch / TeleAlarm (OEM-supplied hardware). Vitakt competes on **commercial flexibility** (no charity donation expectations, modern web portal) versus charity players on trust.

## Strengths

- ✅ Owned nationwide ARC (Vitakt Notruf- und Serviceleitstelle) — 24/7.
- ✅ Both landline (Vitakt-Basis) and GSM (Vitakt-Vario) options.
- ✅ Pflegekassen-approved — fully subsidised for eligible users.
- ✅ Pflegedienst-Hausnotrufbereitschaft add-on — physical-response option.
- ✅ Commercial flexibility vs charity competitors.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Fixed-home only** — no GPS pendant / mobile.
- ❌ Germany-only — no EU expansion.
- ❌ Smaller charity-trust signal vs Malteser/Johanniter.
- ❌ No caregiver app (web-portal-only).
- ❌ No D2C marketing at scale (competes with deep-pocketed charity rivals).
