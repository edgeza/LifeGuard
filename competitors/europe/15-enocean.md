## Differentiation

EnOcean is the **inventor and licensor of the energy-harvesting wireless standard** — sensors that never need batteries because they harvest kinetic, solar, or thermal energy. This is the technological bedrock for **zero-maintenance** smart-home/building sensors. In PERS terms, EnOcean sensors are used by partners (e.g. Gigaset, Kieback&Peter, Schneider Electric) to build AAL/ambient-sensing systems. EnOcean is not a PERS competitor directly; it is a **building-block supplier** that other telecare platforms integrate.

## Strengths

- ✅ Inventor and licensor of energy-harvesting wireless — patent-protected.
- ✅ ISO/IEC 14543-3-1X standardisation.
- ✅ Sub-1GHz + Bluetooth — interoperable.
- ✅ Zero-maintenance sensors — install-and-forget.
- ✅ EnOcean Alliance with 5,000+ products.
- ✅ 24-bit rolling code + AES-128 encryption — secure.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Not a PERS product** — building-block only; needs an integrator.
- ❌ Energy harvesting limits what sensors can do (low power budget).
- ❌ EnOcean Alliance fragmented; integration complexity for newcomers.
- ❌ Competing standards (Zigbee, Z-Wave, Matter) gaining smart-home mindshare.
- ❌ No D2C brand or consumer product.
