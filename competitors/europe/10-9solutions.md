## Product lineup

- **9Solutions cloud-based RTLS platform** — real-time locating system (people, assets, alarms) with room-level accuracy.
- **Safety buttons / personal tags** — BLE-based wrist-worn or pendant safety tags that work as both call buttons and locating devices.
- **HERO lone-worker solution** — mobile app + safety button; man-down / dead-man switch; registered work locations for indoor accuracy; real-time responder view; time-based tasks with auto-alerts.
- **Access control** — RFID / BLE-based door access integrated with locating.
- **Asset tracking** — wheelchairs, beds, medical equipment tracked in real time.
- **Nurse call integration** — wired + wireless call points integrate into the cloud platform.
- **Mobile + web dashboard** — for caregivers and managers; mobile app for responders.

## Differentiation

9Solutions is the **leading indoor-location-based safety system in the Nordics** — 80% of Finland's university hospitals use it. Versus traditional nurse-call (wired, single-room), 9Solutions' RTLS gives room-level accuracy across an entire facility and unifies nurse-call + lone-worker + asset tracking in one cloud platform. Direct competitors: Ascom TeleCARE IP, Tunstall, Sensio Care, Meshtech.

## Strengths

- ✅ 80% of Finland's university hospitals — dominant in its home market.
- ✅ Unified platform: nurse-call + personal safety + RTLS + asset tracking.
- ✅ 100% cloud-based — easier deployment vs legacy wired nurse-call.
- ✅ BLE + cellular + GPS multi-modal locating.
- ✅ Reference deployments at scale (Hospital Nova: 13,000 devices).
- ✅ Man-down / dead-man switch for lone-worker compliance.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Institutional/care-home focus — not a consumer PERS product.
- ❌ Requires BLE infrastructure deployment (cost).
- ❌ Nordic-centric — limited recognition in DACH / Southern Europe.
- ❌ Not a mobile pendant for general seniors; designed for care-staff and dementia residents inside facilities.
- ❌ Competes with entrenched wired nurse-call (Ackermann, Tunstall, Schrack) on retrofit cost.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Discharge transition**: 9Solutions serves care homes; residents who discharge home need a personal PERS pendant. MyLifeLine EV07BA could be the "step-down" product.
- **Family/resident pendant**: 9Solutions tags are facility-located. A pendant that works in the community (MyLifeLine) plus the 9Solutions facility tag = continuum of care.
- **Lone-worker complementary**: HERO is a B2B solution for facility staff. A consumer-facing pendant (MyLifeLine) could be sold to families of vulnerable relatives.
