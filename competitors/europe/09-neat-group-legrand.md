## Product lineup

- **NOVO carephone family** — digital home hub; variants for IP / 4G / 2G / 3G networks. SCAIP protocol; multi-protocol support (NEIP, SCAIP, BS8521-2, RB2000, SIP, etc.).
- **NOVO 4G / NOVO 4G VOICE / NOVO IP** — variants; 4G with VoLTE; multi-network SIM (roaming).
- **CMP (Care Management Portal)** — cloud portal for remote device programming, diagnostics, alarm routing, OTA firmware updates.
- **SMILE** — wrist-worn or neck-worn alarm trigger; small, comfortable; designed for dementia-friendly use.
- **NEAT M2M service** — managed multi-network SIM with monthly billing.
- **Battery backup** — 80 hours standard; 400 hours with extended battery.
- **Bluetooth Low Energy** — for certified telehealth peripherals.

## Differentiation

NEAT pioneered the **first digital telecare system in 2017 (NOVO)** and is now the technical backbone of Legrand Care's offer. Direct competition: TeleAlarm TA74, Chiptech SEVEN, Essence Care@Home, Tunstall Smart Hub. The NOVO family's main selling point is full IP-first architecture + CMA (Cloud Management Portal) + multi-protocol support + extended battery backup (best in class — 400 hours).

## Strengths

- ✅ "First mover" in IP-based digital telecare (2017).
- ✅ Best-in-class battery backup (80h / 400h).
- ✅ Multi-network SIM roaming — resilient connectivity.
- ✅ Cloud Management Portal — fleet-scale device management.
- ✅ Legrand Group distribution muscle.
- ✅ SCAIP/NEIP multi-protocol.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No owned ARC — partner-dependent.
- ❌ Brand awareness is now subsumed under "Legrand Care" — NEAT legacy fading.
- ❌ No D2C channel.
- ❌ Less global recognition than Legrand Care Tynetec (UK) brand.
- ❌ No caregiver app of its own.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Mobile SKU complement**: NEAT's NOVO is fixed-home; MyLifeLine EV07BA could be the mobile pendant that partners bundle with NOVO.
- **CMP-style reseller SaaS**: NEAT's CMP is operator-facing; MyLifeLine's white-label reseller platform is the missing piece for security-company resellers who don't want to operate a fleet portal themselves.
