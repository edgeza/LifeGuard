- **Evondos remote-care system** — web-based dashboard for home-care staff; tracks medication compliance in real time.
- **Evondos mobile app** — for home-care workers.
- **Multi-dose sachet compatibility** — accepts pharmacy-packed dose rolls.
- **Locked medicine repository** — only authorised staff can access (preventing overdose / misuse).

- **Missed-dose alerts** — automated alert to care team when dose not taken.

## Differentiation

Evondos is the **leading automated medication-dispensing service in the Nordics** — 99% dose-accuracy at scale (millions of doses). It addresses a problem MyLifeLine does NOT — pharma-adherence — making it a complementary rather than competing service. Versus traditional pillboxes / dosettes (the default home medication), Evondos adds locked storage + automated reminders + remote-care escalation + video-call support. Direct competitor: Medication-dispensing robots from Careousel, Pivotell, Hero (US); in EU: Pillo (IT).

## Strengths

- ✅ 99% dose-accuracy — clinically validated.
- ✅ Multi-million dose dispensed — proven at scale.
- ✅ Locked storage — overdose / misuse prevention.
- ✅ Two-way video medication call — supports remote verification.
- ✅ Pharmacy integration — sachet dispensing.
- ✅ Strong Finnish municipal adoption.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Not a PERS product** — doesn't address falls / SOS / GPS.
- ❌ Robot form factor — stationary, not wearable.
- ❌ Requires pharmacy integration (sachets) — onboarding cost.
- ❌ Newer in NL/UK/IE — less mature than Nordic ops.
- ❌ Expensive vs simple pillbox.

## MyLifeLine opportunity
