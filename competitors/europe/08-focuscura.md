## Product lineup

- **cAlarm Start** — wired internet-connected home base + pendant alarm. 24/7 monitoring included; self-install.
- **cAlarm Comfort** — GSM wireless home base + pendant; plug-in only (no landline needed).
- **cAlarm Smart accessory pendant** — extra necklace/bracelet pendant with jewellery-grade styling.
- **cAlarm Plus extras** — smart smoke detector with SMS-to-caregiver, "good morning" daily check SMS service via motion sensors, pull-cord additional button (bathroom/bedroom).
- **cKey** — secure home-access solution: key safe or invisible electronic door-opener for caregivers.
- **Bundled cAlarm + cKey** — integrated home safety + access package.

## Differentiation

FocusCura is the **NL-leading consumer-direct personal-alarm brand**, recognised for its simple online ordering, no-contract model, and the cAlarm+cKey bundle that solves both alarm-routing AND caregiver-home-access (a common operational gap in NL elderly care). ASSA ABLOY's 2020 acquisition gave it lock-industry DNA and a deep network of NL housing-association installs. Direct NL competitor: SafeToGo, SeniorenAlarm, and the many LA-subsidised Wmo-hulpmiddelen providers.

## Strengths

- ✅ Owned by ASSA ABLOY — Scandinavian lock-industry DNA; deep housing-association distribution.
- ✅ Pure online D2C ordering — modern, transparent.
- ✅ cAlarm + cKey bundle is unique in NL market — solves two problems.
- ✅ WMO/Wlz framework integration — Dutch municipalities fund cAlarm as "hulpmiddel".
- ✅ No long-term contracts — consumer-friendly.
- ✅ Belgian, Swedish, German distribution.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No mobile GPS pendant — fixed-home only.
- ❌ No built-in fall detection — accessory required.
- ❌ Limited app — SMS-based caregiver alerts.
- ❌ Smaller scale than Tunstall NL, Bosch NL, or Legrand Care NL.
- ❌ NL-focused — limited recognition outside Benelux.
