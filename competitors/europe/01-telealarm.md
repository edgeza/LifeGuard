## Product lineup

- **TA74 4G carephone** — IP/4G home hub, multi-protocol (SCAIP, RBIP, SIP, TS 50134-9, RB2000), 4G VoLTE, LAN, PSTN fallback. Up to 20 wireless transmitters; 869 MHz radio (EU); activity monitoring; vocal reminders; IP32 (TA74) / IP67 (pendant). Remote programming via TeleAlarm Cloud Services.
- **TA74 IO** — adds wired I/O (1 input / 1 relay output) for institutional/grouped-living.
- **TA74 4G T** — PSTN-compatible variant for legacy lines.
- **NurseCall systems** — wireless call systems for residential care / nursing homes.
- **TeleAlarm Cloud Services** — free cloud for installers (pre-program devices before shipping).
- **CMS (Cloud Monitoring System)** — centralized monitoring platform for ARCs.

## Differentiation

TeleAlarm is one of the longest-established European carephone OEMs (since 1956) and competes primarily against Legrand Care's NOVO and Chiptech's SEVEN at the B2B/B2G reseller level. Its TA74 4G is positioned as "Swiss Design — Made in Germany" with full multi-protocol compliance (TS 50134-9, SCAIP, RBIP, SIP), making it the OEM of choice for service providers serving the German Pflegekassen market where protocol compliance and Krankenkassen-listung are non-negotiable.

## Strengths

- ✅ 70-year brand heritage in European carephone engineering.
- ✅ Multi-protocol (SCAIP/TS 50134-9/RBIP/SIP) — drop-in replacement for legacy stockists.
- ✅ Cloud pre-programming reduces installer time-to-deploy.
- ✅ Listed by Deutsche Telekom as IP-fähige Hausnotrufgeräte supplier.
- ✅ TA74 IO variant for grouped living / care homes.
- ✅ EU footprint: Leipzig office + UK contact + Swiss parent.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No consumer-facing brand or D2C channel.
- ❌ No GPS pendant / mobile solution in own catalogue (must partner).
- ❌ No built-in fall detection in core hardware.
- ❌ B2B/B2G sales motion means MyLifeLine's reseller-distribution model is a closer peer competitor than its consumer subscribers.
- ❌ No German Krankenkassen accreditation of its own — depends on accredited service provider.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Mobile pendant SKU**: TeleAlarm customers keep asking for a mobile GPS companion. MyLifeLine's EV07BA pendant could be the partner product for TeleAlarm hardware — sell white-label into TeleAlarm's service-provider base.
- **Caregiver app gap**: TeleAlarm Cloud is installer-facing. A family/caregiver app for TeleAlarm-deployed service providers is open territory.
- **D2C disruption**: TeleAlarm's downstream service providers (Malteser/DRK/Johanniter) all price D2C at €25-€45/mo. A mobile-anywhere pendant at lower price point competes with their mobile add-ons.
