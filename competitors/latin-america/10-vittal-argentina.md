## Differentiation

Argentina's leading private emergency medical response provider, combining 24/7 ambulance dispatch with a telemedicine family-doctor service (VittalDoc) included in all plans at no extra cost. Competes more with SAME (public 107) than with PERS wearables, but addresses the same consumer need (emergency help at home).

## Strengths

- ✅ Established brand trust in Argentine emergency medical services.
- ✅ VittalDoc (family doctor) included free in all plans — a unique bundle value.
- ✅ 24/7 online medical consultations (vittal idoc) — triage capability that can prevent unnecessary ambulance dispatch.
- ✅ Direct hospital coordination — works with user's prepaga for covered transfers.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No wearable, no PERS device — entirely reactive (user must call, cannot auto-detect falls).
- ❌ No GPS tracking or family-notification system — families have no visibility during an event.
- ❌ No transparent pricing — all plans contact-to-sales.
- ❌ Ambulance dispatch — resource-heavy and expensive service model; users may hesitate to call for non-critical situations.
- ❌ Primarily AMBA (Buenos Aires metro) focused — coverage may be thinner in interior provinces.
