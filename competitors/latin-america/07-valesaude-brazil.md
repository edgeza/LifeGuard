- **Rede de Farmácias** — up to 50% discounts at partner pharmacies.
- **Vale Saúde Sempre App** — mobile app for booking, consultations, digital card.

## Differentiation

A health-access subscription (not PERS) that competes with PERS by promising fast, affordable 24/7 access to medical help without leaving home. Its low-cost app-first model appeals to the same demographics as entry-level PERS — price-sensitive Brazilian families seeking health security for parents/elders.

## Strengths

- ✅ Brazilian household name in the subscription-health space.
- ✅ Extremely low price point — `R$39.90/consulta` — accessible to the mass market.
- ✅ 24/7 GP access + nurse orientation via video — a real substitute for calling an ambulance in non-emergency situations.
- ✅ Broad network of physical clinics, labs, and pharmacies — offers both digital and in-person touchpoints.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No PERS device, no fall detection, no GPS tracking, no emergency monitoring — purely consultative.
- ❌ Cannot dispatch emergency response — the service explicitly says "Em caso de emergência, procure um pronto atendimento."
- ❌ No caregiver/family features — individual-only app model.
- ❌ No senior-specific device or interface — assumes smartphone literacy.
