## Product lineup

- **Planes AMD (Atención Médica Domiciliaria)** — home medical visits: médico general dispatched to patient's home for non-life-threatening conditions. Triggered by calling OMT (24/7 phone triage).
- **Orientación Médica Telefónica (OMT)** — 24/7/365 medical triage call centre with trained professionals.
- **Planes de Medicina Prepagada** — full prepaid medical plan including: specialist consultations, procedures, emergency network, ambulance/transfer, hospitalization coverage, seguro de desempleo.
- **Médico en Casa** — broader home-health options beyond emergency visits.
- **Seguro de Desempleo** — pays plan premiums for up to 9 months if subscriber loses job.

## Differentiation

A full prepaid-medicine provider offering a bridge between insurance and emergency response through its AMD (home doctor visits) and 24/7 OMT medical triage. MedPlus is the Colombian competitor that "competes with the hospital" — if a PERS user presses a button and says "I feel sick," MedPlus can send a doctor home, while a traditional PERS can only call an ambulance.

## Strengths

- ✅ Full medical ecosystem — not just emergency response but ongoing care: specialists, exams, hospital network, pharmacy benefits.
- ✅ AMD (home doctor visit) — unique capability PERS companies cannot match. For non-emergency conditions, a doctor at home is more valuable than an ambulance.
- ✅ 24/7 medical triage call centre (OMT) — medically trained operators, not just dispatchers.
- ✅ 8 Colombian cities covered — solid urban coverage.
- ✅ Seguro de desempleo — premium payment relief for unemployed subscribers, addressing a major Colombian consumer anxiety.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No wearable, no automatic fall detection, no GPS tracking — entirely phone-call-driven.
- ❌ No caregiver/family visibility layer — families have no way to monitor or track loved ones.
- ❌ AMD is only for non-life-threatening conditions — does not replace emergency response for falls, strokes, heart attacks.
- ❌ Opaque pricing — plans must be quoted, not self-serve purchasable online.
- ❌ Not designed for seniors living alone — AMD assumes someone is there to make the phone call.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Partner for the PERS gap** — MedPlus has the doctors and the call centre, but not the wearable. MyLifeLine provides the device; MedPlus provides the medical escalation when the button is pressed.
- **Integrate OMT into MyLifeLine's escalation path** — button press → nurse triage (MyLifeLine) → if non-emergency, hand off to MedPlus AMD for a home visit. Reduce ambulance dispatches, lower cost, better patient experience.
- **Offer a "prepagada lite" PERS bundle** — combine a MyLifeLine PERS device with a MedPlus telemedicine + home-visit add-on, creating a mid-tier "private emergency+care" package at a mass-market Colombian price.
- **Expat Colombian market** — adult children in the US/Spain who want to ensure their parents in Colombia have private medical access. MedPlus has the network; MyLifeLine has the monitoring.
