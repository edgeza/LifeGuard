# Blue Security Mobile App

> **Category:** Regional (Durban/KZN) armed-response + panic app
> **Website:** https://bluesecurity.co.za/mobile-panic-app/

## Overview
Blue Security is a Durban-based armed-response company serving KwaZulu-Natal. Their mobile app extends panic and alarm notifications beyond the home alarm contract to wherever the user is. App sends GPS to Blue's control centre, dispatches armed officers / ambulance / SAPS / roadside.

## Core Offering
- **In-app panic button** with live GPS location.
- **Rapid response** from Blue Security armed officers, ambulance services, SAPS, roadside assistance.
- **Home/business alarm notifications** — full alarm monitoring on the same app.
- Focused on KZN coast (Durban, Pietermaritzburg, surrounding).

## Pricing
Likely bundled with Blue Security alarm contract (not publicly listed).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Regional density** — KZN coverage is more uniform than national apps in that province.
- Combined alarm + personal-panic on one app — convenient for existing alarm customers.
- Local control room = faster regional response than national apps.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- Geographic limit to KZN — irrelevant outside the region.
- No wearable pendant or medical-grade elderly device.
- No fall detection, no HR, no medical profile workflow.
- No B2B SaaS platform.

## Target Customer
Blue Security alarm customers; KZN residents; Durban/Pietermaritzburg urban users.
