# Gauteng e-Panic Button

> **Category:** Government-affiliated panic app (Gauteng Provincial Government)
> **Website:** Google Play: za.gov.gautengPanicButton_new

## Overview
The Gauteng e-Panic Button is a provincial-government-issued emergency-response app providing access to a nationwide network of private armed-response and medical-emergency services. Targets Gauteng residents but works nationally. Free app.

## Core Offering
- **Standalone panic button** dispatching private armed response + medical.
- **Add loved ones** to your account.
- **Nationwide** coverage through partner networks.

## Pricing
Free (government-issued).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Free** — disrupts subscription pricing.
- Government endorsement / trust signal.
- "Add loved ones" family feature.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable hardware.
- Government-app UX is typically slower to evolve than commercial.
- No fall detection, no HR, no elderly medical profile.
- No B2B platform.
- Limited marketing push compared to commercial competitors.

## Target Customer
Gauteng residents; lower-income users wanting a free government panic app.
