# Copwatch Panic App

> **Category:** Regional armed-response app (Gauteng), built on AURA
> **Website:** https://www.copwatch.co.za (Play Store: com.aura.panic.copwatch.prod)

## Overview
Copwatch Security is a Johannesburg-based armed-response company with an AURA-platform panic app. Like GuardMe, Bidvest, PPA — it's a white-label of the same AURA dispatch infrastructure, differentiated by the Copwatch regional brand.

## Core Offering
- Mobile panic button → AURA control room calls back, dispatches nearest armed-response vehicle from **multiple security companies** (not just Copwatch's own fleet).
- 24/7 nationwide dispatch.
- Audio confirmation, response-vehicle tracking.

## Pricing
Subscription-based, sales-led.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Multi-fleet aggregation (not limited to Copwatch vehicles).
- AURA backbone = proven dispatch.
- 3.7 ★ on Google Play (limited reviews).

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable.
- No elderly medical features.
- No B2B platform play.
- "No OTP / phishing" complaints in reviews signal onboarding fragility.
- Generic white-label — no real brand differentiation.

## Target Customer
Copwatch customers; Gauteng residents.
