# Rescue-Alert (Rescue Alert SA)

> **Category:** Lone-worker / corporate panic button specialist
> **Website:** https://rescue-alert.co.za/panic-buttons/

## Overview
Rescue Alert positions itself as a panic-button and lone-worker specialist serving SA individuals and corporates. Their content marketing heavily emphasises lone-worker protection, anti-kidnapping, corporate travel, employee safety — i.e. **B2B / corporate** rather than consumer panic.

## Core Offering
- Lone-worker live tracking + SOS alarms.
- Anti-kidnapping device line.
- Employee panic buttons for staff.
- Corporate travel security devices.
- Both hardware devices and software platform.

## Pricing
Sales-led (corporate quotes).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Strong lone-worker / corporate narrative** — plays to OHS Act compliance (similar to IMS, UTrack, Eworks).
- Anti-kidnapping positioning is a niche few address.
- Bundles hardware + software + service.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- No prominent elderly / frail-care wearable line.
- No medical-grade fall detection positioning.
- Less brand visibility than MyLifeLine.
- No documented API / WebHooks (MyLifeLine has these).
- Less retail / consumer presence.

## Target Customer
Corporate employers, security companies, executives at risk, field workers, anti-kidnap deployments.
