# Discovery 911 (Virtual Panic Button)

> **Category:** Insurance-app-embedded panic button (Discovery Insure)
> **Website:** https://www.discovery.co.za/car-and-home-insurance/discovery-911-virtual-panic-button

## Overview
Discovery Insure's **virtual panic button** lives inside the Discovery Insure app — one of SA's most-used insurance super-apps. It dispatches to **AURA** (armed response) or **Netcare 911** (medical), letting existing Discovery Insure customers summon help with one tap on their phone. Pure software feature — no wearable hardware.

## Core Offering
- **One-tap panic** from the Discovery Insure app home screen.
- **Armed response** via AURA — closest responder dispatched.
- **Medical response** via Netcare 911 — SA's largest private EMS brand.
- **Nationwide coverage** through both partner networks.

## Pricing
Free to qualifying Discovery Insure policyholders (bundled into insurance product).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Discovery Insure distribution** — Vitality / Insure app is in millions of SA phones.
- Free for existing customers — pricing power is via insurance premium, not subscription.
- **Netcare 911 medical** brand is the gold standard in SA EMS.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable pendant, no elderly medical watch.
- Gated to Discovery Insure customers (massive funnel, but closed ecosystem).
- No fall detection / HR / medical-allergy operator workflow like MyLifeLine.
- No B2B platform play — Discovery is a consumer-facing insurance product.

## Target Customer
Existing Discovery Insure policyholders; Vitality-active families; Discovery's high-LSM customer base.
