# AidCall

> **Category:** Elderly-targeted medical alert smartwatches + pendant
> **Website:** https://aidcall.co.za

## Overview
AidCall is a direct medical-alert competitor to MyLifeLine, focused on elderly and chronically-ill users in SA. Their range includes the **AC-04 mobile pendant**, **AC-05 smartwatch** and **AC-06 smartwatch** — each with 24/7 AidCall response centre, two-way voice, GPS, fall detection, and silent SOS. Pitches the same elderly-vertical that MyLifeLine addresses.

## Core Offering
- **AC-06 Smartwatch** — modern design, real-time GPS, two-way voice, fall detection, IP67, health monitoring, 24/7 response.
- **AC-05 Smartwatch** — touchscreen, silent SOS (tap designated contact), fall detection with auto-SOS countdown.
- **AC-04 Mobile Pendant** — fall detection, charging dock, lanyard, 24/7 response centre.
- **30-second response time** target.
- Personal AidCall Management Plan — manage device remotely.

## Pricing
Not publicly itemised on website (contact sales). Pendant system typically once-off device + monthly subscription.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Same vertical, same form factor** — direct head-to-head on elderly wearable medical alert.
- Watch designs look more like consumer smartwatches than MyLifeLine's EV06 (potentially better end-user adoption).
- "Silent SOS via touchscreen tap" is a clever covert activation AidCall markets well.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **No B2B SaaS platform** for security companies / estates (MyLifeLine's killer differentiator).
- No public API.
- No HR/SpO₂ on the AC-06 — only "health monitoring" mentioned in marketing, not the explicit HR + SpO₂ + step counter MyLifeLine's EV06 has.
- No medical/allergy operator console described — the pendant seems to be a pure end-user device with response centre, not a platform with admin controls.
- Website is less professional than MyLifeLine's (small operator feel).

## Target Customer
Independent seniors, elderly with chronic conditions, active fall-risk users, families wanting a stylish elderly smartwatch.
