# Medi Response "Assist Me"

> **Category:** Medical alert pendant + smartwatch for elderly
> **Parent:** Medi Response (Johannesburg-based emergency medical services company, est. 2012)
> **Website:** https://mediresponse.co.za/assist-me-panic-button-subscription/

## Overview
Medi Response's **Assist Me** line is the closest direct SA competitor to MyLifeLine on the elderly medical-alert pendant axis. The hardware is a 4G panic button with fall detection, two-way voice, GPS, HR monitor, SpO₂ monitor, step counter and IP67 rating — paired with Medi Response's 24/7 operations control centre. The **Safety Watch** variant adds wrist-watch form factor with the same medical sensors.

## Core Offering
- **Assist Me Panic Button** (pendant):
  - 24/7/365 panic alert monitoring by Medi Response Operations Control Centre
  - Emergency SMS to family / next of kin
  - Two-way voice communication
  - GPS location
  - Fall detection
  - Low-battery SMS
  - IP65 splash-proof
- **Safety Watch** adds:
  - IP67 water resistance
  - Heart-rate monitoring
  - SpO₂ (oxygen saturation) monitoring
  - Step counter
- Smartphone NOT required — device is fully standalone.

## Pricing
- **R 2,999.99 ex VAT** (once-off device)
- **R 2,056.20 ex VAT** (yearly subscription)

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Direct feature parity** with MyLifeLine's EV06/EV07 — fall detection, HR, SpO₂, step counter, two-way voice, GPS, IP67.
- Backed by an actual EMS company with ambulances and paramedics (credible medical response).
- Family SMS alerts to next of kin built in.
- Medical/medical-aid claim pathway via Medi Response's own paramedic network.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **No B2B platform / API** — Medi Response is the operator; MyLifeLine licenses its platform to security companies and estates.
- No documented public API or WebHooks (MyLifeLine has these).
- Public reviews on Trustpilot/Google note slow onboarding response (one complaint about a week with no reply).
- Smaller brand presence than MyLifeLine.
- Pendant isn't sold as a retail SKU on a public Shopify-style store.

## Target Customer
Elderly individuals, chronic-condition patients, families of frail relatives, retirement villages (currently small in this vertical).
