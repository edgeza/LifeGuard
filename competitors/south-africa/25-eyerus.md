# Eyerus

> **Category:** Personal-safety aggregator app
> **Website:** https://eyerusapp.co.za

## Overview
Eyerus is an SA personal-safety app that aggregates 250+ response companies, 2,500+ vehicles, and 5,400+ responders across 9 provinces and 278 municipalities. Headquartered in Constantia Kloof, Gauteng.

## Core Offering
- **In-app panic button** with GPS dispatch to nearest armed / medical responder via aggregated network.
- **National coverage** across all 9 provinces.
- **Family / multiple user** features.

## Pricing
Not publicly listed; subscription-based.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Wide aggregator network (250+ response companies).
- National coverage.
- Simple consumer proposition.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable hardware.
- No fall detection / HR / medical-grade wearable.
- No B2B platform / API.
- No medical profile operator workflow.
- Smaller brand presence than the leading competitors.

## Target Customer
SA consumers wanting a one-app solution to call for help anywhere.
