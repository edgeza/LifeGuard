# TriggerSA

> **Category:** On-demand emergency-response + lifestyle app
> **Website:** https://triggersa.com (App Store: id1529220176)

## Overview
TriggerSA brands itself as "the only Emergency Response App that puts money back into your pocket" — combining on-demand panic response with roadside, medical, legal and shopping-cashback benefits. Targets the **value-conscious family / individual** segment.

## Core Offering
- **Trigger (panic) button** — autonomously dispatches closest armed-response.
- **On-demand services:** armed response, roadside assistance, medical assistance, lawyers (subscription-tied).
- **Family plans** with shared panic coverage (Single / Plus One / Plus Two / Plus Three / Plus Four).
- **Cashback / rewards** at partner retailers — embedded in the app.

## Pricing
- **Panic Monthly Single:** R34.99
- **Platinum Monthly Single:** R99.99 (full bundle with cashback)
- **Family tiers** scaling up to R379.99 for 5 members.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Lifestyle rewards integration** is unique — sweetens the subscription for budget-conscious users.
- Aggressive family pricing.
- 5.0 ★ on App Store (small sample).
- "Plus one / Plus two" tiering is a creative family-onboarding model.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable.
- No elderly medical positioning.
- No B2B platform.
- Cashback tie-ins may signal thinner core-safety differentiation.
- 5.0 ★ is from very few reviews — too early to claim quality.

## Target Customer
Value-conscious SA families; commuters; small-business owners wanting bundled safety + cashback.
