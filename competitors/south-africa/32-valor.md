# Valor (Voice-Activated Panic App)

> **Category:** Voice-activated panic super-app
> **Website:** https://valor.co.za

## Overview
Valor markets itself as "the world's first voice-activated panic app" — uses a custom voice command (instead of a button press) to silently trigger armed-response dispatch and bank-asset lockdown. Targets high-net-worth individuals, executives, and bank customers.

## Core Offering
- **Voice-activated custom command** → silent panic trigger.
- **Immediate armed security + rescue team dispatch** with GPS location.
- **Bank-alert integration** — locks down assets on panic trigger.
- Patented voice-recognition process.

## Pricing
Sold via brokers / banks (white-label / partnership-led).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Voice activation** — unique differentiator (handbag / pocket / duress scenarios).
- Bank-asset integration is novel.
- Targets HNW / executive market that MyLifeLine doesn't address.
- Marketing position is premium / exclusive.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable pendant.
- No elderly / fall-detection positioning.
- No B2B SaaS / API for security companies.
- Smaller market (executive segment) than MyLifeLine's mass-market consumer + B2B play.
- Voice recognition has known reliability issues in noisy environments.

## Target Customer
HNW individuals, executives, bank VIP customers, broker-distributed channels.
