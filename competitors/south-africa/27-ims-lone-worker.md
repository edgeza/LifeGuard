# IMS Lone Worker (iMobility SA)

> **Category:** Lone-worker personal safety device (B2B)
> **Website:** https://shop.imobility.co.za/productpage/259/IMS_LONE_WORKER

## Overview
iMobility's IMS Lone Worker is a dedicated lone-worker safety device — discrete, lightweight, with panic button, man-down detection, two-way voice, GPS, and long battery. Sold to employers with field / remote / high-risk workers. **Not consumer-facing** — purely B2B.

## Core Offering
- **Lone-worker device** with panic button, man-down detection, two-way voice, GPS.
- **24/7 monitoring** for employer.
- **From ~18p per unit per day** — equivalent to ~R55/month.

## Pricing
~R55/m/unit (18p/day); volume-discounted for employers.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Lone-worker vertical specialist** — fit for OHS Act compliance (employer duty of care).
- Man-down detection + timed check-ins + false-pin (duress) features.
- Pricing per-day is convenient for short-term / contractor deployments.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **B2B-only** — no consumer / elderly positioning.
- No fall detection calibrated for elderly fragility.
- No HR / SpO₂ for chronic-condition monitoring.
- Not a panic-button app — purely a hardware + SIM + monitoring service.
- Less brand recognition in the personal-safety consumer segment.

## Target Customer
Employers of field workers, security guards, maintenance crews, lone contractors — for OHS Act compliance.
