# Tracker CareGuard

> **Category:** Consumer panic app (built on AURA) + wearable hardware
> **Website:** https://www.tracker.co.za/for-me/personal-safety-careguard

## Overview
CareGuard is Tracker Connect's personal-safety panic app — leveraging Tracker's vehicle-recovery brand and 1,500+ responder network. Tracker also offers the **Sayf** family-tracking app and the main Tracker app. Built on the AURA dispatch platform, so it shares responder infrastructure with GuardMe, Bidvest Panic Alert, Copwatch and PPA.

## Core Offering
- **SOS panic button** → 24/7 Tracker call centre calls back, dispatches nearest armed or medical responder.
- **1,500+ armed and medical responders** nationally.
- **Family plan (R79/m)** with multi-user SOS; **Single (R49/m)**.
- Supports **Tracker wearable panic hardware** (pendant).

## Pricing
- CareGuard for Me: **from R49/month**
- CareGuard for MyFamily: **from R79/month**

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Massive vehicle-recovery installed base; insurance / fleet channel.
- Family pricing aggressive at R79 for whole household.
- Hardware pendant + app combo.
- AURA platform delivers strong response network.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- No fall detection / HR monitoring / SpO₂ on the pendant.
- No medical-profile operator workflow like MyLifeLine's.
- Tracker is vehicle-tracking-first, not elderly-care-first.
- Fair-use cap: 6 calls/rolling 12 months before extra fees (per Ts&Cs).
- AURA-platform dependency means they are mostly a thin UI on top of the same dispatch layer.

## Target Customer
Existing Tracker vehicle-tracking customers; families; fleet operators; Tracker insurance policyholders.
