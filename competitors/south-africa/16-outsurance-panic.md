# OUTsurance Panic Assistance

> **Category:** Insurance-app-embedded panic feature (OUTsurance)
> **Website:** https://www.outsurance.co.za/outsurance-digital/panic-assistance/

## Overview
OUTsurance's Panic Assistance is a feature inside the OUTsurance app — tap-and-hold panic button dispatches the closest medical or armed-response unit to your phone's GPS, 24/7. Bundled with OUTsurance insurance products.

## Core Offering
- **Tap-and-hold panic button** in the OUTsurance app.
- User selects **Medical** or **Armed Response** at activation.
- **OUTsurance call centre** calls to confirm; if no answer, help is sent automatically.
- **24/7 nationwide** dispatch via partner network.

## Pricing
Free to OUTsurance policyholders (insurance-product feature).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Big SA insurance brand with high app install base.
- Free bundled panic — no separate subscription.
- Simple UX (tap → choose medical/security → confirm).

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable.
- No fall detection, no HR monitor, no medical alert watch.
- Gated to OUTsurance customers.
- No B2B platform.
- No medical/allergy profile surfaced to responders.

## Target Customer
OUTsurance policyholders (car/home insurance customers); OUTsurance app users.
