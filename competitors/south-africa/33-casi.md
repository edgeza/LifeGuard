# Casi (Calling All Stations Immediately)

> **Category:** Consumer panic app aggregator
> **Website:** MyBroadband coverage; otherwise lean presence

## Overview
Casi is a Cape Town–originated personal-safety app that has expanded nationally. Aggregates 150+ armed-response companies into a single R35/month subscription. Notable for aggressive pricing and AA partnership stake.

## Core Offering
- One-tap panic button.
- 150+ armed-response partner companies.
- 3-5 minute claimed response time.
- AA partnership (AA holds a stake in Casi).

## Pricing
**R35/month** — among the cheapest in market.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Cheapest** subscription-tier pricing for full national panic coverage.
- Wide aggregator network (150+ companies).
- AA partnership = credibility + member-base distribution.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable.
- No fall detection / HR / elderly wearable.
- No B2B platform.
- No medical profile operator workflow.

## Target Customer
Price-sensitive SA consumers; AA members; Cape Town / urban residents.
