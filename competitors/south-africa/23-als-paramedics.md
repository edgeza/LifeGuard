# ALS Paramedics Panic Button

> **Category:** Medical-only standalone panic button (ALS Paramedics)
> **Website:** https://alsparamedics.co.za/panic-button/

## Overview
ALS Paramedics (an SA private ambulance service) sells a standalone wearable panic button for medical emergencies — the device auto-alerts ALS Paramedics' control room on fall or SOS, dispatches an ambulance to GPS. Pendants are pre-configured with online registration and include charging dock, lanyard, car sticker, wallet card.

## Core Offering
- **Standalone 4G pendant** — no phone required, pre-installed SIM.
- **Fall detection** with auto-alert.
- **GPS tracking** — emergency contacts receive GPS location instantly.
- **Voice calls** — emergency contacts can call the device.
- **35 g**, 61 × 44 × 16 mm — small and light.

## Pricing
Contact ALS Paramedics (sales-led).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Medical-only positioning** — clear value proposition for elderly/chronic patients.
- ALS Paramedics operates its own ambulances, so dispatch is to a trusted paramedic fleet.
- Cheap pendant hardware (similar to MyLifeLine's EV04/07 form factor).
- Pre-configured + ready to use out of the box.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Medical-only** — no armed-response dispatch (the biggest gap for SA's security-conscious market).
- No smartwatch variant.
- No documented HR / SpO₂ / step counter.
- No B2B platform.
- Limited online presence / smaller brand.

## Target Customer
Elderly with chronic medical needs, families wanting medical-only safety pendant, ALS Paramedics existing customers.
