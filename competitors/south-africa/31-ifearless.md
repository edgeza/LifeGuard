# iFearLESS

> **Category:** Women-focused personal safety app
> **Website:** https://ifearless.co.za

## Overview
iFearLESS is a personal-safety app specifically tailored for SA women — single tap alerts go to trusted contacts AND local armed-response authorities. Live location sharing + panic button + medical / fire / road-accident dispatch.

## Core Offering
- **Panic button** → armed-response + trusted contacts with one tap.
- **Live location sharing** with family / friends.
- **Multi-emergency type** dispatch (medical, fire, road, security).
- Real-time GPS tracking.

## Pricing
Sales-led.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Niche positioning for women — strong marketing angle in SA.
- Live location sharing is well-implemented.
- Combined emergency types (medical, fire, security, road).

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable.
- No fall detection / elderly medical.
- No B2B platform.
- Smaller brand / marketing-reach than MyLifeLine.

## Target Customer
SA women, students, solo travellers, GBV-vulnerable populations.
