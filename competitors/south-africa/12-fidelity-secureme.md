# Fidelity SecureMe (Fidelity ADT)

> **Category:** Hardware panic pendant + home security bundle
> **Parent:** Fidelity Services Group (owns Fidelity ADT)
> **Website:** https://fidelity-services.com/fidelity-secureme/

## Overview
SecureMe is a portable GNSS panic pendant from Fidelity ADT, the largest security-services group in southern Africa. The pendant pairs with the FidelitySecure mobile app and connects into Fidelity's armed-response network. Targets existing Fidelity ADT alarm customers wanting a personal-panic add-on.

## Core Offering
- **SecureMe pendant** — portable GNSS panic button, motion detection, Sigfox network connectivity, USB-C rechargeable, IP-rated.
- **FidelitySecure app** — home/business alarm monitoring + home panic button (operates only at the registered address).
- Direct dispatch into Fidelity ADT armed-response network.

## Pricing
- **R139** promotional price (36-month contract, VAT inclusive). 30-day waiting period may apply.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Fidelity ADT brand** — SA's largest armed-response footprint; consumers trust the name.
- Existing alarm-customer base of millions for cross-sell.
- Sigfox connectivity = long battery life vs GSM.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Home-address panic only** in the app — the mobile pendant is the only out-of-home option.
- Pendant has no fall detection, no HR, no SpO₂, no two-way voice (Sigfox is one-way).
- App is gated to the home alarm contract — not a mobile anywhere-service.
- Older Sigfox network has coverage gaps in SA; MyLifeLine uses 2G/3G/4G cellular.
- 30-day waiting period is a friction point.

## Target Customer
Existing Fidelity ADT alarm subscribers; homeowners wanting a pendant add-on; families of Fidelity ADT customers.
