# ShieldForce

> **Category:** White-label panic / safety app + micro-location
> **Website:** https://www.shieldforce.co.za

## Overview
ShieldForce positions itself as the "world's best white-label panic & safety app", pitched at **campuses, corporations, communities, governments**. Endorsed by the SA Department of Women, Children and People with Disabilities for GBV response. Sells micro-location tracking to 10 metres — finer than GPS.

## Core Offering
- **Panic button** with **micro-location to 10 m** and live tracking on activation.
- **Push notifications, surveys, video guides, newsfeeds, welcome messages, special offers** — modular engagement layer.
- **EMS Locator, Geofencing, WhatsApp integration.**
- **White-label** for campuses, corporates, communities.
- Wearables supported (partners).

## Pricing
Sales-led (white-label SaaS pricing).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Micro-location (10 m)** is a notable accuracy claim over typical GPS (±5-15 m).
- **White-label + SaaS** model is the closest competitor to MyLifeLine's B2B platform play.
- Government endorsement (Department of Women) is a trust signal in the GBV/campus segment.
- WhatsApp integration layer for low-data users.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Micro-location is phone-dependent** (likely BLE/UWB hybrid) — not the wearable-pendent pedigree MyLifeLine has.
- No proprietary medical-grade wearable line.
- No fall detection, no HR, no two-way voice on a dedicated device.
- Focus is community/campus GBV — not the elderly vertical.
- Less established than MyLifeLine's 2015 heritage and Santam award.

## Target Customer
Universities, corporate campuses, communities, NGOs, government GBV programmes, shopping centres.
