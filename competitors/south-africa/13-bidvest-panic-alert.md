# Bidvest Panic Alert

> **Category:** Insurance-product panic app (Bidvest Insurance)
> **Website:** https://bidvestinsurance.co.za

## Overview
Panic Alert is Bidvest Insurance's panic-button product — combines a mobile app with armed-response, courtesy transport after an incident, and emergency medical assistance. Built on the AURA dispatch platform (same as GuardMe/Copwatch/Tracker). Bidvest also runs **Bidvest Protea Coin** (separate, older app) for armed-response dispatch.

## Core Offering
- **Panic Alert mobile app** — one-tap SOS, control-room call-back, armed-response dispatch.
- **24-hour armed response** with 2,000+ agents nationally.
- **Emergency courtesy transport** — if you suffer a "trigger incident" (mugging/assault), Bidvest organises a private car to get you home or to a safe location.
- **Emergency medical assistance** coverage.
- **Device cover** for items damaged in a trigger incident (phones, watches, earphones, tablets).
- **Bidvest Protea Coin app** (separate) — armed response + medical + geo-fence + group features.

## Pricing
Sold as an insurance product — monthly premium (price varies; sales-led via Bidvest Insurance).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Insurance bundling** — coverage for device damage and transport-after-incident is unique; competitors offer pure dispatch.
- Bidvest brand and existing customer base.
- AURA platform = wide response network.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable pendant or medical-grade elderly device.
- No fall detection, no HR, no medical profile operator workflow.
- No B2B platform play.
- Dependent on Bidvest Insurance policy (gated) — not a standalone app.

## Target Customer
Existing Bidvest Insurance policyholders; mass-market SA consumers wanting insurance + safety in one product.
