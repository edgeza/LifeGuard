## Differentiation

Alertus is the hardware-first, UL-listed mass-notification vendor — the only MNS provider that publishes an explicit "four Critical Alerting Layers" architecture (facility, network, outdoor, personal/mobile). It wins K-12 and university RFPs where NFPA 72 Chapter 24 compliance, UL 2572 listing, and physical-speaker deployments matter more than software polish.

## Strengths

- ✅ UL 2572 / UL 2524 / ULC-S576 listed appliances — required for many state-level fire-code-compliant MNS RFPs.
- ✅ Only MNS vendor with a published "four-layer" architecture (facility/network/outdoor/personal) — narrative sells to procurement.
- ✅ Deep K-12 + higher-ed customer references (Auburn, Clemson, FSU, Ohio State, U Maryland, WashU) — campus-safety RFP credibility.
- ✅ Hardware breadth: beacons, sirens, speakers, panic buttons, fire-alarm-interface — true one-stop shop.
- ✅ AlertAware mobile app ties hardware to personal devices (SMS / push / voice / panic-button).
- ✅ AlertBeacon wall-mounted activation pulls are NFPA-72 Chapter 24 compliant for direct first-responder signalling.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No risk-intelligence layer (no Dataminr-style threat feed, no travel-risk management).
- ❌ Limited IPAWS / WEA federal layer (vs. Everbridge's public-warning franchise).
- ❌ Hardware-heavy roadmap creates long deployment cycles (weeks) vs. SaaS-only CEM platforms (days).
- ❌ No AI-powered analytics yet (vs. AlertMedia Analytics 2026 launch).
- ❌ Smaller brand than Everbridge / AlertMedia — limited inbound demand-gen outside K-12/higher-ed verticals.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Hardware-grade panic-button + mobile-app bridge is missing.** Alertus has wall-mounted panic buttons and a separate mobile app (AlertAware); nobody in the consumer market pairs a personal-fall/safety pendant with a multi-modal alert experience that also reaches a caregiver app.
- **K-12-style school-emergency alerting is the proof point.** MyLifeLine can borrow the "everyone gets the alert, everyone acknowledges" UX pattern from AlertAware and adapt it for at-home family safety.
- **UL-listed hardware is a credible B2B partnership lever.** Pairing MyLifeLine's consumer brand with Alertus's hardware for senior-living / assisted-living facilities is a distribution partnership, not a competitive threat.
