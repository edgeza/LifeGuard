## Differentiation

Konexus (formerly AlertSense) is the **civic-government IPAWS pioneer** — the first private-sector provider to demonstrate successful IPAWS-OPEN alert creation. Owned by CivicPlus since 2022, it bundles mass notification with the broader civic-government software suite (agenda management, civic engagement, public records) that CivicPlus sells to 8,000+ local-government customers.

## Strengths

- ✅ First successful IPAWS-OPEN demonstration by a private-sector provider — category-defining federal public-warning credibility.
- ✅ FEMA-approved IPAWS COG (Common Operating Group) authority.
- ✅ CivicPlus parent — distribution into 8,000+ local-government customers.
- ✅ Threat Intelligence Suite (added 2023–2024).
- ✅ Multi-channel (SMS / email / voice / push / mobile / IPAWS) at mid-market pricing.
- ✅ Lower price point than Everbridge for smaller cities/counties ⚠️.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Smaller brand than Everbridge / AlertMedia.
- ❌ No first-party panic-button hardware.
- ❌ No AI-powered analytics like AlertMedia Analytics 2026.
- ❌ North America / US-focused; limited international footprint.
- ❌ Limited public customer-list disclosures.
