## Differentiation

LiveProcess is the **only healthcare-pure-play emergency management platform** — purpose-built around Hospital Incident Command System (HICS), HAvBED bed-management, healthcare-coalition coordination, and Joint Commission compliance. Its ~50,000 active users make it the de facto standard for US hospital emergency operations centres. Nobody else combines "HICS templates + mass notification + resource-request forms + bed management" for healthcare only.

## Strengths

- ✅ Healthcare-only focus — HICS-aligned, Joint Commission-friendly, HAvBED bed-management built in.
- ✅ ~50,000 active users; 600+ healthcare organisations — depth of healthcare-coalition trust.
- ✅ Multilingual notification translation built in (unique among healthcare MNS platforms).
- ✅ Healthcare-coalition (HCC) coordination tools — inter-hospital resource requests during COVID demonstrated scale.
- ✅ Aware (2025) — purpose-built for situations-of-any-scale, modern UX refresh.
- ✅ Lightweight Communicator tier for organisations not ready for the full Emergency Manager platform.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Healthcare-only — no federal, manufacturing, K-12, retail, Fortune 500 verticals.
- ❌ No risk-intelligence layer (no Dataminr-style threat feed).
- ❌ No panic-button hardware.
- ❌ LinkedIn listing implies ~$4.4M revenue and 1 employee — scale concern ⚠️; small company relative to Everbridge / AlertMedia.
- ❌ Limited brand awareness outside healthcare emergency-management circles.

## MyLifeLine opportunity
