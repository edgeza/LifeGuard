# SenTrox (Nigeria)

> **Category:** Personal safety app with private dispatch + "remote-kill" anti-theft
> **Headquarters:** Lagos, Nigeria
> **Website:** https://sentrox.app

## Overview
SenTrox is a Nigerian-built ("Made in Nigeria · For everyone") personal safety app whose standout differentiator is **anti-phone-snatch protection**: if a thief takes the phone, the app can brick its own session, wipe stored secrets, send a final GPS breadcrumb, and lock every signed-in device — all before the SIM is out. It also bundles NDPR-compliant (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) one-touch SOS with trained-responder dispatch, family-circle and agency-dispatch escalation, and an upcoming 0800-SENTROX hotline.

## Core Offering
- **One-touch SOS hold-button** that pushes live location to family / neighbours / trained responders in seconds.
- **Anti-snatch remote-kill** with recovery PIN (SHA-256 hash, never sent in plain text) — auto-wipe on wrong-PIN streak, SIM-swap, "mark this device stolen" from any other device.
- **Voice in 40 seconds** — in-app SOS dispatches 24/7 to a human operator.
- NDPR / GDPR compliant, AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in flight, "your story never sold."
- **48-hour free premium** trial at signup.

## Pricing
Free tier (consumer) + paid Premium tier. Toll-free 0800 hotline launching.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Designed specifically for Nigeria's **phone-snatch reality** — a different threat model than ZA.
- NDPR compliance from day one (vs MyLifeLine's POPIA only).
- Anti-snatch kill switch is genuinely unique in the African market.
- Targets the high-risk 18–35 commuter / late-night worker.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- App-only — no wearable pendant.
- No medical sensors (fall / HR / SpO₂).
- Nigeria-only — no cross-African presence yet.
- Still in early access, smaller brand than SafeNaija / NPF Rescue Me.

## Target Customer
Nigerian commuters, women, students, professionals, expats, anyone in phone-snatch prone cities (Lagos / Abuja).
