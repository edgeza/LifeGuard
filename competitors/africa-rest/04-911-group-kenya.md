# 911 Group Kenya (911 Security App)

> **Category:** Private-security + roadside + medical response (mobile app + 24/7 control room)
> **Headquarters:** Nairobi, Kenya
> **Website:** https://www.911group.co.ke

## Overview
911 Group is a 20+ year-old integrated East-African security provider (Car Track vehicle tracking, 911 Electronic Security, 911 Emergency Response, 911 Guarding, 911 Road Rescue). Its **911 Security Mobile App** turns a smartphone into a personal panic alarm directly tied to its own 24/7 dispatch centre — not an AURA white-label, but a self-operated response. It is one of the Kenyan market's longest-standing consumer panic apps.

## Core Offering
- **One-tap emergency dispatch** for security / medical / fire / roadside.
- **Real-time GPS** sent to 911 control room with caller identity.
- **24/7 operators** coordinating own armed-response fleet and partner ambulance / fire.
- **Roadside rescue membership** bundled with the app.

## Pricing
Subscription model tied to 911 membership plans; bundled with roadside / vehicle-tracking services.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Operates its **own dispatch infrastructure** in Kenya — boots-on-the-ground response, not just an app.
- Cross-sell into its existing vehicle-tracking, guarding and roadside customer base.
- Established brand in Nairobi since the early 2000s.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **App-only** — no standalone mPERS pendant, no medical alert device.
- No fall detection, no HR/SpO₂, no SpO₂.
- Kenyan-only coverage — no other African countries.
- No medical/allergy profile in operator console.

## Target Customer
Existing 911 Group security / tracking / roadside customers; Nairobi-based families and commuters.
