# SOS Tn / SafeNess / Istijaba — Tunisia

> **Category:** Three national emergency / GBV apps in Tunisia
> **Headquarters:** Tunisia (SafeNess served in Tunisia + Morocco via CAWTAR; SOS Tn App Store by Softwar.io; Istijaba by Gabès Civil Protection)
> **Websites:** https://softwar.io/app/sos-tn · https://play.google.com (SafeNess: app.safeness.safeness) · https://techpression.com (Istijaba)

## Overview
Tunisia has no single dominant app — three complementary Tunisian applications span different niches:
- **SOS Tn** (Softwar.io) — bilingual French / Arabic emergency-services directory: one tap to call SAMU, civil protection, emergency police, towing; nearest hospital with GPS coordinates; SMS-with-GPS message to chosen contact in distress.
- **SafeNess** — sexual-harassment / public-violence prevention app, free, offered by CAWTAR (Center of Arab Women for Training and Research) to 36+ regions in Tunisia and Morocco. Voice recording with timestamp + GPS as potential evidence.
- **Istijaba** — internally-developed app by the Regional Directorate of Civil Protection in Gabès for **internal disaster-management coordination** (live intervention reports, vehicle / staff inventory, geo-mapped incidents), being rolled out nationally and possibly integrated with the national ES-Alert mobile public-notification system.

## Core Offering
- **SOS Tn** — one-tap emergency-call router + GPS hospital locator + SMS-with-GPS distress.
- **SafeNess** — location share with trusted contacts, voice recording as evidence, route-to-victim; Tunisia + Morocco.
- **Istijaba** — internal command-and-control for the Civil Protection Directorate.

## Pricing
SOS Tn free; SafeNess free; Istijaba internal government deployment.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Deep local-government entrenchment (Istijaba in Civil Protection chain of command).
- Free / donor-backed (SafeNess via UNECA / CAWTAR).
- Cultural fit (bilingual Arabic / French, GBV-specific).
- Effectively the default consumer emergency app in Tunisia.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- Each one is **single-niche** (SOS only, GBV only, or government internal coordination only).
- No wearable, no medical sensors, no API.
- Limited to Tunisia (one of SafeNess's regions only; rest is Morocco).
- No elderly fall-detection product.

## Target Customer
Tunisian residents (SOS Tn), women / GBV survivors (SafeNess), Civil Protection Directorate of Gabès (Istijaba).
