# Kiipo — Emergency Dispatch Africa (Ethiopia)

> **Category:** Cloud-based national EMS dispatch (B2G / B2B; consumer ambulance app)
> **Headquarters:** Addis Ababa, Ethiopia + Canada
> **Website:** https://www.kiipo.com/ethiopia

## Overview
Kiipo built and has operated **Ethiopia's first full-circle Emergency Medical Services (EMS) dispatch system** at Nordic Medical Centre (NMC) since 2015 — pre-dating any ZA-style personal panic app in the country. The cloud-based dispatch was specifically engineered for Ethiopian infrastructure (intermittent power, cellular outages, no street names, no 911). With a stated ambition to scale national EMS across 107 million Ethiopians and replicate in other developing markets.

## Core Offering
- **One-tap ambulance app** — exact location streamed to dispatcher in background, no need to describe where you are.
- **Cloud-based dispatch console** — auto-dispatch nearest unit, exact GPS to paramedics on in-vehicle tablets.
- **Resilient by design** — fallback for no power and no data; portable command centre that ran from a van on a dirt road with a single cellphone.
- **Integration with hospital patient-management systems** so the dispatcher and paramedic both have caller medical context.

## Pricing
Consumer subscription model (lite + family + corporate tiers) for ambulance membership; B2B pricing for hospitals and county governments.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **National-scale EMS in Ethiopia** — the only legitimate full-circle emergency medical dispatch in the country, operated since 2015.
- Cloud architecture specifically engineered for unreliable power/data — strong engineering moat.
- Pipeline to expand to other Sub-Saharan markets — pan-African ambitions.
- B2G relationships (NMC, county governments) for public-sector scale.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Ambulance-only** — no armed response, no personal security dispatch.
- No wearable, no fall detection, no medical sensors.
- Ethiopia's regulatory environment is restrictive and currency is non-convertible — constrains expansion.
- No publicly visible partner-platform / API.

## Target Customer
Addis Ababa hospitals (initially NMC), Ethiopian county governments, corporate employers, regional health authorities.
