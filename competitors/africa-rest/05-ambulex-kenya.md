# Ambulex Solutions

> **Category:** Ambulance-subscription + GBV emergency-response app
> **Headquarters:** Nairobi, Kenya
> **Website:** https://ambulexsolutions.org

## Overview
Born out of a personal-tragedy story, Ambulex is Kenya's first **subscription-based ambulance + GBV emergency-response service** at the household level. Subscribers pay as little as **Ksh 200/year** (≈ $1.50) for an annual membership that gives them priority dispatch to the nearest ambulance unit, with a 15-minute response target. Non-subscribers pay market rates (often Sh100k+).

## Core Offering
- **Mobile app + USSD code** for one-tap ambulance request from any phone, smart or feature.
- **Digital dispatch** that auto-assigns the nearest available unit and shares live location.
- **GBV integration** — separate journey to connect users to police, safe houses, social workers.
- Tiered plans: Lite, Plus, Prime, Total, Golden Shield (seniors), CareRide, Corporate.
- **M-Pesa integration** for micro-subscriptions (Paybill 4079331).

## Pricing
- **Ksh 200/year** household subscription (Lite tier) — radically accessible.
- Senior and family plans tiered upward; corporate plans for employers.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Extremely low price point opens the mass market (Sh200/yr vs MyLifeLine's R180/m device fee) — different tier entirely.
- USSD works on feature phones, not just smartphones.
- Genuine GBV value-add (safe house, counsellor network).
- Strong narrative / press coverage since launch.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Medical-only** — no security / armed-response dispatch, no personal safety app function.
- No medical alert wearable / pendant for elderly — purely ambulance dispatch.
- No fall detection, no GPS-on-a-lonely-pensioner scenario.
- Kenya-only today.

## Target Customer
Bottom-of-pyramid Kenyan households, GBV survivors, employers (corporate plan), senior citizens.
