# Pre-Secure (Zambia)

> **Category:** Established security company + consumer Panic App
> **Headquarters:** Lusaka, Zambia
> **Website:** https://www.presecure.co.zm

## Overview
Pre-Secure is one of Zambia's longest-running private security companies (founded 1972, 53+ years). Its **Panic App** extends the manned-guarding and alarm-receiving-centre business to a smartphone panic button — at the press of a button Pre-Secure rapid response dispatches to the user's home.

## Core Offering
- **Panic App** — one-tap panic from any mobile, dispatch to the Pre-Secure team (Lusaka + national rollout).
- **53-year-old manned-guarding operation** providing the response capacity.
- **Alarm receiving centre** (ARC) for back-office coordination.
- Custom installations beyond the app.

## Pricing
**ZMK 7,200 / 12-month subscription** to the Panic App. Once-off ≈ K 7,200.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Owns its own response fleet** in Zambia — boots-on-the-ground, not a third-party dispatch.
- Long operating history gives trust / credibility.
- "Global" press of the panic button — works from any country, dispatch to the home in Zambia.
- B2B / estate / corporate extension potential.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Zambia-only**; ZMK 7,200/yr is subscription-locked.
- App-only — no medical wearable.
- No fall detection, no HR / SpO₂.
- No API / Webhooks.

## Target Customer
Zambian homeowners, expatriates with property in Lusaka, corporate clients.
