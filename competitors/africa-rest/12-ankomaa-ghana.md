# Ankomaa Life Alert (Ghana)

> **Category:** PERS / medical-alert pendant (closest direct MyLifeLine analogue in Ghana)
> **Operator:** Ankomaa Healthcare Services (Tema + Accra)
> **Website:** https://ankomaahealthcareservice.com/ankomaa-life-alert/

## Overview
Ankomaa Life Alert is Ghana's clearest **direct analogue to MyLifeLine** — a small, lightweight medical-alert pendant that works indoors and outdoors, even in the shower, with one-button 24/7 emergency-operator response. Ankomaa Healthcare Services is a private home-health company operating in Ghana and the US.

## Core Offering
- **Lightweight, wearable pendant** — small, designed for seniors / vulnerable loved-ones.
- **Shower / outdoor rated** — works anywhere with cellular coverage, including the bathroom.
- **24/7 trained emergency operators** answering the press.
- **Home-care back-office** — Ankomaa runs caregiving registries in both countries, so the alert can trigger an in-home response or family dispatch.

## Pricing
Device + monthly monitoring fee (contact Ankomaa).

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- Closest **feature-for-feature Ghanaian competitor** to MyLifeLine's pendant proposition.
- Home-health vertical gives a cross-sell into caregiving (where MyLifeLine has no current offering in Africa).
- US/Ghana dual presence may be useful for diaspora-funded subscriptions.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **Pendant-only** — no companion watch, no fall detection / HR / SpO₂ published.
- Ghana-only — no other African countries.
- Single-vertical brand (medical alert) — no B2B partner platform.
- No documented API / Webhooks.
- Smaller brand, less marketing muscle than AURA or SnooCODE RED.

## Target Customer
Ghanaian elderly, family caregivers paying for loved ones, US diaspora, retirement-home operators in Tema / Accra.
