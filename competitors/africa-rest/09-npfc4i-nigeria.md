# NPF Rescue Me! / N-Alert (NISPSAS)

> **Category:** Government-coordinated state police + civil-defence emergency platform
> **Operator:** Nigeria Police Force + Federal Ministry of Interior (NISPSAS)
> **Website:** https://www.npf4i.com · https://nispsas.com.ng · https://www.nalerts.com.ng

## Overview
Two related federal platforms bundled together. **"Rescue Me!"** is the Nigeria Police Force's consumer panic app — by tapping an emergency type, you are routed into the NPF's national command and control centre where trained operators track your location and dispatch police, ambulance, fire. **NISPSAS / N-Alert** is the broader Nigerian Internal Security and Public Safety Alert System run by the Federal Ministry of Interior, integrating NPF, NSCDC, Federal Fire, Immigration, Correctional, with stealth "Know Your Neighbour" distress trigger, IoT-CCTV/electric-fence integration and PCASS early-warning broadcast.

## Core Offering
- **8 emergency categories** (homicide, medical, rape, assault, robbery, kidnapping, domestic, burglary) routed to NPF dispatch.
- **Report-a-crime** with photo / voice / video evidence; complaints against officers via PCRRU (Public Complaints Rapid Response Unit).
- **NISPSAS / N-Alert:** mobile app + Response Coordination Platform + IoT/PCASS for statewide deployments; KYN stealth trigger for GBV; CCTV / fence / SOS-device integration; election / Safe-School / disaster modules.
- NDPR-compliant.

## Pricing
Free.

## Strengths vs MyLifeLine
- **Backed by the federal government** — direct line into police control rooms, with public-sector credibility.
- Multi-hazard (kidnapping, insurgency, school / farm attacks, fire, flood, election security) — far beyond personal panic.
- IoT integration: CCTV alarms, fence alarms, panic devices.
- Free.

## Weaknesses vs MyLifeLine
- **State-only response** — no private-responder optimisation, no SLAs.
- App reliability historically inconsistent (999/112 in Nigeria widely panned).
- No wearable / personal device integration.
- No medical/allergy profile in operator console.

## Target Customer
General Nigerian public, schools / Safe-School programme, election observers, NSCDC / fire / immigration agencies.
