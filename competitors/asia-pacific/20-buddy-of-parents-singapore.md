## Differentiation

BOP is the only Singapore D2C PERS specifically built around a single-button hardware + flat SGD $20/month subscription + BOP App family-visibility model. Selling point: 3-year battery life, 2-year warranty, multi-home / multi-user / family-sharing caregiver app — built for adult-child buyers monitoring parents.

## Strengths

- ✅ Singapore-based 24/7 Response Centre (not foreign-routed).
- ✅ BOP App with multi-home / multi-user / family sharing.
- ✅ 3-year battery life — longest in SG PERS segment.
- ✅ Flat SGD $20/month — no hidden tiers.
- ✅ Direct 995 (SCDF) / 999 (police) escalation.
- ✅ Built for adult-child buyers monitoring multiple parents.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Singapore-only — no ASEAN expansion.
- ❌ No fall detection — disqualifies fall-risk seniors.
- ❌ 2-year contract minimum — long Minimum Period of Service.
- ❌ No GPS pendant SKU.
- ❌ Telco-network dependent — relies on telco coverage.
- ❌ Limited brand awareness outside SG government-affiliated channel.
