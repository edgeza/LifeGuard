## Product lineup

- **HoME+ 2.0 kit** — 4 motion sensors (kitchen, bathroom, living room, bedroom), 1 door sensor (main door), 1 proximity keyfob, 1 portable panic button, 1 wall-mounted panic button, 1 intercom + gateway (wifi).
- **Caregiver mobile app** — real-time updates for caregivers (activity, alerts).
- **Community-Responder escalation** — 160 trained first-aid responders dispatched when needed.
- **24/7 helpline** — basic first-aid and psychological first aid.
- **No cameras / no fall detection** — privacy-by-design.

## Differentiation

HoME+ is the only Singapore PERS that explicitly excludes cameras and fall detection in favour of motion-only privacy-by-design monitoring. Selling point: 160-strong trained Community Responder network, Singapore Red Cross trust brand, and integration with the national Age Well SG programme scaling to ~26,800 more seniors in public rental housing.

## Strengths

- ✅ Non-profit (Singapore Red Cross) — strongest trust brand for senior-care in SG.
- ✅ 160 trained Community Responders — first-aid-trained escalation (vs call-centre-only rivals).
- ✅ Privacy-by-design (no cameras, no fall detection) — addresses the cultural objection to surveillance.
- ✅ Multilingual (4 official SG languages).
- ✅ Age Well SG / HDB AAS government integration — direct public-sector channel.
- ✅ Caregiver mobile app included.
- ✅ 24/7 helpline with first-aid and psychological first aid.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No fall detection — disqualifies the product for fall-risk seniors (the largest PERS segment).
- ❌ No mobile GPS pendant — in-home only.
- ❌ Subsidised paths are means-tested — middle-class seniors fall outside HDB AAS coverage.
- ❌ Pricing opaque on private-pay path.
- ❌ Limited coverage of 62 surrounding islands (similar to CareBell exclusion).
- ❌ Reaches only ~1,200 stay-alone seniors as of latest public data — small scale vs population need.
