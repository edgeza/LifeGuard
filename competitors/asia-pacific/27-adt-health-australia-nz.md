## Differentiation

ADT Health is the medical-alert sub-brand of NYSE-listed ADT Inc., leveraging the parent company's 6m+ home-security customer base for cross-sell. Selling point: institutional credibility, no-long-term-contract, cross-sell from existing home-security install.

## Strengths

- ✅ NYSE-listed (ADT) institutional credibility.
- ✅ 6m+ home-security customer base — built-in cross-sell funnel.
- ✅ No long-term contract required.
- ✅ Cross-region ANZ coverage (ADT ANZ = Chubb partnership).
- ✅ Multiple form factors (in-home basic / plus / on-the-go GPS).
- ✅ Annual-plan lockbox + free-shipping promotion.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No first-party caregiver app.
- ❌ Falls detection is paid add-on (vs CareAlert / LiveLife built-in).
- ❌ Foreign-owned — Australian / NZ consumers can counter with MePACS / St John / VitalCALL local-owner narrative.
- ❌ Limited HCP / DVA / NDIS pathway integration visible (vs MePACS, RDNS).
- ❌ Australian PERS brand awareness weaker than ADT-home-security brand.
