## Product lineup

- **SECOM Home Security (Parent-Watch Plan / 親の見守りプラン)** — JPY 5,100/month (tax incl. 5,510円) for the Smart NEO rental pack; 1× My Doctor pendant + 1× fire sensor + door/window + 2× space sensors; alarm + activity-anomaly + 24/7 nurse consultation + 24/7 emergency dispatch.
- **SECOM My Doctor Plus** — medical-emergency pendant with cellular phone + GPS locator; alerts SECOM control centre; trained emergency-response personnel dispatched; nurse consultation via SECOM Medical Support Center; partner adult-day-service integration.
- **SECOM Mimamori Phone 2 (みまもりホン2)** — mobile phone form factor; pull the strap = emergency call; guardman dispatch on request (paid).
- **COCO-SECOM (ココセコム)** — GPS tracker (carried in pocket/bag); press SOS = emergency call + guardman dispatch (paid).
- **Mago Channel with SECOM (まごチャンネル with SECOM)** — smart-TV-based well-being monitoring (no dispatch).
- **SECOM My Doctor Watch** — wristwatch panic button.
- **YORiSOS App (Apple Watch integration)** — additional monthly contract for wearable emergency reporting.

## Differentiation

SECOM invented Japan's home-security industry in 1981 and the Japanese panic-button PERS in 1986. Selling point: 40+ year track record, fully-owned control-centre + guardman-dispatch, SECOM Medical Support Center nurse consultation, and the broadest product portfolio in Japan (My Doctor, Mimamori Phone, COCO-SECOM, Mago Channel).

## Strengths

- ✅ Industry founder — 40-year brand trust in Japan home-security.
- ✅ Owned control centre + emergency-response personnel nationwide (faster than 119 dispatch).
- ✅ SECOM Medical Support Center — 24/7 nurse consultation included in Parent-Watch Plan.
- ✅ Multiple product form factors (pendant, phone, watch, GPS tracker, smart TV).
- ✅ Apple Watch (YORiSOS) integration — modern wearable path.
- ✅ 5-year cumulative R&D advantage in elderly-pendant ergonomics.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ 5-year contract typical — high switching cost / friction.
- ❌ JPY 5,100/mo (≈US$34) — significantly more expensive than MyLifeLine EV07BA wholesale.
- ❌ No fall detection in core pendant (activity-rhythm proxy only).
- ❌ Caregiver app is shallow (in/out detection only) vs modern dashboards.
- ❌ Foreign-language support limited.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Disrupt on contract + price.** SECOM's 5-year + JPY 5,100/mo is the obvious friction for adult-child buyers; MyLifeLine's no-contract wholesale model is the opposite.
- **Mobile pendant SKU.** SECOM's Pendant is home-only; Mimamori Phone 2 is bulkier; MyLifeLine EV07BA is the modern slim pendant.
- **Caregiver app depth.** "いつでもみまもり" is in/out detection; MyLifeLine's caregiver dashboard with location + alert log + vitals trends is the upgrade.
- **B2B apartment-developer partnerships.** SECOM has the established tie-ups; MyLifeLine could be the "second pendant" for renters / younger families in the same buildings.
- **Cross-border:** SECOM has Thailand operations; MyLifeLine's no-monthly-fee model could undercut SECOM's Thai pricing.
