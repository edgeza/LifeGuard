## Differentiation

Ready Digital is the only Singapore-headquartered radar-based ceiling fall-detection provider; it joins a small APAC contactless-fall-detection cohort (Tunstall's UK play, Vayyar Care's B2B senior-living play, HomeInstead radars). Selling point: ceiling radar is camera-free and wearable-free — privacy-by-design + zero-burden for the senior.

## Strengths

- ✅ Ceiling-mounted radar — no cameras, no wearables; privacy + ergonomics.
- ✅ High specificity — "alerts only for serious falls, minimizing false alarms" (company claim).
- ✅ Singapore startup — local language + cultural fit.
- ✅ Healthcare-provider + senior-care facility partnerships.
- ✅ Caregiver smartphone notifications.
- ✅ Installation + setup service included.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ In-home only — no mobile coverage.
- ❌ Single-sensor approach — limited activity monitoring vs multi-sensor HoME+ / Korea 119.
- ❌ No trained-care-specialist ARC — alerts route to caregivers only.
- ❌ No clinical integration.
- ❌ Pricing opaque.
- ❌ Brand awareness limited outside Singapore partnership channels.
