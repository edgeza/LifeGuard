## Product lineup

- **RDNS Alarms (SA)** — in-home pendant alarm + base unit + 24/7 monitoring (Tunstall 24/7 ARC). South Australian Government Personal Alert Systems Rebate Scheme eligible.
- **Silver Chain CareLink Personal Alarm (WA)** — WA state government co-funded; free or nominal $2.50/wk for Pension Concession Card holders; 1,739+ seniors covered as of 1995 restructuring ⚠️ (current count higher).
- **Falls detectors** — in-home fall detector add-on.
- **Home Hospital** — acute-care-in-the-home service (separate product).
- **Personal alarms and fall detectors** — bundled with home-care packages (Silver Chain CarePLUS).

## Differentiation

RDNS / Silver Chain is the largest non-profit nursing-association PERS in Australia and the only one co-funded by state governments (CareLink WA, PASRS SA). Selling point: government co-funding pathway + home-hospital service bundling; "the nursing association you already trust" is the trust anchor.

## Strengths

- ✅ Non-profit nursing association — strongest trust brand in WA and SA PERS.
- ✅ State-government co-funded pathways (CareLink WA, PASRS SA) — nominal or free for low-income elderly.
- ✅ Bundled with home-care services (CarePLUS, Home Hospital) — single-vendor integrated care.
- ✅ NDIS, DVA, HCP, CHSP accredited.
- ✅ Silver Chain Home Hospital channel differentiates against pure PERS rivals.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Limited interstate brand recognition (WA / SA centric).
- ❌ CareLink WA pathway depends on PCC eligibility — many seniors fall outside subsidy.
- ❌ Mobile GPS pendant SKU is not core — competitors (LiveLife, Tunstall Gem4/5) lead on go-anywhere.
- ❌ Caregiver app less mature than MePACS, INS LifeGuard, or Tunstall's published layers.
- ❌ Reliant on Tunstall's 24/7 monitoring in SA — corporate dependency vs wholly-owned ARC.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Caregiver-app integration for non-funded clients.** RDNS has the trust brand but lacks a modern app for adult-child caregivers of self-funded clients; partner or compete for that segment.
- **Mobile SKU partnership.** RDNS could white-label MyLifeLine EV07BA as the Silver Chain mobile SKU for go-anywhere seniors not served by CareLink in-home.
- **B2G export.** Silver Chain's WA CareLink model is exportable as a state-provincial partnership blueprint in markets with similar welfare structures (NZ, Singapore HDB AAS, Korea 119 Ansim Call).
