## Product lineup

- **Emergency Button (Home)** — Smart Home Base Unit + wearable; press = 24/7 emergency response team connection; emergency nurses talk to the patient; ambulance dispatched if needed.
- **Help Trigger (Mobile)** — wearable with automatic fall detection; GPS location sent to ViMUT Alarm Centre on activation.
- **Tele Nurse** — online symptom-tracking + health guidance (reduces unnecessary hospital travel).
- **Online Health Screening** — bundled.
- **Three packages** — Silver, Gold, Platinum (entry from 1,000 THB/month).

## Differentiation

ViMUT Life Link is Bangkok's first hospital-branded in-home emergency alert + response service combining home-base-unit + mobile-fall-detection + ViMUT Alarm Centre + ViMUT Hospital clinical integration. Selling point: hospital-branded clinical escalation; nurse triage at the moment of emergency; tele-nurse bundled.

## Strengths

- ✅ Hospital-branded — clinical escalation trust anchor.
- ✅ Nurse-staffed 24/7 Alarm Centre (similar to INS CareCall AU positioning).
- ✅ Fall detection built into mobile SKU.
- ✅ Tele Nurse + Online Health Screening bundled.
- ✅ Three-tier pricing (Silver/Gold/Platinum).
- ✅ Thai + English bilingual support.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Bangkok-first — limited national coverage.
- ❌ Subscription pricing not transparent for Gold/Platinum tiers.
- ❌ No caregiver app depth visible.
- ❌ No B2G / government integration.
- ❌ Single-hospital brand — no expansion to other Thai hospital groups.
- ❌ Limited marketing outside ViMUT patient channel.
