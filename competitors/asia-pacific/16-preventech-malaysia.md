## Differentiation

Preventech is positioned as "Malaysia's 1st elderly/senior emergency-response smart band" with vital-sign anomaly detection plus a multi-partner dispatch network (St John + Red Crescent + private medical partners). Selling point: 50% faster response than 999 (Malaysian emergency number).

## Strengths

- ✅ "Malaysia's 1st" positioning — first-mover advantage in Malaysian senior smart-band segment.
- ✅ Vital-sign anomaly detection — beyond panic button.
- ✅ Multi-partner dispatch (St John + Red Crescent + private medical) — credible escalation.
- ✅ Real-time platform (vs periodic check-in competitors).
- ✅ Local partner network in Penang, Selangor, KL — Malaysia's three main economic zones.
- ✅ Trilingual (English / Bahasa / Mandarin).

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Coverage limited to Penang, Selangor, KL — Sabah, Sarawak, east-coast states not yet covered.
- ❌ Pricing not transparent — quote-only.
- ❌ Small team; brand awareness limited outside Penang.
- ❌ No published caregiver app / dashboard.
- ❌ No B2G (Malaysian Ministry of Health) integration visible.
- ❌ Claims "50% faster response than 999" not independently verified.
