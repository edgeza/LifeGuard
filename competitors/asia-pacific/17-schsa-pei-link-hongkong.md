## Product lineup

- **一線通® 平安鐘 (PE Link — Wireless PEL)** — 4G mobile-network plug-and-use base unit; pendant SOS; 24/7 monitoring; emergency aid + location tracking + outpatient appointment booking + medicine-taking reminder + day-to-day inquiries.
- **一線通® 守護卡 (Guardian Card)** — wearable SIM-based mobile SOS card with location-search + speed-dial; family-app visibility.
- **EasyHome® Service** — premium home-care tier (3 care levels: standard → advanced).
- **Rehabilitation + medical-escort services** — adjunct home care.
- **Smart Accident Detection System** — pilot scheme using AI fall detector.

## Differentiation

SCHSA 一線通® 平安鐘 is Hong Kong's only large-scale 24/7 PE Link service and the non-profit social enterprise that has operated the entire Hong Kong PE Link ecosystem since 1996. Selling point: 30-year brand trust, SWD-subsidy channel reaching ~4,000 low-income elderly/disability beneficiaries, bilingual operators, and an expanding product line (Guardian Card + Smart Accident Detection pilot).

## Strengths

- ✅ Non-profit social enterprise — strongest trust brand in HK senior-care.
- ✅ SWD subsidy pathway — direct government-funded channel for low-income cohort.
- ✅ Bilingual (Cantonese + Mandarin + English) — covers all major HK populations.
- ✅ 30-year brand and operational history; founded in response to a public-health crisis.
- ✅ 18-district Care Team referral network.
- ✅ Active expansion to AI fall detection + Guardian Card wearable (2025–2026).

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Hong Kong-only — no mainland China / Macau export.
- ❌ Standard PE Link is button-press only; AI fall detection is pilot-scale.
- ❌ Caregiver app depth is shallow (location + usage logs only).
- ❌ SWD-subsidy means-tested — middle-class seniors fall outside.
- ❌ Limited medical-record integration (vs Apple Health, Samsung Health).
- ❌ Slow government-mandated upgrade cycles (procurement-driven).

## MyLifeLine opportunity
