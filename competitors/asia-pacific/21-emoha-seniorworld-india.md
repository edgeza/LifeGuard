# Emoha Elder Care / SeniorWorld (ASLI-affiliated, India)

> **Verification flag:** ✅ verified across multiple sources
> **Last updated:** 2026-07-17
> **Country:** India / South Asia
> **Primary segment:** Senior home-care subscription with embedded 24/7 emergency response + DIY safety devices
> **Profile scope:** India (Delhi-NCR primary; partner network pan-India)

**Note on task brief:** The task brief referenced "ASLI Care" as a single competitor. ASLI (Association of Senior Living India) is in fact a non-profit senior-living industry body that accredits multiple senior-care providers. Emoha Elder Care and SeniorWorld are ASLI-member PERS / eldercare operators covered here.

## Product lineup (Emoha)

- **24/7 Emergency Response** — core subscription feature.
- **Preventive Health Check-ups** — bundled with subscription.
- **Electronic Health Records** — cloud-based elder health records.
- **Doctor + Physiotherapist Home Visits**.
- **Hospital-visit assistance + routine errands**.
- **Home-safety solutions**.
- **Engagement programmes** — emotional-happiness focus.

## Product lineup (SeniorWorld)

- **easyfone** — simplified mobile phone + service designed for elders.
- **JUVO** — DIY home-safety devices for elderly (range of fall-detection / panic-button devices).
- **SilverWings** — assisted-travel packages for elderly.

## Differentiation

Emoha + SeniorWorld form the most credible ASLI-affiliated elder-care subscription + emergency-response offering in India, with the "JUVO" hardware line and the SeniorWorld Club community (2.5 lakh+ members). Selling point: emotional-happiness engagement focus + ex-defence caregivers + 24/7 emergency response bundled into a monthly subscription.

## Strengths

- ✅ ASLI-ecosystem affiliation — credibility within Indian senior-living industry.
- ✅ SeniorWorld Club 2.5 lakh+ community — large funnel for subscription conversion.
- ✅ JUVO DIY home-safety line — accessible entry-tier product.
- ✅ Emoha's ex-defence personnel caregivers — trust narrative.
- ✅ Bundled subscription (24/7 + health records + doctor visits + errands).
- ✅ Emoha app — caregiver / family layer.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Limited scale (Emoha ~60 families in Delhi).
- ❌ No mobile GPS pendant SKU (JUVO is home-DIY).
- ❌ No fall detection in core subscription (sold as add-on).
- ❌ Quote-only pricing — no transparent published monthly rate.
- ❌ Reliance on ASLI-member referral funnel (industry-body affiliation is a constraint).
- ❌ Limited national brand recognition outside Delhi-NCR.
