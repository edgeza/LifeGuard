## Differentiation

CareLink Thailand is positioned as a "medical-escort + daily-check-in" hybrid for Bangkok families whose elderly live alone. Selling point: 365-day support, "first in Thailand," LINE-based alerts (matches Thailand's #1 messaging platform).

## Strengths

- ✅ LINE-based alerts — matches Thailand's #1 messaging platform usage.
- ✅ "1st in Thailand" positioning — first-mover for Bangkok medical-escort + check-in hybrid.
- ✅ Free entry tier — accessible.
- ✅ 365-day support branding.
- ✅ Real-time status updates during medical escort.
- ✅ Vacation mode (pause when family is together).

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Bangkok-only.
- ❌ No hardware / no wearable.
- ❌ No fall detection.
- ❌ Daily check-in requires senior self-action (not automatic).
- ❌ Limited brand awareness outside Bangkok.
- ❌ No government / B2G integration.
