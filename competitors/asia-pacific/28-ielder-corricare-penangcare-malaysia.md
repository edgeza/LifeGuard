## Differentiation

This is a combined profile of three Malaysia-based PERS / eldercare providers operating at different scales. iElder and CorriCare are commercially-positioned D2C/B2B Malaysian PERS; Penang Care Society is the NGO pathway for dementia-tracker (GPS4E) deployment. Combined, they represent the breadth of Malaysian senior PERS — commercial to charitable.

## Strengths

- ✅ Multiple Malaysian channel options (commercial + charity).
- ✅ Penang Care Society GPS4E is SIRIM-endorsed (national standards).
- ✅ CorriCare IMS is enterprise-grade for residential-care facilities.
- ✅ iElder CareWatch is wireless + multi-form-factor.
- ✅ Local language + cultural fit (Bahasa Malaysia, English, Mandarin).
- ✅ Hospital / residential-care B2B partnerships.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Pricing opaque across all three.
- ❌ Brand awareness limited outside Penang / KL.
- ❌ No trained-care-specialist ARC for clinical escalation.
- ❌ Limited cross-state coverage (Penang-centric for Penang Care Society).
- ❌ No national-scale programme integration (unlike Korea 119 Ansim Call).
