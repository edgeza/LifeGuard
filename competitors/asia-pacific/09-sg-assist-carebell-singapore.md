## Product lineup

- **CareBell mobile application** — smartphone-based SOS button that routes to SG Assist Call Centre (24/7/365).
- **CareGiverCircle Membership Programme** — caregiver community + free 12-month CareBell + free Singlife Group Term Life (1-year complimentary for first 200 successful applicants; Singaporean/PR/Employment-pass eligible).
- **SG Assist travel-assistance product suite** — extends to ASEAN travel emergencies.
- **Hardware CareBell Device** — physical pendant that pairs with smartphone app (subscription included).

## Differentiation

SG Assist CareBell is the only Singapore-headquartered PERS smartphone-app with a 24/7 in-country call centre and a multi-language (EN/MN/MS/TA) SG response team. Selling point: bilingual Singapore-based triage + CareGiverCircle caregiver community + Singlife-sponsored caregiver insurance for first 200 applicants.

## Strengths

- ✅ Singapore-only in-country 24/7 call centre (vs foreign-routed).
- ✅ Four Singapore official languages supported — meaningful for Singapore's multilingual elderly.
- ✅ Singlife caregiver insurance partnership — first-in-Singapore bundled offering.
- ✅ CareGiverCircle membership community — adds emotional brand moat beyond technology.
- ✅ Subscription model with published SGD $20/month rate — transparent pricing.
- ✅ B2B partnerships with geriatric day-care and senior activity centres (referral channel).

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Singapore-only — no Malaysia, Indonesia, or ASEAN expansion.
- ❌ Smartphone-dependent — Care Recipient must have an Internet-enabled smartphone.
- ❌ 6/12/24-month contracts — long Minimum Period of Service vs no-monthly-fee rivals.
- ❌ No fall detection (purely button-press).
- ❌ No first-party pendant hardware (only the smartphone app + a paired hardware CareBell pendant).
- ❌ Limited caregiver-app depth (vs INS CareCall AU / Tunstall Gem5 depth).

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **ASEAN cross-sell from Singapore.** MyLifeLine has no Singapore presence; CareBell's Care Recipient geography (Singapore) is a clear switcher segment for adult-child buyers willing to switch from app-dependent to pendant-only hardware.
- **Hardware-only differentiator.** CareBell requires a smartphone; the EV07BA is a standalone 4G pendant with own SIM — useful for elderly who don't carry smartphones.
- **Caregiver-app depth.** CareGiverCircle is a community; MyLifeLine's dashboard + location + alert-log is more operational for adult-child caregivers managing remote parents.
- **B2B eldercare and geriatric-day-care partnership.** MyLifeLine could pitch the EV07BA as the partner pendant for SG Assist's CareGiverCircle corporate clients.
