## Differentiation

Peoplesafe is the **UK public-sector lone-worker default** — the largest provider for UK central and local government, with the broadest accreditation set (BS 8484:2022 + BS 9518 + EN 50518 + DTAC + Secured by Design + Cyber Essentials Plus + ISO 27001 + ISO 9001). Where SoloProtect is the Cheltenham-based challenger, Peoplesafe is the procurement-safe Derby-based incumbent.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Largest UK public-sector market share** — procurement-safe default for NHS, councils, blue-light services.
- ✅ **Most extensive UK certification stack** — BS 8484:2022 + BS 9518 + EN 50518 + DTAC + Secured by Design; no competitor matches this depth.
- ✅ **Owned purpose-built ARC** — EN 50518-compliant, 24/7/365, BS 9518 monitoring accreditation.
- ✅ **Travelsafe app + body-worn camera + dashcam** — the broadest product portfolio in UK lone worker.
- ✅ **3-4 second ARC answer time** — best-in-class published SLA.
- ✅ **Award-winning Nexus platform** — proactive people-risk management vs reactive alerting.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Multi-year contracts required** — friction for SMB customers.
- ❌ **UK + Ireland primary footprint** — limited EU, AU/NZ, North America presence.
- ❌ **Premium pricing vs app-only competitors** — quote-only hardware + ARC bundle exceeds £30-$50/user/month.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no satellite option** — not for hazardous-environment industries.
- ❌ **No white-label programme** — Peoplesafe brand only.
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc or Vatix Wi-Fi positioning.
