## Differentiation

Duress is the **"privacy-first + live-video verification"** lone-worker app: only when an emergency is declared does the device stream live video + audio + GPS to the A1-graded EOC. Most competitors require ongoing location tracking; Duress markets the absence-of-tracking as a privacy feature, appealing to community workers, social workers, and remote-area staff concerned about constant employer surveillance.

## Strengths

- ✅ **A1-graded in-house EOC** — same accreditation tier as SafeTCard and My Safety Buddy; URN police dispatch.
- ✅ **Live video verification** — significantly reduces false-alarm police dispatches vs audio-only ARC vendors.
- ✅ **Privacy-first design** — location only shared when alarm is active; competitive differentiator in worker-surveillance-sensitive verticals (community services, NFPs, unions).
- ✅ **Australia-data-sovereignty** — all data stays in-country; aligns with Australian Privacy Principles.
- ✅ **Phoenix BLE trigger** — discreet activation without unlocking phone.
- ✅ **Strong mobile-app UX** — repeat App Store / Play Store positive reviews.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **AU/NZ/UK primary footprint.** Limited brand recognition in North America.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent certification publicly listed** — UK public-sector buyers used to seeing the BS standard may require due-diligence sign-off.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no satellite option** — not a fit for hazardous-environment industries.
- ❌ **Hardware lineup is thinner than SoloProtect or Blackline.**
- ❌ **No public API** — limited integration story vs StaySafe or SHEQSY.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Privacy-first + white-label is a defensible niche.** Workers globally are pushing back on constant tracking; MyLifeLine can copy the Duress "only-on-alarm" architecture as a marketing wedge.
- **Live-video verification as a managed-monitoring feature.** MyLifeLine's managed-ARC tier can offer live video verification at the same A1 grade as Duress for SMB customers Duress's enterprise pricing excludes.
- **Multi-region data residency.** Duress is AU-only; MyLifeLine can offer EU + UK + AU + US data residency from one product, removing a hard constraint for multinational buyers.
