## Differentiation

WorkSafe Guardian is the **Ideagen-owned Australian lone-worker app with cross-sell into a global EHS portfolio** — Ideagen's enterprise customers in 100+ countries can buy the safety module from the same vendor that supplies their risk, audit, and compliance software. The "Australia's first lone-worker app" positioning is also a distinctive heritage claim.

## Strengths

- ✅ **First-mover in Australia** — "Australia's first lone-worker app" heritage claim.
- ✅ **25+ years of security-industry experience** (vendor figure) — deep operational knowledge.
- ✅ **Multiple activation methods** — press, shake, voice command, BLE button — good UX flexibility.
- ✅ **Ideagen corporate backing** — global enterprise channel + ISO 27001 corporate stack.
- ✅ **Quick to deploy, scalable** — positioned as a lightweight enterprise solution.
- ✅ **Free trial with no payment details** — low procurement friction.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Limited brand recognition outside AU + Ideagen's enterprise customers.**
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc or TraknProtect.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no satellite, no dedicated hardware.**
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK / EU regulated buyers face accreditation gaps.
- ❌ **No mass-notification module** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No published public pricing** — quote-only friction.

## MyLifeLine opportunity
