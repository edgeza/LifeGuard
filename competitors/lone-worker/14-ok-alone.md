## Differentiation

OK Alone is the **SMB-friendly price-disruptor** of the lone-worker app segment — month-to-month contracts, transparent per-worker pricing in USD and GBP, free tier (Essential) for self-monitored customers, and a 24/7 ARC tier for higher-risk deployments. The transparent $10/£7 per worker per month price is published on the vendor's own site, which is rare in this segment.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Transparent pricing published online** — $10/£7 per worker per month for the Worker Plan.
- ✅ **No long-term contract** — month-to-month flexibility; discounts for quarterly / annual prepay.
- ✅ **Self-monitored entry tier** — Essential plan lets very small businesses start at near-zero cost.
- ✅ **Multi-tier pricing** — Essential → Automated Monitoring → 24/7 Emergency Response → Enterprise.
- ✅ **Garmin + SPOT satellite support** — works with the same hardware as SafetyLine.
- ✅ **Free trial, no credit card** — low procurement friction.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Brand recognition outside NZ / US / UK is limited.**
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** — not for hospitality, healthcare.
- ❌ **No mass-notification** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no dedicated wearable hardware** — high-risk roles need Blackline / SoloProtect hardware.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 listed** — UK regulated buyers face accreditation gaps.
- ❌ **Less established brand than SoloProtect or Peoplesafe** — harder to win UK public-sector tenders.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Transparent pricing + month-to-month as the wedge.** MyLifeLine can adopt OK Alone's pricing transparency and contract flexibility as a competitive differentiator vs the quote-only, multi-year-lock-in incumbents.
- **Self-monitored tier for sub-25-seat accounts.** Many small businesses want basic check-in safety without paying for an ARC; OK Alone has validated that this tier sells.
- **Multi-region white-label + multi-language.** OK Alone is English-only; MyLifeLine can launch with EN/ES/FR/AU baked in to win non-English markets.
