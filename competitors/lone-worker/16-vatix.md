## Product lineup

- **Vatix Lone Worker App** — iOS + Android; one-touch SOS that places an instant phone call to the ARC; timed working sessions; privacy mode (location off outside working hours); voice notes; Wi-Fi indoor positioning.
- **Safe Pro hardware device** — dedicated cellular wearable; fall detection (man-down) as a £1/device/month add-on; two-way voice.
- **Hybrid deployments** — app + Safe Pro hardware in the same account; common pattern for high-risk roles getting hardware and lower-risk getting app.
- **Management portal** — unlimited manager users; alarm response instructions; usage dashboards.
- **Self-monitoring option** — alarms route to nominated internal contacts.
- **Conexa eSIM from Wireless Logic** — multi-network SIM for resilient connectivity.

## Differentiation

Vatix is the **"app-first + UK BS 8484 + Wi-Fi indoor positioning"** challenger to SoloProtect and Peoplesafe — the unique technical differentiator is **advanced Wi-Fi indoor positioning**, which addresses the gap that Pure GPS leaves indoors. Combined with BS 8484:2022 accreditation and a hybrid app + dedicated-device deployment model, Vatix is one of the strongest UK mid-market alternatives.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Wi-Fi indoor positioning** — rare capability in the lone-worker app segment; addresses the gap where GPS fails indoors.
- ✅ **Two UK-based ARCs** — better resilience than single-ARC vendors.
- ✅ **BS 8484:2022 accredited** — guarantees Level-1 police URN bypass for UK buyers.
- ✅ **Hybrid app + Safe Pro hardware** — same account can mix deployment types.
- ✅ **Privacy mode** — location only shared during active sessions; worker privacy respected.
- ✅ **Self-monitoring option** — flexible for organisations that want internal routing.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **UK + Ireland primary footprint** — limited brand recognition in North America, AU/NZ, EU.
- ❌ **Limited public API** — enterprise integration friction.
- ❌ **No mass-notification** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no satellite option** — not for hazardous-environment or remote-area work.
- ❌ **Hardware requires 12-month contract with upfront fee** — friction for SMB customers.
- ❌ **No white-label programme** — Vatix brand only.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Wi-Fi indoor positioning as a standard feature.** Vatix's strongest technical differentiator is replicable; MyLifeLine can ship Wi-Fi indoor positioning as a default feature instead of an upsell.
- **Self-monitoring tier for sub-50-seat accounts.** Vatix's self-monitoring option is rigid; MyLifeLine's free or low-cost self-monitored tier can win more SMB customers.
- **White-label + multi-language.** Vatix is UK-English-only; MyLifeLine can win EU + LATAM + non-English markets.
