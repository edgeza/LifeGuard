## Product lineup

- **RAYVN Emergency Notification** — four-channel alert: automated voice call + email + push notification (mobile app) + SMS; replies (Yes / Ok / No) auto-categorise availability.
- **RAYVN Emergency Communications Solution (ECS)** — high-throughput SMS module (10× faster than standard RAYVN); intended for >500-recipient mass notifications.
- **RAYVN Mobile App** — iOS + Android; push alerts + acknowledgement + incident logs.
- **RAYVN Web Console** — scenario templates, contact lists, group targeting, live sending monitor with error tracking.
- **Cyber-attack / silent-evacuation scenario library** — pre-built notification templates for common crisis scenarios.

## Differentiation

RAYVN is the **Scandinavian mass-notification challenger** to Everbridge / AlertMedia / Alertus — pure software, no hardware, with **multi-channel replies (Yes / No / OK)** built in so the platform auto-categorises team availability in real time. Strong in Nordic industrial, healthcare, and public-sector accounts.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Reply-tracking built in** — Yes/No/OK categorisation is a workflow differentiator vs Everbridge's one-way blast.
- ✅ **High-throughput ECS module** — designed for >500-recipient blasts; useful for IT outages and silent evacuations.
- ✅ **Strong European data residency** — Norwegian HQ + EU cloud hosting; GDPR-aligned.
- ✅ **Scenario library** — pre-built templates for common crisis scenarios shorten time-to-first-alert.
- ✅ **Multi-language UI** — Scandinavian + English + German out-of-the-box.
- ✅ **ISO 27001 + GDPR** — meets EU regulated buyer requirements.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **No lone-worker wearable, no app-based SOS, no GPS tracking, no indoor location** — pure notification platform.
- ❌ **Limited North American brand recognition.**
- ❌ **Smaller customer base than Everbridge / AlertMedia.**
- ❌ **No ARC; customer-self-monitored.**
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent for lone worker monitoring.** RAYVN is for incident notification, not personal safety monitoring.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Bridge the lone-worker + mass-notification gap.** RAYVN is for the comms half; MyLifeLine's duress app + ARC tier completes the loop. Position MyLifeLine as a "RAYVN with the safety half built in" or partner on a bundled offering.
