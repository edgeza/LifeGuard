## Differentiation

Fleetistics / CrewChief is the **fleet-management-adjacent lone-worker solution for drivers and field workers** — the value proposition is "you already have fleet tracking; add lone-worker protection for the same driver without buying a separate platform". The Geotab integration is the key wedge for fleet operators.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Fleet + lone-worker in one product** — eliminates the need for separate vendors for drivers.
- ✅ **Geotab integration** — leverages existing fleet telematics data.
- ✅ **"Pennies a day" pricing positioning** — cheap add-on to fleet subscription.
- ✅ **Multiple internal notification channels** — SMS, toast, email, voice call.
- ✅ **Accident report form** — drives documentation for insurance/liability.
- ✅ **Invisible mode** — covert operation; location only shared during emergency.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Not connected to 911** — explicitly noted as a feature gap; routes to designated employees only.
- ❌ **No ARC** — customer-self-monitored only.
- ❌ **No mass-notification at enterprise scale.**
- ❌ **No indoor location, no gas detection, no satellite.**
- ❌ **Fleet-focused branding** — not a fit for non-fleet safety buyers (community workers, healthcare, hospitality).
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK / EU regulated buyers face accreditation gaps.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Cross-sell from fleet to safety.** Fleetistics proves the demand; MyLifeLine can offer a deeper lone-worker product with ARC + mass-notification as the next step up for fleet operators who outgrow CrewChief.
