## Differentiation

SafeTCard is the **Australian family-owned dedicated-device duress vendor** — first-mover in the Australian market (since 2010), with the **only ASIAL-certified Grade A1 ARC dedicated to lone workers located in Australia**. Family-owned positioning and Queensland-based ARC are trust signals for Australian government and community-services buyers.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Australia-first heritage** — first dedicated lone-worker vendor in AU (since 2010).
- ✅ **ASIAL Grade A1 ARC** — only ASIAL-certified Grade A1 ARC dedicated to lone workers in Australia.
- ✅ **Family-owned positioning** — trust signal for Australian government + NFP buyers.
- ✅ **99.95% response rate under 60s** — strong published SLA.
- ✅ **Multiple device form factors** — ID badge, GPS duress, Bivy Stick satellite.
- ✅ **Industry coverage** — healthcare, retail, libraries, NFPs, regional areas.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **AU + NZ only footprint.**
- ❌ **Limited public pricing** — quote-only friction.
- ❌ **Hardware-first SKU** — entry cost >$400 per device; friction for SMB.
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc or TraknProtect.
- ❌ **No mass-notification module** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK / EU regulated buyers face accreditation gaps.
