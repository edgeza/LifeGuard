## Differentiation

The Sentry is the **explicit price-disruptor** of the UK lone-worker market — published pricing of £39.50/user/year with zero setup fee, zero hardware cost, no multi-year lock-in, and 3 free Friends & Family licences per commercial user. The vendor publishes a side-by-side comparison table explicitly positioning against Safepoint, StaySafe, Vatix, Ok Alone, Peoplesafe, and SoloProtect.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Lowest published price in the UK** — £39.50/user/year vs SoloProtect's £36+/year and StaySafe's £114+/year.
- ✅ **Zero setup fee, zero hardware cost, no multi-year lock-in** — radical transparency vs UK competitors.
- ✅ **Friends & Family licences** — unique B2B-to-consumer feature; worker can extend protection to family.
- ✅ **Annual contract only** — flexibility that enterprise rivals don't offer.
- ✅ **Compliance documentation pack included** — no premium tier for compliance.
- ✅ **Internal escalation pathway** — uses the customer's own team for response, avoiding ARC overhead.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **No 24/7 ARC** — all UK regulated buyers requiring BS 8484:2022 Level-1 police URN bypass cannot use this product alone.
- ❌ **Limited feature set** — no fall detection, no indoor location, no gas detection, no satellite, no mass-notification.
- ❌ **UK-only brand awareness.**
- ❌ **No enterprise integration story** — minimal public API, no SSO documentation.
- ❌ **No BS 8484:2022 certification listed** — friction for UK public-sector buyers.
- ❌ **Newer entrant** — track record unproven at scale vs SoloProtect's 250,000+ users or Peoplesafe's public-sector dominance.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Mirror The Sentry's pricing transparency + add ARC capability.** The Sentry's weakness is no ARC; MyLifeLine can offer £39.50/year as the self-monitored tier plus an ARC tier at a higher price, undercutting SoloProtect and Peoplesafe while closing the certification gap.
- **Friends & Family as a global wedge.** Personal-safety licences bundled with commercial licences is unique and globally replicable.
- **Annual contract + zero setup fee as marketing.** These two policy choices are deal-closers for SMB procurement.
