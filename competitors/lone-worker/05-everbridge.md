## Product lineup

- **Everbridge 360 Core** — mass notification basics (SMS, voice, email, push); contact database; event templates.
- **Everbridge 360 Professional** — adds advanced automation, integration APIs, expanded contact-DB limits.
- **Everbridge 360 Enterprise** — adds Visual Command Center, Risk Intelligence, AI-driven orchestration, workforce resilience, Safety Connection (employee check-in + travel-risk + lone-worker monitoring).
- **IT Alerting** — module for IT incident management.
- **Risk Intelligence** — global threat monitoring (weather, civil, geo-political).
- **Crisis Management** — incident orchestration, dashboards, after-action reporting.
- **Visual Command Center** — situational awareness dashboard for ops centres.

## Differentiation

Everbridge is the **largest and most entrenched CEM platform globally** — its brand is shorthand for "mass notification" the same way that BlackBerry was shorthand for "secure mobile" in the 2000s. Customers buy Everbridge partly for the breadth of features and partly because it is the only vendor their auditors and regulators recognise.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Largest customer base and brand recognition in CEM.** 6,500+ global customers; procurement-safe default in US public sector and Fortune 500.
- ✅ **Broadest module portfolio** — Mass Notification + IT Alerting + Risk Intelligence + Crisis Management + Visual Command Center on a single platform.
- ✅ **Global threat intelligence** — 200+ countries monitored; geo-political and weather incident alerts integrated into the console.
- ✅ **FedRAMP + HIPAA + SOC 2 + ISO 27001** — meets the most demanding regulated-industry bar.
- ✅ **Visual Command Center** is the most polished situational-awareness dashboard in the category.
- ✅ **Safety Connection adds lone-worker capability** without forcing a separate vendor contract.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Most expensive at scale.** $194K enterprise average vs AlertMedia's $75K — Everbridge is the premium-tier anchor.
- ❌ **Implementation overhead.** $50K-$100K+ implementation fees for enterprise; not a self-serve product.
- ❌ **Not a real ARC.** Customers must run their own 24/7 monitoring or layer in a partner ARC like Peoplesafe or SoloProtect.
- ❌ **Annual escalators and overage clauses** — common in master agreements (Vendr).
- ❌ **Lone-worker feature set is thin** compared to dedicated vendors — it's a safety module, not a product.
- ❌ **UX complexity.** Repeatedly cited as "harder to learn" than AlertMedia; admins need dedicated training.
