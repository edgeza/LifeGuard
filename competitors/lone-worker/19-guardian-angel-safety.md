## Differentiation

Guardian Angel Safety is the **ANZ-region ARC reseller/monitoring specialist** — instead of building a device, Guardian Angel integrates with the best-of-breed devices (Blackline, Garmin inReach, Meitrack) and provides the A1-graded monitoring + response service as a layer. Two-operator incident handling and bi-annual government audit are differentiators.

## Strengths

- ✅ **A1-graded dual-ARC (NZ + Australia)** — better resilience than single-ARC vendors.
- ✅ **Multi-vendor monitoring** — works with Blackline, Garmin, Meitrack; customers can mix device types under one ARC contract.
- ✅ **Bi-annual government audit** — government-grade ARC certification; rare outside the largest vendors.
- ✅ **Two-operator incident handling** — better escalation discipline than single-operator ARCs.
- ✅ **Back-up facility in a different city** — disaster-recovery redundancy rare in mid-market vendors.
- ✅ **Police-vetted operators** — strong trust signal for government + utility buyers.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **ANZ-only footprint.**
- ❌ **No proprietary device** — relies on third-party hardware; doesn't control the full SKU.
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc.
- ❌ **No mass-notification** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK / EU regulated buyers face accreditation gaps.
- ❌ **Limited public pricing** — quote-only friction.
