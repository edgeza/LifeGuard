## Differentiation

TraknProtect is the **vertical-specialist panic button vendor for hotels and resorts** — where CriticalArc is for higher-ed campuses, TraknProtect is for housekeeping and room-service staff who need **floor and room-level location** during a guest-on-employee incident. The discrete wearable button is the only employee-facing form factor, which fits the hospitality dress-code and discretion requirement.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Best-in-class indoor location for hospitality** — floor + room-level accuracy via BLE 5.1 + Wi-Fi; few competitors match this in a hotel environment.
- ✅ **False-alarm prevention engineering** — dual contact pads on the safety button are a real differentiator vs single-pad buttons (single-press duress requires deliberate action).
- ✅ **AHLA partner / industry alignment** — pre-cleared for AHLA's 5-Star Promise on hotel safety.
- ✅ **Hardware agnostic platform** — works with existing hotel Wi-Fi infrastructure; doesn't require a new network build.
- ✅ **Mobile + stationary button options** — covers housekeeping (mobile) and back-of-house (fixed).
- ✅ **Asset tracking as upsell** — extends the platform beyond pure employee safety.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Hospitality-only vertical** — no play in healthcare, construction, utilities, social work, hospitality adjacencies (restaurants, casinos).
- ❌ **No mass-notification beyond security alerting** — limited enterprise-wide crisis-management story.
- ❌ **No lone-worker app** — button-only; high-risk remote workers still need a SoloProtect or Blackline.
- ❌ **No ARC.** Customer-self-monitored; no professional 24/7 monitoring.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK / EU buyers face certification gaps.
- ❌ **Customer concentration risk** — hospitality is cyclical; the platform's TAM is narrow.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Cross-vertical panic button SaaS.** TraknProtect is locked to hospitality; MyLifeLine can offer the same BLE-beacon + room-level-location pattern to healthcare (nurse duress), retail (back-of-house), education (classroom Alyssa Law) with one platform.
- **White-label the panic button for security integrators.** Security-installation companies want to add panic-button services; MyLifeLine can be the SaaS backend.
- **Add an ARC layer TraknProtect refuses.** Many hotels don't run 24/7 security desks; a managed-monitoring tier on top of TraknProtect hardware is a viable white-label product.
