## Differentiation

SHEQSY is the **workflow-aware lone-worker app for non-profits and government** — appointment-aware check-ins, pre-start safety forms, hazard reporting, overtime alerts, and navigation-to-appointment sit on top of the duress feature. Where SoloProtect and Blackline sell "protection", SHEQSY sells "visibility + safety + accountability" for dispersed teams.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Quick Panic widget** — duress activation without unlocking the phone; a real UX differentiator vs standard app-only SOS.
- ✅ **Workflow-aware features** — pre-start forms, hazard reporting, overtime alerts — built for non-profit community workers who do more than just "be present somewhere".
- ✅ **Affordable SMB positioning** — marketed as the most affordable enterprise-grade lone-worker app.
- ✅ **Optional 24/7 monitoring centre** — flexible ARC vs self-monitored deployment.
- ✅ **Calendar + scheduling integrations** — appointment-aware check-ins are unique among the app-first vendors.
- ✅ **Public APIs** — easier HR / scheduling integration than SoloProtect or Peoplesafe.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **AU/NZ/US primary footprint** — limited EU + UK presence.
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc or TraknProtect.
- ❌ **No mass-notification** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK/EU regulated buyers face accreditation gaps.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no dedicated wearable hardware** — high-risk roles need Blackline / SoloProtect hardware.
- ❌ **Brand awareness outside APAC + US is low.**

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Workflow-aware features as the wedge.** SHEQSY has the right idea with pre-start forms + appointment-aware check-ins; MyLifeLine can replicate these as standard product features and win non-profit / community-services customers globally.
- **Public APIs as a wedge.** SHEQSY publishes APIs; many competitors don't. MyLifeLine's API-first architecture is a defensible enterprise differentiator.
- **White-label + multi-language.** SHEQSY is English-only and APAC-primary; MyLifeLine can win EU + LATAM + non-English markets with the same product.
