## Differentiation

StaySafe's distinctive edge is **"low-signal mode"** — the app automatically escalates from mobile data → 2G → SMS, with the vendor claiming ~16% more coverage area than competitors that depend purely on data connections. Combined with the EcoOnline parent (a major EHS software group), StaySafe is the **app-first choice for organisations that already have an EcoOnline chemical/SDS stack** and want to bolt on worker safety.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Low-signal mode is a real differentiator** — automatically drops to SMS when data fails, useful for remote UK/AU workers.
- ✅ **Strong certifications pedigree** — BS 8484 + Cyber Essentials Plus + ISO 27001 (via EcoOnline).
- ✅ **Strong onboarding tooling** — fully automated in-app training, progress tracking, with optional on-site training for enterprise.
- ✅ **Garmin inReach satellite integration** — unique among the app-first lone-worker vendors.
- ✅ **EcoOnline cross-sell** — chemical-incident mass notification, SDS, training records all from one vendor.
- ✅ **Free 14-day trial** — lowest friction procurement for SMBs.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Outsourced ARC.** Unlike SoloProtect/Peoplesafe, StaySafe doesn't own the ARC; partner ARCs add a layer of contractual complexity for regulated buyers.
- ❌ **Limited Bluetooth indoor location.** Hotel/hospital customers need TraknProtect-style room-level accuracy.
- ❌ **No gas detection.** Not a fit for hazardous-environment industries.
- ❌ **Higher entry price than app-only competitors.** €9.99/mo is ~2-3× The Sentry's £39.50/year (~£3.30/mo equivalent).
- ❌ **36-month contract mentioned in competitor comparisons** ⚠️ — needs verification; if accurate, it locks out monthly buyers.
