## Product lineup

- **SoloProtect ID Classic** — ID-badge-format cellular pendant; discreet, single-button SOS; the entry-tier hardware device.
- **SoloProtect Shield** — heavier-duty wearable with man-down, fall detection, GPS; for high-risk environments.
- **SoloProtect Curve** — small, modern cellular pendant with one-touch SOS, fall detection, two-way voice.
- **SoloProtect Lone Worker App** — smartphone app (iOS + Android) that turns any work phone into a Red Alert device; no hardware required; Bluetooth Button accessory for discreet activation.
- **SoloProtect Insights** — web-based manager platform with location map, alert dashboard, mass notifications, ready2talk chaperone service.

## Differentiation

SoloProtect is the **UK's BS 8484:2022 + EN 50518 reference vendor**: the in-house ARC plus the ID-badge form factor plus the institutional trust built since the Connexion2 era (Council of Europe, NHS, water utilities) make it the procurement-safe default for UK public sector and regulated industries.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Owned, EN 50518-compliant ARC.** NSI-Gold and BS 8484:2022 — guarantees UK Level-1 police URN bypass on Red Alert.
- ✅ **Lowest published app-only entry price in the UK** at £3/user/month — the "price of a coffee per day" pitch is widely cited in industry press.
- ✅ **SoloProtect Insights is a mature manager console** — location history, alert analytics, mass notification, chaperone (Ready2Talk), mass-notification push.
- ✅ **Bluetooth Button accessory** — optional £BLE hardware triggers Red Alert without unlocking the phone.
- ✅ **Multi-form-factor hardware portfolio** (ID badge, Curve, Shield) covers discreet reception staff through to high-risk utilities workers.
- ✅ **3-second ARC answer / 41-second response time** — published SLA, faster than Peoplesafe's 4-second claim ⚠️ and Blackline's <60-second claim.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Quote-only pricing for hardware SKUs.** The headline £3/user/month is for the app; Shield/Curve pricing requires a sales conversation.
- ❌ **Multi-year lock-in required for hardware.** Customers on monthly app pricing don't get the cheapest hardware rates.
- ❌ **No Bluetooth indoor location.** Hotels, hospitals, warehouses with room-level resolution must use TraknProtect or SHEQSY+Bluetooth wearables.
- ❌ **No gas detection.** Cannot compete with Blackline in hazardous-environment work.
- ❌ **UK-centric brand.** Limited recognition outside UK/Ireland — AU/NZ resellers are still ramping up.
- ❌ **Mass notification only via app** — cannot SMS/call non-app users like AlertMedia can.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **White-label the ARC layer, not the brand.** SoloProtect's ARC is its moat; a MyLifeLine white-label offering can lease ARC capacity (or stand up an EN 50518 ARC) and undercut SoloProtect on flexible contracts while keeping the same Level-1 police response.
- **Indoor location as a differentiator.** SoloProtect lacks room-level location; MyLifeLine's Bluetooth beacon + Wi-Fi fingerprinting roadmap can address hospitality/healthcare niches SoloProtect can't.
- **Multi-tenant SaaS for SMEs.** SoloProtect's enterprise contract minimums leave 1-25-seat customers underserved; MyLifeLine's self-serve app plan is a natural entry point.
