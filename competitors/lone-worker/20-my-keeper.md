## Product lineup

- **Athena+ DATI beacon** (Dispositif d'Alarme pour Travailleur Isolé) — dedicated wearable beacon with SIM card (multi-operator GRPS/4G), SOS button, 8 programmable alert tones, sound diffuser; 98% cellular coverage across France; no distance limits.
- **SecurIT SOS app** — smartphone app for business-smartphone-equipped workers; manual and automatic alerts (fall, prolonged immobility, loss of verticality); geolocation; pairs with Keeper BLE button.
- **Keeper BLE alert button** — 23g; nRF52 ultra-low-power chipset; pairs with SecurIT SOS app for one-tap SOS without unlocking phone.
- **Hestia+ siren** — paired with Athena+ for PPMS scenarios.
- **Daycare / school PPMS module** — Specific Safety Plan (Plan Particulier de Mise en Sûreté) compliance for French schools and daycare centres.
- **Weekly operational status reports** — automated reporting on equipment health.

## Differentiation

My Keeper is the **French-market lone-worker + school-PPMS specialist** — the only vendor in this dossier that bundles lone-worker protection with the **Plan Particulier de Mise en Sûreté (PPMS)** regulatory compliance module that French schools, daycares, and municipalities are legally required to maintain.

## Strengths

- ✅ **PPMS (French school safety plan) compliance built in** — regulatory requirement for all French schools and daycares; My Keeper owns this niche.
- ✅ **Athena+ DATI beacon** — French-certified dedicated device; meets EU DATI (Dispositif d'Alarme pour Travailleur Isolé) regulatory requirements.
- ✅ **98% France cellular coverage via multi-operator SIM** — best-in-class for remote-area French workers.
- ✅ **CNIL + GDPR compliant** — meets French + EU data protection requirements.
- ✅ **24,700+ users** — significant European user base.
- ✅ **Three automatic alert triggers** (fall, immobility, loss of verticality) — robust detection logic.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **France-primary footprint** — limited brand recognition outside France.
- ❌ **French-language only** — major blocker for English-speaking markets.
- ❌ **No dedicated English-language docs** on the website (auto-translated) — friction for international buyers.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** — UK buyers face accreditation gaps.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no satellite option, no mass-notification at enterprise scale.**
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Multi-region PPMS / school safety compliance.** PPMS is France-specific, but similar school-emergency-response regulations exist in Germany, Spain, Italy; MyLifeLine can build compliance modules for each.
- **Multi-language EU expansion.** My Keeper is French-only; MyLifeLine can launch EN/FR/DE/ES/IT school-safety and lone-worker products.
- **White-label for the UK public sector.** UK schools have similar safety obligations under KCSIE (Keeping Children Safe in Education); MyLifeLine can offer a UK-localised equivalent.
