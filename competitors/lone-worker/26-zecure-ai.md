## Differentiation

Zecure.ai is a UK-focused app that combines **Journey Safe tracking + Duress Pin (covert distress) + evidence-capture (audio + video) + branded client customisation** in one product. The "branded customisation" capability makes it one of the few UK lone-worker apps that explicitly offers a white-label interface layer.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Duress Pin feature** — covert distress signal; misleads the attacker while silently notifying monitoring. Rare in UK lone-worker apps.
- ✅ **Journey Safe feature** — real-time journey tracking for high-risk travel (e.g. social workers, nurses on home visits).
- ✅ **Audio + video evidence capture on alarm** — supports prosecution.
- ✅ **Branded customisation** — clients can mirror their organisation's branding in the lone-worker alarm interface. A white-label-adjacent feature.
- ✅ **24/7 police-approved monitoring** — UK Level-1 police response implied.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Limited public information** — single primary source; product depth unverified.
- ❌ **UK-only brand awareness.**
- ❌ **No indoor Bluetooth location** like CriticalArc or TraknProtect.
- ❌ **No mass-notification module** — needs AlertMedia / RAYVN cross-sell.
- ❌ **No BS 8484 / EN 50518 equivalent** listed — friction for UK public-sector buyers.
- ❌ **No gas detection, no satellite, no dedicated hardware.**
