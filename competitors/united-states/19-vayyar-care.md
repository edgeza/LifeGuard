## Differentiation

Vayyar Care is the leading 4D imaging radar supplier for contactless fall detection globally; the device detects falls with no wearable and no camera, preserving privacy. It is exclusively B2B (senior-living operators and home-care agencies) — not a D2C PERS competitor to MyLifeLine, but a relevant technology-partner / threat if MyLifeLine ever ships an in-home radar sensor or partners into senior-living B2B.

## Strengths

- ✅ Privacy-by-design 4D imaging radar — no camera, no wearable, ideal for bathroom fall detection.
- ✅ Works through steam / darkness / obstructions (shower curtains).
- ✅ Behavior-pattern analytics beyond falls — bathroom visits, time in bed, mobility trends; predictive fall risk.
- ✅ Integrates with leading nurse-call and senior-living platforms (K4Connect, TekTone, NtelCare).
- ✅ Global regulatory certifications (FCC, CE, others — verify per market ⚠️).
- ✅ Track record across US, UK, EU, Australia senior-living deployments.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ B2B only — no D2C consumer subscription; MyLifeLine does not compete directly.
- ❌ Single-room coverage — multi-room requires multiple sensors (capex scales with home/apartment size).
- ❌ Not mobile — only covers in-home; outdoor fall detection requires another solution.
- ❌ Pricing is per-sensor / per-facility; not transparent.
- ❌ No voice-call subsystem — relies on partner nurse-call or family SMS pathway.
- ❌ Healthcare-IT integration complexity vs consumer-focused PERS.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **B2B partnership channel.** Vayyar is a partner more than a competitor for MyLifeLine — partner for D2C in-home radar add-on or for senior-living B2B contracts.
- **Bathroom-fall narrative.** 80% of falls occur in the bathroom; if MyLifeLine ever ships a contactless sensor, Vayyar is the licensing target.
- **Behaviour-analytics data flywheel.** Vayyar's analytics can augment MyLifeLine's caregiver-app dashboards — partner integration can be a marketed feature.
- **B2B scale for MyLifeLine.** Senior-living operator channel is replicable; MyLifeLine can pitch operator-level contracts to complement the D2C business.
