## Product lineup

- **Lively Mobile2** — all-in-one mobile medical alert with enhanced GPS, two-way voice; $79.99 device + service plan.
- **Jitterbug Smart4** — smartphone for seniors with large icons, voice typing, urgent-response button; $119.99 device.
- **Jitterbug Flip2** — basic flip phone; $79.99 device.
- **Jitterbug Flip3 / Smart5** — generational refreshes 2024+ (same pricing tiers).
- **Urgent Response service** — push button connects to trained agents 24/7 (used with any Lively phone / Mobile2).
- **Nurse On-Call** — included in Premium package.
- **Care Advocate** — chronic care coordination +$10/mo optional.
- **Lively Link app** — caregiver-facing app: location history, device status, alert log.

## Differentiation

Lively is the only US medical-alert service operated by a tier-1 national retailer (Best Buy) and the only one with exclusive AARP co-brand access. The Lively Mobile2 is a recognized leading-edge device in the senior-friendly category, and the Lively Link caregiver app is among the most polished in independent reviews.

## Strengths

- ✅ Tier-1 retail backing — only PERS operated by Best Buy Health; benefits from in-store retail discovery.
- ✅ Verizon network — strongest cellular reputation among US PERS operators; backed by Rootmetrics ratings.
- ✅ AARP exclusive partnership — accessibility into AARP's 38M members + AARP-endorsed product marketing.
- ✅ Lively Mobile2 sets the bar for senior-friendly mobile hardware — IPX7 water resistance, up to 40-hour battery, large help button.
- ✅ Lively Link caregiver app consistently top-rated for usability and onboarding (clean setup flow).
- ✅ Multi-device family — three phones + one dedicated medical alert give AARP households a one-stop shop.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Service pricing has crept — Basic Phone plan starts $14.99/mo but Premium Plus Care Advocate + Fall Detection crosses $54.99/mo.
- ❌ Equipment fee ($79.99-$119.99) — competitors Bay Alarm / Medical Guardian lease for free or one-time $149 promo.
- ❌ $35 activation fee — niche product vs. the no-activation-fee positioning of Bay Alarm / Medical Guardian.
- ❌ Fall detection +$9.99/mo + Care Advocate +$10/mo — pile of add-ons obscures all-in pricing.
- ❌ Best Buy retail chain sales staff not specialized in PERS — in-store knowledge gap.
- ❌ Lively is the only D2C PERS brand for the Best Buy portfolio; consumer confusion between Lively vs. senior tech category remains.
