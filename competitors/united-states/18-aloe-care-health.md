- **The Hub (Smart Hub 2)** — voice-activated 4G hub; motion, temperature, air-quality sensors; Wi-Fi hotspot; check-in calling; caregiver speed-dial.

- **Mobile Companion 2** — 4G LTE wearable with GPS; automatic fall detection; caregiver speed dial.
- **Essentials plan** — Hub + Care Button.
- **Total Care plan** — Hub + Mobile Companion 2.

## Differentiation

Aloe Care Health is the only US PERS with a voice-activated smart-HUB architecture (vs a pendant or watch); it bundles motion / temperature / air-quality sensors and a multi-user caregiver app, and is sold into Medicare Advantage and senior-living operators as a B2B channel. The April 2026 Arlo Technologies acquisition adds home-security retail distribution.

## Strengths

- ✅ Voice-activated smart Hub — distinguishes from pendant/wrist wearables ("Alexa-like" PERS).
- ✅ Bundled environmental sensors (motion, temperature, air quality) — broader than any pure-PERS competitor.
- ✅ Multi-user caregiver app (unlimited caregivers) — operationally simple for adult-child / case-manager teams.
- ✅ Medicare Advantage / health-plan distribution — institutional channel.
- ✅ Arlo acquisition (April 2026) — public-company backing and home-security retail cross-sell.
- ✅ Built-in WiFi hotspot extends caregiver access in low-broadband households.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Brand awareness mid-tier vs Bay Alarm / Medical Guardian / Lively.
- ❌ Single device (Hub) is hub-dependent — if the Hub fails, the Mobile Companion 2 is the only fallback.
- ❌ Fall detection accuracy reported as inconsistent (warning in their own marketing).
- ❌ Acquisition transition (Arlo) introduces service-quality uncertainty in 2026 ⚠️.
- ❌ Less depth on individual wearable form factors vs Kanega / Apple Watch — no smartwatch-first SKU.
- ❌ Limited Spanish-language primary support.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Voice-hub narrative without the device dependency.** MyLifeLine could ship a low-cost smart-speaker PERS integration (Alexa skill / Google Home) without requiring the user to install a proprietary hub.
- **Pick up the environmental-sensor story.** Aloe Care has temperature / motion / air-quality; MyLifeLine could partner with a smart-thermostat or air-quality sensor manufacturer for a more comprehensive bundle.
- **Health-plan distribution.** Aloe Care's Medicare Advantage channels are well-paved; MyLifeLine should target senior-MA plans directly with comparable B2B economics.
- **Arlo-distribution learning.** Once Arlo integration stabilizes, the post-acquisition Arlo retail channel is a model for distribution scale.
