## Product lineup

- **MobileHelp Classic** — in-home cellular base + pendant, AT&T-network; "industry's most extensive in-home range (up to 1,400 ft)."
- **MobileHelp Wired** — landline-based home system, $22.87/mo sale price ⚠️.
- **MobileHelp Solo** — mobile-only GPS device (4G LTE) for active seniors.
- **MobileHelp Duo** — bundled Classic + Solo (home + on-the-go).
- **MobileHelp Touch** — touchscreen smartwatch (pre-acquisition) — still listed in legacy pricing ⚠️.
- **MobileHelp CareHub** — caregiver app/portal (event notifications, location, activity log).
- **MobileHelp Elite (2025+)** — refreshed brand/identity unveiled post-acquisition under Medical Guardian.

## Differentiation

MobileHelp was the brand that brought cellular + GPS into US PERS in 2006 (M-PERS pioneer). Post-2024 acquisition, it now operates as Medical Guardian's mobile-first sub-brand with refreshed branding (MobileHelp Elite, July 2025), and its "lowest-price-lead" promotion ($22.87/mo landline) is used to acquire price-sensitive buyers across both Medical Guardian funnels.

## Strengths

- ✅ M-PERS pioneer — pioneered GSM+GPS medical-alert integration in the US (2006).
- ✅ Lowest published US PERS pricing in 2026 — Wired landline at $22.87/mo sale undercuts Bay Alarm Medical for the cost-driven buyer.
- ✅ Nationwide AT&T cellular coverage advertised — strong coast-to-coast reliability story.
- ✅ MobileHelp CareHub — caregiver app with event notifications, online tracking, and location history.
- ✅ Acquired by Medical Guardian (2024) — combined entity is the largest US PERS specialist outside Connect America / Lifeline.
- ✅ Duo bundle (home + mobile) is one of the most cost-effective hybrid plans in the US market.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Brand consolidation risk post-2024 acquisition — MobileHelp domain pages are gradually being migrated under medicalguardian.com; long-term brand independent of Medical Guardian is uncertain.
- ❌ Fall detection is an upsell (+$11/mo) — slightly above the $10/mo category norm.
- ❌ No smartwatch running Apple Watch Fall Detection API.
- ❌ Customer-service reviews flag longer wait times since acquisition (shared operations with Medical Guardian).
- ❌ Marketing depth on caregiver data is shallow compared with Medical Guardian MyGuardian Portal — CareHub is functional but not best-in-breed.
- ❌ HQ relocation to Tampa in 2025 ⚠️ may signal cost-driven consolidation that affects service quality perception.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Exploit service-quality friction during the MobileHelp × Medical Guardian integration.** Public ConsumerAffairs and BBB comments reflect uneven experience; a positioning message of "always a human, always a callback in under 60 seconds" lands well.
- **Undercut on landline entry pricing.** MobileHelp Wired's $22.87/mo annual price is the published floor; MyLifeLine can match with no-equipment-fee and no-activation-fee messaging.
- **Build the "active senior" stack.** MobileHelp Solo + a treadmill of fall-detection smartwatches is the active-senior story Apple Watch is taking; MyLifeLine should pick up the mobile-GPS-only narrative with pricing transparency.
- **Target post-acquisition detractors.** Search and SEO capture of "MobileHelp cancellation" / "MobileHelp complaints" is an evergreen switcher funnel.
