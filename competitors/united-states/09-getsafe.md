## Differentiation

GetSafe is the in-home PERS for seniors who refuse to wear a pendant — voice-activated wall buttons, pull cords, and base-station coverage substitute for the wearable. The flat $29.95/mo monitoring fee regardless of home size is the cleanest commercial story in the US PERS market for the active-aging cohort that doesn't want pendant-stigma.

## Strengths

- ✅ Pendant-free design — voice-activated and pull-cord wall buttons reach seniors who will not wear a wearable.
- ✅ Flat $29.95/mo monitoring fee — same regardless of home size; clearest commercial story in US PERS.
- ✅ TMA Five-Diamond Certified, US-based monitoring (shared with Bay Alarm Medical).
- ✅ Reasonable equipment pricing with steep 50%+ sale discounts.
- ✅ Voice activation widens the activation paths (three: button, voice, pull-cord).
- ✅ 30-day risk-free trial; no long-term contract.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Limited fall-detection reach — only the (optional) lanyard pendant has the accelerometer; voice/pull-cord doesn't support fall detection natively.
- ❌ In-home only by default — outside-the-home protection requires the GetSafe Mobile GPS ($20/mo + accessories).
- ❌ Brand awareness vs. Bay Alarm Medical is lower; many consumers don't realize it's the same ownership.
- ❌ No wearable smartwatch in lineup — falls behind SOS Smartwatch, MGMove, Lively Mobile2, Kanega.
- ❌ Stigma claim ("no pendant") may exaggerate — the Mobile GPS button still requires a wearable or pocket carry.
- ❌ Limited product marketing vs the Bay Alarm Medical investment; smaller share of voice in the parent family.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Pendant-free design is a high-margin, niche product space.** MyLifeLine could launch a comparable wall-button product without the wearable as an option for the active-aging market.
- **Flat monthly fee narrative.** $29.95/mo flat regardless of house size is a powerful headline; MyLifeLine could match or beat at $24.95/mo flat.
- **Voice activation.** GetSafe's voice activation is currently limited; MyLifeLine could pair voice activation with a smart-speaker integration (e.g., MyLifeLine Alexa skill).
- **Pendant + non-pendant combos.** The "either wear it or don't" positioning is currently missing from most US PERS; MyLifeLine can lead with "designed for how you live."
