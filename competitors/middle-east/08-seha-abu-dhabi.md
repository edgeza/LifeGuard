## Product lineup

- **14 Hospitals** — including Sheikh Khalifa Medical City, Tawam Hospital, Corniche Hospital, Al Rahba Hospital, etc.
- **70 Ambulatory Healthcare Centres** — including AHS family medicine, urgent care, school health services.
- **Ambulatory Healthcare Services (AHS)** — 38 healthcare centres (2 diagnostic/screening, 2 children's specialty, 2 well-baby vaccination, 4 dental specialty, 1 occupational health, 31 family medicine + specialty).
- **Mobile Clinic Solutions** — 3 mobile clinic vehicles + 1 mobile mammography clinic; specialty consultations, chronic-disease management, Ifhas screening, vaccinations, ECG, ultrasound, lab tests.
- **Home Vaccination for Seniors** — "We Reach You, Wherever You Are" initiative with ADPHC + FDF + Majalis Affairs Office.
- **Homecare Centre** — single dedicated homecare centre (capacity not published) ⚠️.
- **Disease Prevention & Screening Centres** — 10 centres including Ifhas screening.
- **Family Development Foundation Partnership** — geriatric home-visit services with the FDF (Sheikha Fatima's foundation).
- **Mobile Preventive and Treatment Services** — 7 days/week, 8am-10pm; insurance from 30+ companies accepted.

## Differentiation

SEHA is the **largest health-services network in the UAE** and the only public hospital network authorised to operate across the entire Abu Dhabi emirate. Its AHS mobile-clinic program is the only government-run at-home preventive + diagnostic service in the UAE, and the ADPHC + FDF + SEHA "We Reach You, Wherever You Are" senior home-vaccination initiative is the model public-sector elder-care program.

## Strengths

- ✅ **Government mandate** — statutory operator of Abu Dhabi's public hospitals; no private competitor can match the institutional footprint.
- ✅ **Largest single UAE healthcare network** — 14 hospitals + 70 ambulatory + 38 healthcare centres + 255 schools.
- ✅ **Mobile Clinic Solutions** — the only government-run mobile-clinic service in the UAE; 7 days/week, 8am-10pm.
- ✅ **Free for Thiqa-insured Emiratis** — no subscription PERS can outcompete free for the national-citizen segment.
- ✅ **ADPHC + FDF + Majalis partnerships** — multi-government-agency elder-care coordination.
- ✅ **Mobile preventive + diagnostic at home** — ECG, ultrasound, lab tests, chronic-disease management delivered to the doorstep.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Abu Dhabi emirate only.** No SEHA hospitals in Dubai, Sharjah, or the Northern Emirates — Dubai residents must use DHA-licensed private providers.
- ❌ **Not a PERS / wearable subscription brand.** No pendant, no smartwatch, no 24/7 trained-care specialist centre for individual subscribers.
- ❌ **No caregiver app** for ongoing family visibility.
- ❌ **Public-service KPIs** — long appointment wait times for non-urgent consultations; not a concierge experience.
- ❌ **Single dedicated homecare centre** — capacity constrained vs. private competitors (Manzil, FRH, Medcare).
- ❌ **Mobile clinic hours 8am-10pm** — no 24/7 emergency response (SRCA / DCAS handle emergencies, not SEHA).
