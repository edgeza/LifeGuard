## Product lineup

- **Doctor on Call** — DHA-licensed GP arrives at home / hotel / office within 30-45 minutes, 24/7/365. AED 700-1,200 per visit published ⚠️.
- **Doctor at Hotel** — VIP/private-doctor at Dubai hotel rooms (Jumeirah, Palm, Downtown, Business Bay, Al Barsha are signature catchment areas).
- **Nurse at Home** — wound care, post-surgical, IV infusions, injections, catheter care, PEG/NG tubes.
- **Physiotherapy at Home** — orthopedic, neuro, post-surgical, geriatric mobility.
- **IV Drip Therapy** — wellness IVs (Myers cocktail, NAD+, glutathione, hangover, hydration).
- **Lab Tests at Home** — sample collection by phlebotomist, partnered with NRL and other labs.
- **Teleconsultation** — video/audio consultations 24/7.
- **Medicine Delivery** — same-day prescription delivery.
- **DNA Testing** — secure at-home DNA tests.
- **First Aid Training Center** — corporate/group training.
- **Private Ambulance** — non-emergency inter-facility transport.
- **Peadiatrician on Call** — DHA-licensed paediatric home visits.
- **Dental Services** — at-home dentist visits.

## Differentiation

FRH is **the first JCI-accredited home healthcare provider in the GCC** and the only Dubai-headquartered player with documented 300,000+ patient visits and 180+ premium corporate clients (hotels, embassies, HNW families). Its 30-45-minute doctor-arrival promise in Dubai — backed by a hotline number (0505050387) that doubles as the brand "800-DIALDOC" — is the strongest consumer brand recall in UAE home healthcare.

## Strengths

- ✅ **JCI-accredited** — only home-healthcare provider in Dubai with this badge at the time of writing.
- ✅ **Volume proof** — 300,000+ patient visits and 180+ premium brand clients (hotels + embassies) is the largest documented patient base for any independent UAE home-healthcare startup.
- ✅ **30-45 minute ETA** — published, consistent, and signature. Used as the headline metric across every district page.
- ✅ **Multilingual hotline + WhatsApp booking** — the expat-heavy Dubai market expects 0505050387 / 800-DIALDOC; no other home-healthcare brand owns this number-association.
- ✅ **Hotels + VIP clientele** — Jumeirah/Palm/Al Barsha district pages target the same HNW + transient-patient segment that MyLifeLine's adult-child buyer cares about.
- ✅ **DHA-licensed from day one** — regulatory credibility vs. uninsured handyman-style competitors.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ **Not a PERS / wearable product.** No pendant, no fall detection, no smartwatch. A fall at 3am still requires the senior to be conscious and able to call 0505050387.
- ❌ **Per-visit pricing model.** No monthly subscription for "always-on" protection. A frail elder averaging 2-4 visits/month quickly outpaces $30-$80/mo PERS subscriptions.
- ❌ **No family/caregiver app** for ongoing visibility. No vitals dashboard, no medication reminders, no fall log.
- ❌ **Dubai-centric** despite claiming Abu Dhabi / RAK / Fujairah / Qatar coverage — marketing pages, brand recall, and dispatch density are Dubai-skewed.
- ❌ **Private-pay dominant.** Insurance reimbursement is "available" but most patients pay out-of-pocket or via premium corporate retainers.
- ❌ **No structured chronic-disease management** for the long-tail (CHF, COPD, dementia, post-stroke). Pure acute-care + episodic wellness (IV drips).

- **Bundle subscription PERS with FRH visits.** MyLifeLine's pendant + 24/7 care specialist → FRH dispatch on confirmed medical need = a recurring-revenue + per-visit hybrid that neither player offers today.
