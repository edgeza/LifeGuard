## Product lineup

- **Medihill In-Home System (Home Medical Alert)** — landline or cellular module; SOS + GPS system; heart-rate monitoring; pedometer.
- **Medihill Hotpod** — GPS Mobile + Fall Detection; water-resistant portable unit (multiple colour choices).
- **Medihill Smartwatch** — touch-screen smartwatch medical alert (GPS Medical Alert).
- **Medihill HealthTracker (Libris)** — portable 4G personal emergency response with fall detection and geofence.

## Differentiation

Pure budget-tier D2C with transparent pricing and the only competitor that publishes every tier's price on a single product page (MH_Landline-M $47.49, MH_Watch1-M $71.49, etc.). Discount ladder for longer commitments (10–25% off) is unusually detailed.

## Strengths

- ✅ Transparent published pricing across in-home + portable + smartwatch + tracker SKUs — best-published rates on the Canadian independent side.
- ✅ Discount ladder (10/15/20/25%) for 3/6/12-month commitments — drives average revenue per user.
- ✅ Web Monitoring Application included on portable systems — lighter version of competitor caregiver-app stories.
- ✅ No activation fee; free lockbox + shipping reasonable.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Fall detection is a $15.99/month add-on — competitors include it.
- ❌ Multiple SKUs with inconsistent annual totals — confusing optics for comparison shoppers.
- ❌ Brand pull is below Life Assure / Lifeline / Bedford — fewer inbound links in trade press.
- ❌ Accreditation weaker than Galaxy / Life Assure / SecurMEDIC — ULC / CSAA not advertised.
- ❌ Single-language support.
