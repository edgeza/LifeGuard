## Product lineup

- **G7c** — 4G cellular lone-worker monitor (also supports single-gas and multi-gas cartridges)
- **G7x** — adds Iridium satellite connectivity for off-grid (G7x + G7 Bridge satellite uplink)
- **G8 Lone Worker** — 4G LTE auto-carrier-switching monitor with dual-band GPS/GNSS, fall detection, no-motion, missed check-ins, silent SOS, push-to-talk, text messaging
- **Loner Mobile** — turns any iPhone/Android into a safety device using the same Blackline Live platform
- **G7 Standard Cartridge** — no-gas lone-worker monitor with 3G + optional satellite
- **Field-replaceable cartridges** — single-gas / multi-gas cartridges let a G7 start as a lone-worker device and grow into a multi-gas detector over time

## Differentiation

Canadian-public company with the deepest employee-worn gas-detection / lone-worker convergence in the world. Five-Diamond-certified in-house SOC and a hardware fleet that can pivot from no-gas lone-worker to a multi-gas detector via field-replaceable cartridges — unique industrial narrative that no app-only competitor can match.

## Strengths

- ✅ Five-Diamond in-house SOC; 99% of calls answered in <60s; 8M+ alerts responded to lifetime.
- ✅ Field-replaceable cartridge architecture — G7 grows from lone-worker to multi-gas over time without rip-and-replace.
- ✅ Dual-band GPS/GNSS, 4G LTE auto-carrier switching, push-to-talk (walkie-talkie), text messaging, fall detection, no-motion, missed-check-in.
- ✅ Iridium satellite (G7x + G7 Bridge) covers off-grid oil-and-gas / forestry / remote-construction.
- ✅ Blackline Live cloud portal with ZoneAware™ perimeter alerts, AlertLink proximity-based alerts, real-time map.
- ✅ Mandated "BS8484 accredited" level-1 response in the UK — translates well to Canadian occupational health frameworks.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ No consumer / individual / senior-PERS line — strictly enterprise.
- ❌ High device hardware cost — out of reach for SMB / sole-trader buyers.
- ❌ Per-user monthly cost is opaque public-side; sales-led.
- ❌ Hardware-led, not app-led — competes against app-only entrants (StaySafe, Neovigie, Be Safe) on price/installation simplicity.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **SMB tier** — solo contractors / small construction / small home-care agencies cannot afford Blackline's enterprise pricing; a self-serve PERS-style monthly plan would win this segment.
- **Senior-PERS convergence** — Blackline's G7/G8 platform does not serve the senior PERS market; MyLifeLine can reuse the ELS/lone-worker technology pattern for solo seniors living alone.
- **Insurance-bundled channel** — workers' comp insurers in Canada (WSIB, WCB-Alberta/Saskatchewan/BC, etc.) want verified monitoring; integration layer is a sellable add-on.
- **Carbon-monoxide / gas-detector adjacency** for seniors (boilers, attached garages, snowed-in homes) — Blackline owns the industrial version of this argument.
