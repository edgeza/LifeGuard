## Product lineup

- **SmartSAFE Home** — in-home base + pendant; extreme-temperature monitoring; waterproof.
- **SmartSAFE Home Duo** — supports two simultaneous users in one home (couples / roommates).
- **SmartSAFE Home with Fall Detection** — pendant-based fall detection; extreme-temperature monitor.
- **SmartSAFE S (SmartWatch)** — touchscreen watch with SOS, fall detection, heart-rate, step counter, weather, body-temp, blood-oxygen (SpO2).
- **SmartSAFE PRO** — premium fall-detection smartwatch; GPS; up to 5-day battery; vitals (HR, BP, body temp, SpO2); 12-month warranty.
- **SmartSAFE Mobile** — wireless two-way voice pendant with fall detection.

## Differentiation

Quebec-based bilingual PERS specialist with the widest product breadth — including a couples / duo in-home SKU that most rivals don't bother making, and an "extreme temperature" monitoring feature that gives a winter-utility hook for seniors in cold-climate provinces.

## Strengths

- ✅ Quebec bilingual — strong anchor in Quebec market where English-only competitors are weaker.
- ✅ SmartSAFE Duo — only competitor that explicitly markets for couples / two-user households in one home base.
- ✅ Six ULC-certified stations — robust redundant monitoring infrastructure.
- ✅ Wide product breadth (4G smartwatch PRO with vitals, in-home, mobile, duo, panic buttons).
- ✅ "99% customer satisfaction rating" claim (per securmedic.com).

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Pricing is not transparent online — every product requires a phone call.
- ❌ Toronto/Ontario distribution presence is small relative to Bedford or Senior Protection.
- ❌ SmartSAFE PRO's $99.95 programming fee is material vs. all-included competitors.
- ❌ No smartwatch running the Apple Watch Fall Detection API — owned cellular smartwatch only.
- ❌ Limited digital / app messaging in marketing (caregiver portal not advertised).
