- **Safe Alone App** — iOS/Android smartphone lone-worker app — GPS, scheduled safety check-ins, panic button, missed-check-in escalation.
- **eALERT** — desktop panic button for office staff / reception / clinic front desks.
- **SOS Wearables** — physical SOS pendant / wearable for high-risk roles.
- **Satellite & Remote Monitoring** — Iridium satellite device for 100% global coverage for off-grid sites (mining, forestry, energy).
- **CheckMate Dashboard** — web console for supervisors; every worker's status, location, schedule, audit trail, compliance reporting.

## Differentiation

Longest-established Canadian alarm-monitoring business (ProTELEC since 1968) rebrands its 24/7 live-monitoring capability into a modern lone-worker SKU. Differentiation: trained operators act on alerts, not just notify — and the satellite option supports the 600+ remote communities in northern Canada where App-only solutions fail.

## Strengths

- ✅ 5-Diamond ULC-certified monitoring; sub-60-second response; trained human operator — not a notification forwarder.
- ✅ No smartphone required — workers without phones can use a landline check-in.
- ✅ Satellite option (Iridium) covers off-grid; App + wearable + satellite device mix-and-match.
- ✅ Compliance depth — built around Canadian OHS / Bill C-45; automated audit reports.
- ✅ All-inclusive per-user pricing — no setup fees.
- ✅ 24-hour deployment — competitor's most aggressive on onboarding.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ Falls-detection-only-on-special-hardware — not a senior-PERS product.
- ❌ Enterprise-only sales motion — no individual-consumer channel.
- ❌ Multi-decade brand equity is in the alarm/central-station space, not lone worker.
- ❌ Hardware devices (eALERT, wearables, satellite) not on the marketing site — procurement is sales-led.

## MyLifeLine opportunity

- **Compliance-as-a-service for OHS.** Bill C-45 documentation is mandatory; offer a turnkey "audit pack" feature set.
- **PSW / home-care worker channel** — same product, different buyer (community-care agencies vs. industrial).
- **Satellite-inclusive starter SKU** — for solo professionals in remote regions (e.g. trap-line operators, park rangers) who need fall + SOS + satellite but cannot afford Blackline's enterprise contract.
- **Worker-payroll integration** — pairing CheckMate-style monitoring with payroll (or workers' comp discount) is an unoccupied buyer.
