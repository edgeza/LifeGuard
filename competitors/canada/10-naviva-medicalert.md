## Product lineup

- **NAVIVA Pendant** — wearable pendant (also worn on wrist or belt loop); fall detection; two-way voice; GPS; SOS.
- **NAVIVA Smartwatch** — wrist-worn, fall detection, two-way voice, SOS, GPS.
- **NAVIVA Micro** — coming fall 2026; super-discreet wearable attachable to a legacy MedicAlert bracelet; long battery life.

## Subscription bundles

- **NAVIVA Only** — GPS device + monitoring with no MedicAlert classic services.
- **NAVIVA Add-On** — adds GPS to existing MedicAlert Classic subscribers.
- **NAVIVA Essentials** — NAVIVA GPS + MedicAlert emergency health-record service + custom-engraved red-logo MedicAlert bracelet in one subscription.
- **NAVIVA Memory Support** — NAVIVA GPS + MedicAlert service + blue-logo MedicAlert bracelet (typically for dementia / cognitive impairment).

## Differentiation

Only PERS in Canada tied to a national charity's decades-old medical-ID database and health-record management service. NAVIVA bridges passive medical ID (bracelet + Personal Health Record) and active PERS into one subscription — value for chronic-condition seniors whose first-responder engagement is the deciding factor.

## Strengths

- ✅ Charity trust signals — "trusted more than a million Canadians for 65 years"; not-for-profit mission.
- ✅ Healthcare-record integration — Personal Health Record is reviewed by physicians and nurses; PERS alerts funnel first-responders to that record.
- ✅ Caregiver-platform flexible tier — Memory Support bundle addresses dementia-segment buyers directly.
- ✅ NAVIVA Micro roadmap for late 2026 — strong product-pipeline narrative.
- ✅ "SOS connects to MedicAlert 911 line" angle is brand-distinctive.

## Weaknesses / gaps

- ❌ SOS feature "will not function" outside Canada or US — limits snowbird portability beyond those countries.
- ❌ Pricing and SKUs not yet fully detailed on public site; product launched April 2025 and still ramping.
- ❌ Per-Terms charity-conversion friction ("Service Plan fees are either prepaid upon registration or paid monthly") — billing complexity in the tiny-print.
- ❌ Smaller installed base vs. Lifeline — NAVIVA is new; the brand-equity comes from the underlying MedicAlert legacy, not from new device marketing.
